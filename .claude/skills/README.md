## 📚 Skill Files

### 1. Sales Discovery Coach (490 lines)
**File:** `sales-discovery-coach.md`

**What it does:**
- Roleplay 5 customer personas (CMO, Coordinator, Provider, CFO, IT)
- Practice discovery calls with real-time feedback
- Score your calls (0-100) on 5 dimensions
- Analyze transcripts from real calls

**When to use:**
- Before important customer calls (30 min practice)
- After customer calls (get feedback on real transcripts)
- When learning a new persona or tactic

**Try it:**
```
"Practice a discovery call with a CMO at a 500K-member health plan"
```

### 2. Sales Objection Handler (465 lines)
**File:** `sales-objection-handler.md`

**What it does:**
- Master 6 common objections (price, build-ourselves, security, integration, timing, budget)
- Get 3 reframe options for each
- Competitive positioning (Epic, Optum, build-in-house, DIY)
- ROI calculations and handling checklists

**When to use:**
- When a customer raises an objection in real-time
- Before calls to prepare for likely objections
- To deepen your understanding of competitive positioning

**Try it:**
```
"A customer just said 'Your pricing is too high compared to our Epic add-on'"
```

## 💡 Usage Patterns

### Pattern 1: Pre-Call Preparation (30-45 min)
1. Know your prospect's role (CMO? CFO? IT?)
2. Use Demo Designer to build your pitch
3. Use Objection Handler to prep for likely concerns
4. Use Discovery Coach to practice once
5. Go crush the call!

### Pattern 2: Post-Call Analysis (15-20 min)
1. Share call transcript with Discovery Coach
2. Get feedback and score
3. Learn from mistakes
4. Iterate in next practice session

### Pattern 3: Pipeline Management (30-45 min)
1. List your deals to Sales Manager
2. Get prioritized scoring
3. Focus on hot deals (80+)
4. Get coaching on stalled deals

### Pattern 4: Daily Coaching (5-10 min)
- Quick question about discovery? → Discovery Coach
- Objection came up? → Objection Handler
- Demo question? → Demo Designer
- Strategy question? → Sales Manager
