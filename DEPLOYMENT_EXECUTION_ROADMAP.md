## CONTINGENCY PLANS

**If email open rates are below 20%:**
- Subject line A/B testing
- Audience segmentation refinement
- Re-evaluate messaging and positioning
- Consider consultant for email optimization

**If discovery calls don't convert to proposals:**
- Sales coaching (revisit Discovery Call Guide 2)
- Increase deep dive call frequency
- Involve customer reference (peer testimonial)
- Reduce perceived risk with pilot offer

**If blog traffic is below 400/week:**
- Increase LinkedIn promotion of blog content
- Guest posting on industry publications
- SEO optimization of blog posts
- Consider paid social ads ($100-200/week)

**If Q1 webinar attendance is below 75:**
- Increase email promotion (send 2-3 additional emails)
- LinkedIn ads targeting quality directors ($200-300/week)
- Partner with industry associations for co-promotion
- Offer incentive (free assessment, discount if they register)

**If sales cycle extends beyond 90 days:**
- This is normal (many deals take 4-6 months to close)
- Focus on pipeline development (proposals issued) rather than closes
- Continue nurture for proposals in negotiation
- Target: 3-5 proposals in pipeline by end of Week 12
