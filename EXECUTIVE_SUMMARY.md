# HDIM Executive Summary

## HealthData-in-Motion: AI-Powered Healthcare Quality Management

---

## The Opportunity

Healthcare organizations lose **$50B+ annually** in quality incentive payments due to manual processes, siloed data, and delayed insights. Value-based care now represents 40% of provider revenue, yet most organizations still track quality measures using spreadsheets and monthly reports.

**HDIM** is a SaaS platform that delivers real-time quality intelligence at the point of care, helping healthcare organizations capture more incentive revenue while improving patient outcomes.
