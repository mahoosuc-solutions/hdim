## Key Messages (Talking Points)

### For Sales Reps

**Opening:** "HDIM isn't just another interoperability platform. We're solving the three biggest gaps in healthcare:"

1. **Mental Health is Part of Health**
   - "50% of depressed patients go undiagnosed in primary care. Depression worsens outcomes for diabetes, hypertension, COPD—everything you're measuring with HEDIS. We screen automatically (PHQ-9), create care gaps, and ensure psychiatry engagement."

2. **High-Risk Patients Need Intensive Support**
   - "20% of your patients drive 80% of your costs. But your care coordinators treat everyone equally. We identify who's at risk (chronic disease burden, recent ED use, mental health factors) and recommend the intervention level: intensive for high-risk, self-service for low-risk."

3. **FHIR is Healthcare's Standard—We're Built on It**
   - "Most EHRs retrofitted FHIR on top of HL7v2. We built FHIR-native from day one. That means faster integration, less custom code, and future-proof interoperability. 8-12 week implementation, not 18-24 months."

### For Executives

**Board/Investor Pitch:** "HDIM is a complete population health platform—not just a data exchange layer."

- Mental health integration + quality measures = complete patient picture
- Risk stratification + care coordination = efficient resource allocation  
- FHIR-native architecture = competitive advantage + future-proofing
- Result: Higher HEDIS scores, lower costs, better patient outcomes

### For Implementation Partners

**Sales Tool:** New capabilities = differentiating proof points

- "We come pre-loaded with test data (10 patients, 5 HEDIS measures). Your team can validate requirements on Day 1, not Month 3."
- "Mental health screening is included. No additional build. Your clinicians get depression detection out of the box."
- "Risk stratification is built-in. No need to buy a separate analytics tool."
