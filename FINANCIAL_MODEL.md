| Metric | Calculation | Value |
|--------|-------------|------:|
| Average ACV | Blended | $84,000 |

## 10. Financial Dashboard

### Key Metrics to Track Monthly

| Metric | Target | Formula |
|--------|--------|---------|
| MRR | Growing | Sum of monthly subscriptions |
| ARR | $300K → $4M | MRR × 12 |
| MRR Growth | >10%/mo | (MRR - Prior MRR) / Prior MRR |
| Gross Margin | >80% | (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue |
| CAC | <$30K | S&M Spend / New Customers |
| LTV:CAC | >5x | LTV / CAC |
| Payback | <12 mo | CAC / (ACV × Gross Margin / 12) |
| Burn Rate | <$50K/mo | Monthly cash decrease |
| Runway | >18 mo | Cash / Monthly Burn |
| NRR | >100% | (Starting MRR + Expansion - Churn) / Starting MRR |
