## THE SOLUTION: Integrated Mental Health Screening

### What HDIM Does

**Step 1: Automated Depression Screening**
- PHQ-9 questionnaire automatically administered during routine visits
- Integrated into patient workflows (not a separate process)
- Results captured in EHR and HDIM system

**Step 2: Auto-Create Care Gaps**
- Positive PHQ-9 (≥10) automatically creates a care gap: "Depression screening positive—refer to psychiatry/counseling"
- Gap appears in care coordinator dashboard with same priority as diabetes/hypertension gaps
- Routed to appropriate team (psychiatry, behavioral health, primary care)

**Step 3: Integrated Care Coordination**
- Care coordination templates for depression management
- Comorbidity tracking: "Patient with HbA1c >9 AND depression: Start diabetes + mental health interventions together"
- Links depression management to medication adherence (expected outcome: better diabetes/HTN control)

**Step 4: Outcome Tracking**
- PHQ-9 re-assessment at 8 weeks (measure treatment effectiveness)
- Depression remission rate tracked alongside other quality measures
- Integrated with risk stratification (depression is risk factor for other comorbidities)
