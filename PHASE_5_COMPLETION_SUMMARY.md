## LESSONS LEARNED FROM 9-DAY EXECUTION

**What Worked:**
1. **Feature-driven messaging:** Mental health + risk stratification are strong differentiators
2. **Financial anchor:** $3M Year 1 benefit resonates with decision-makers
3. **Multi-audience approach:** CMO, CFO, CIO all get personalized messaging
4. **Speed:** 90-day implementation timeline is a competitive advantage
5. **Case study credibility:** St. Mary's results make the ROI believable

**What to Monitor:**
1. **Email fatigue:** 15 templates across 90 days could be high volume—segment aggressively
2. **Sales adoption:** Do sales reps actually use the battle cards? Monitor in weekly calls.
3. **Blog traffic:** If below 400/week, boost with paid social or SEO optimization
4. **Proposal conversion:** Target 40-50% conversion; if lower, may need longer sales cycle
5. **Customer implementation:** Early customers need high-touch support to demonstrate ROI

**Key Insights for Scaling:**
1. Mental health screening = most powerful differentiator (emotional + financial hook)
2. Risk stratification = must-have add-on (customers want both for full ROI)
3. CEO/Board positioning = critical objection handler (financial ROI overcomes most concerns)
4. Telepsychiatry partnership = enables mental health strategy (removes "no capacity" objection)
5. 90-day implementation = trust builder (realistic, proven timeline)
