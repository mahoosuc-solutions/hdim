## ❓ FAQ - Common Questions

### Q: "How long does implementation take?"
**A**: "Typical implementation is 6-8 weeks from kickoff to go-live. The timeline depends on your EHR system and integration complexity:
- Week 1-2: Requirements gathering and technical architecture review
- Week 3-4: FHIR API integration and data mapping
- Week 5-6: User acceptance testing and training
- Week 7-8: Production deployment and go-live support

We can do a phased rollout (start with 1-2 clinics) or full deployment depending on your preference."

### Q: "What EHR systems do you integrate with?"
**A**: "We integrate with any FHIR R4-compliant EHR, including:
- Epic (SMART on FHIR launch, bulk FHIR APIs)
- Cerner/Oracle (FHIR APIs)
- Athenahealth (FHIR APIs)
- eClinicalWorks, Allscripts, NextGen (FHIR APIs)

Because we use standard FHIR protocols, integration is typically straightforward. We have implementation guides for the major EHR vendors."

### Q: "Do physicians need to learn a new system?"
**A**: "No additional physician workflow required. We pull data from what they're already documenting in the EHR—patient demographics, vital signs, questionnaire responses, etc. The scoring and care gap creation happens automatically in the background.

Optionally, physicians can view the dashboards if they want insight into their panel's mental health metrics, but it's not required. Most organizations have care managers or quality staff as the primary users."

### Q: "What about data security and HIPAA compliance?"
**A**: "We're built for healthcare:
- Full HIPAA compliance with BAA
- SOC 2 Type II certified
- Data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Audit logging for all PHI access
- Multi-tenant architecture with data isolation
- HIPAA-compliant cache eviction (2-minute TTL)

We can provide our security documentation and SOC 2 report for your InfoSec team to review."

### Q: "Can we start with a pilot before full commitment?"
**A**: "Absolutely—we recommend it. Typical pilot:
- **Scope**: 1-2 clinics, 50-100 patients
- **Duration**: 90 days
- **Success Metrics**: Time savings, quality improvement, physician satisfaction
- **Investment**: $50-100K for pilot

If the pilot meets success criteria, we convert to a full contract. If not, no obligation to continue. This way you validate ROI with real data before scaling."

### Q: "How does pricing work?"
**A**: "Pricing is based on number of physicians and patients:
- Small practices (10-50 physicians): $150-500K/year
- Mid-size organizations (50-200 physicians): $500K-1.5M/year
- Large health systems (200+ physicians): $1.5M-5M/year

We can structure as:
- Annual subscription

Happy to provide a custom quote based on your organization size."

## 🎯 Sales Mantras

### Remember These Principles

1. **Sell outcomes, not features**
   - Bad: "We have automated PHQ-9 scoring"
   - Good: "You'll reduce physician time by 393 hours per year"

2. **Use their data, not generic stats**
   - Bad: "ACOs average 8% depression remission"
   - Good: "You mentioned your remission rate is 12%—what would it be worth to get to 35%?"

3. **Show, don't tell**
   - Bad: "Our platform is easy to use"
   - Good: [Show them clicking 3 times to see patient overview]

4. **Create urgency with deadlines**
   - Bad: "You should consider this"
   - Good: "2025 CMS digital quality mandate is 8 months away—when are you starting implementation?"

5. **Always be closing... the next step**
   - Bad: "Let me know if you have questions"
   - Good: "I'll send you a calendar invite for our technical deep-dive. Does Tuesday at 2pm work?"
