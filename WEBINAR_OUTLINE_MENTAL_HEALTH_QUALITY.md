## Notes for Presenters

1. **Keep energy up.** Mental health is a serious topic, but the webinar should feel forward-looking and solution-focused, not depressing.

2. **Use patient story strategically.** Sarah's story is the emotional anchor. Let it land. Pause after mentioning her HbA1c improvement.

3. **Emphasize ROI multiple times.** Attendees are busy people making financial decisions. Mention the $300K-$450K Year 1 baseline, 0.5 Star rating lift (with note that it scales to $2M+ in larger systems), and the 27% closure improvement at least 3 times.

4. **Keep technical details light.** Don't get lost in PHQ-9 scoring, FHIR APIs, or backend architecture. Focus on outcomes.

5. **Make the CTA easy.** Three CTAs offered (free assessment, resource download, schedule call). Make all three obvious and easy to claim.

6. **Pause for questions.** Especially after Sarah's story and after the solution overview. Give attendees time to think and formulate questions.
