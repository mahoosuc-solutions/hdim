Step 1: Start by giving your measure a clear, descriptive name that reflects its clinical purpose. Include additional context in the description.
Step 2: Define the population criteria. Initial Population is the broadest group, Denominator narrows it down, and Numerator defines those meeting quality criteria.
Step 3: Use the sliders on the right panel to configure clinical parameters. Changes will automatically update the CQL code.
Step 4: Add exclusions for patients who should not be measured, and exceptions for valid medical reasons to not meet criteria.
Step 5: Test your measure with sample patients to verify it works correctly before publishing to production.
