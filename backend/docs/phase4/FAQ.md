# Phase 4 FAQ

## How are anomalies detected?
Anomalies are detected using metric baselines and sustained threshold breaches.

## How is root cause determined?
Root cause is ranked by dependency and temporal correlation evidence.

## How are costs assigned?
Costs are attributed by service, feature, and tenant dimensions.

## Which dashboards are canonical?
Platform overview, business metrics, and security/compliance dashboards are baseline dashboards.

## Where are on-call runbooks?
Runbooks are maintained under `docs/runbooks/`.
