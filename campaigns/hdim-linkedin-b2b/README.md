## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Risk 1: High Cost Per Lead (CPL >$300)
**Likelihood**: Medium | **Impact**: High

**Mitigation**:
- Start with tight targeting (CIO/CMIO only, 500+ employees)
- Use Lead Gen Forms (lower friction than landing pages)
- Pause ad sets with CPL >$300 after 50 clicks
- Test 3 creative angles to find winner

### Risk 2: Low Lead Quality (<60% qualified)
**Likelihood**: Low-Medium | **Impact**: High

**Mitigation**:
- Use job title targeting (not just job function)
- Add qualifying question to form: "Current EHR System?"
- Exclude job seniority below "Director"
- Sales team qualifies within 24 hours

### Risk 3: LinkedIn Ad Fatigue (Frequency >4)
**Likelihood**: High (months 2-3) | **Impact**: Medium

**Mitigation**:
- Rotate creative every 30 days
- Pause ads when frequency >3.5
- Expand audience after 60 days
- Introduce new ad formats (Document Ads, Video)

**Full risk analysis**: See `/strategy/channel-strategy.md` (Risk Assessment section)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to use all 3 ad variants?**
A: No. Start with Variant 1 (Data-Driven) only. Add Variant 2 in Week 2 if budget allows. Skip Variant 3 unless you're comfortable with contrarian messaging.

**Q: Can I run this campaign with a smaller budget?**
A: Yes, but $5K minimum recommended. Below $5K, you won't have statistical significance to optimize effectively. See "Budget Scenario 1" in `/strategy/budget-allocation.md`.

**Q: How long until I see results?**
A: First leads within 3-7 days. First demos within 14-21 days. First closed deals within 30-60 days (given your 30-60 day sales cycle).

**Q: What if Cost Per Lead is >$300 after Week 1?**
A: Don't panic - Week 1 CPL is always high (learning phase). If CPL >$350 after Week 2, pause and diagnose (see `/strategy/kpis.md` - "Red Flags" section).

**Q: Do I need a landing page or just use LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms?**
A: Lead Gen Forms are primary (60% budget). Landing pages are for Sponsored Content (30%) and retargeting. Both are needed for full strategy.

**Q: Can I target outside the US?**
A: Yes, but recommended to start US-only for 90 days, then expand to Canada. Healthcare regulations vary by country.

**Q: What's the sales team's role?**
A: Critical. Sales must respond to all leads within 24 hours (ideally 4 hours). Slow follow-up kills conversion rate. See demo booking best practices in `/strategy/kpis.md`.

**Q: Should I hire an agency or run in-house?**
A: In-house is feasible if you have LinkedIn experience. Otherwise, freelancer for creative design + in-house for campaign management.
