## Production Notes

### Visual Style Guide

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Deep healthcare blue (#1E3A5F)
- Accent: Vibrant teal (#00B4A0) for CTAs and highlights
- Success: Green (#4CAF50)
- Warning: Amber (#FFA726)
- Alert: Red (#EF5350)
- Background: Clean white/light gray (#F5F7FA)

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Inter Bold
- Body: Inter Regular
- Data/Numbers: SF Mono or similar monospace

**Animation Style:**
- Smooth, professional transitions (ease-in-out, 300ms)
- Subtle micro-interactions on hover/click
- Data visualizations should feel alive but not distracting
- Avoid flashy or gimmicky effects

**Screen Recording Guidelines:**
- Use demo environment with realistic but fictional data
- Cursor movements should be smooth and intentional
- Pause briefly on key information (0.5-1 second)
- Avoid showing any loading states or errors

### Audio Guidelines

**Voiceover:**
- Professional, warm, confident tone
- Pace: ~150 words per minute (conversational)
- Gender: Consider having both male and female voices across the series

**Background Music:**
- Subtle, modern, professional
- Avoid generic "corporate" music
- Build energy during action sequences
- Softer during explanation sections
- Music should never compete with voiceover

**Sound Effects (minimal):**
- Soft click on button interactions
- Subtle whoosh on transitions
- Success chime when goals are achieved
- Keep overall sound design clean and professional

### Accessibility

- All videos should have closed captions
- Ensure sufficient color contrast for on-screen text
- Voiceover should describe key visual elements
- Provide transcript for each video

## Delivery Specifications

### Primary Formats
- MP4 (H.264) for web playback
- 1920x1080 minimum, 4K (3840x2160) preferred
- 30fps
- Bitrate: 10-15 Mbps for high quality

### Platform-Specific Exports
- **LinkedIn:** 1:1 square crop option, <10 min
- **YouTube:** 16:9, full HD
- **Website:** Compressed for fast loading (~5 Mbps)
- **Sales Presentations:** Highest quality MP4

### Thumbnail Requirements
- 1920x1080 static image per video
- Clear title text readable at small sizes
- Key visual from the video
- Consistent branding across all thumbnails
