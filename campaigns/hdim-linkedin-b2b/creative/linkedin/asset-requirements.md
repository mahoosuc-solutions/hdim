## Technical Specifications (All Variants)

**File Formats**:
- **Primary**: PNG (recommended for text/graphics)
- **Alternative**: JPG (only if file size >5MB as PNG)
- **Source**: PSD, AI, or Figma (editable)

**Dimensions**:
- **Horizontal**: 1200 × 627 px (1.91:1 ratio)
- **Square**: 1080 × 1080 px (1:1 ratio)
- **Safe Zone**: 90% center (avoid critical text in outer 10%)

**File Size**: <5MB (recommended <2MB for fast loading)
**Resolution**: 72 DPI minimum (screen resolution)
**Color Mode**: RGB (not CMYK)

## Design Guidelines

**Tone**: Professional, modern, technical (NOT consumer/flashy)
**Audience**: C-level healthcare IT executives (CIO, CMIO, CTO)
**Aesthetic**: Enterprise B2B SaaS (think: Salesforce, Workday, Epic Systems)

**DO**:
- ✅ Use clean, minimalist design
- ✅ High contrast text for mobile readability
- ✅ Data visualization (charts, metrics)
- ✅ Professional healthcare color palette
- ✅ White space for clarity

**DON'T**:
- ❌ Stock photos of doctors/nurses (cliché)
- ❌ Overly bright/flashy colors
- ❌ Comic Sans or playful fonts
- ❌ Cluttered design with too many elements
- ❌ Consumer-style graphics

## Review & Revision Process

**Round 1** (Initial Concepts):
- Designer delivers first drafts of all 3 variants
- Stakeholder review (2-3 business days)
- Feedback via annotated comments (Figma comments, Google Docs, etc.)

**Round 2** (Revisions):
- Designer incorporates feedback
- Delivers revised versions
- Final review (1-2 business days)

**Round 3** (Final):
- Minor tweaks only
- Export final files in all required formats
- Deliver with source files

**Timeline**:
- Round 1: 7 days from brief
- Round 2: +3 days (Day 10)
- Round 3: +2 days (Day 12)
- **Total: 12 business days**
