### Style Guidelines
- **Color Palette**:
  - Primary: Healthcare blue (#0A66C2 - LinkedIn blue)
  - Accent: Success green (#2F8F3D) for "After" metrics
  - Alert: Warning red (#E4625E) for "Before" metrics (use sparingly)
  - Background: Clean white or light gray (#F8F9FA)
- **Typography**:
  - Headline: Bold, Sans-serif (Helvetica Neue or Roboto), 32-36pt
  - Metrics: Extra Bold, 48-56pt for numbers, 18-20pt for labels
  - Body: Regular, 14-16pt
- **Imagery**:
  - Avoid stock photos of doctors/nurses (cliché)
  - Use clean data visualization
  - Professional, modern aesthetic (think Stripe, not WebMD)
  - Optional: Subtle hospital/medical building silhouette in background (10% opacity)

### Visual Elements
1. **Arrow**: Bold green arrow showing Before → After transformation
2. **Metrics Cards**: Card-based design with shadow for depth
3. **Badge**: "Case Study" badge/seal in bottom right
4. **Data Visualization**: Small bar chart showing 82% improvement

## Format Specifications

**Platform**: LinkedIn Sponsored Content (Single Image)

**Image Specifications**:
- **Dimensions**: 1200 × 627 px (1.91:1 ratio - horizontal)
  - Also create: 1080 × 1080 px (1:1 square) for mobile optimization
- **File Type**: PNG or JPG
- **File Size**: <5 MB (recommended <2 MB for fast loading)
- **Resolution**: 72 DPI minimum
- **Safe Zone**: Keep critical text/elements within 90% center area (10% margin)

**Design Assets to Include**:
- HDIM logo (high-res PNG with transparent background)
- Regional Medical Center logo (if permission granted, otherwise use "Regional Medical Center" text)
- Icon set: Clock icon, checkmark icons
- Color-coded metric cards

## Designer Brief

**Objective**: Create a professional, data-driven LinkedIn ad that showcases real results from a healthcare client. The ad should appeal to C-level healthcare IT executives who are data-driven decision-makers.

**Target Audience**: CIOs, CMIOs, CTOs at hospitals and health systems (200+ employees)

**Key Message**: HDIM delivers measurable, dramatic ROI in prior authorization efficiency

**Design Requirements**:
1. **Above-the-fold Impact**: Headline + before/after metrics must be readable in LinkedIn feed (thumbnail view)
2. **Mobile-First**: 60% of LinkedIn traffic is mobile - ensure metrics are legible on small screens
3. **Professional Tone**: Healthcare IT buyers expect enterprise-grade design (avoid flashy/salesy aesthetic)
4. **Trust Signals**: Include "Case Study" badge to signal credibility
5. **Contrast**: High contrast between Before (red/gray) and After (green) for quick visual comprehension

**Brand Guidelines**:
- Follow HDIM brand guidelines for logo usage, colors, typography
- If no brand guidelines exist, use clean, modern SaaS aesthetic (think: Salesforce, Workday, Epic Systems)

**Deliverables**:
1. Primary ad creative: 1200×627px PNG
2. Square variant: 1080×1080px PNG (for mobile)
3. Editable source file (.psd, .ai, or .figma)
4. 2 alternate color schemes (for A/B testing in Month 2)

**Timeline**: Deliver within 5 business days for review

**Why This Creative Will Perform**:

3. **Proof**: "Case Study" signals real customer, not marketing fluff
4. **Clarity**: Visual instantly communicates value prop (3.2 days → 8 hours)

**Expected Audience Reaction**:

- "I want to see how they did it" (curiosity)
- "8 hours seems too good to be true - but it's a case study" (credibility + interest)
