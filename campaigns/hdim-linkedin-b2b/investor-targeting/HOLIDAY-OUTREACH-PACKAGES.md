# Holiday Investor Outreach Packages
**Date**: December 25, 2025 (Christmas)
**Purpose**: Warm, memorable outreach to different investor types

---

## Strategy: Why Christmas Outreach Works

**The Opportunity**:
- Investors have quieter inboxes (most people don't send cold emails)
- Decision-makers often reflect on next year during holidays
- Personal touch stands out vs. transactional outreach
- Sets up January meetings when deal flow resumes

**The Risk to Avoid**:
- Don't be overly salesy on a holiday
- Keep it warm, brief, and genuine
- Focus on connection, not hard pitch
- Respect that it's a family day

**Recommended Approach**:
- Short, personal messages
- Genuine holiday wishes
- Light mention of what you're building
- Plant seed for January follow-up
