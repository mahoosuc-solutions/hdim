### December 30 - 10 AM

**Post Type**: Prediction / Thought Leadership

```
My 5 predictions for healthcare IT in 2026:

1. FHIR becomes table stakes, not a differentiator
   The CMS mandate means everyone must comply.
   Winners will be those who go beyond compliance.

2. Prior authorization automation finally hits scale
   Gold carding + FHIR APIs = less friction.
   Patients will feel the difference.

3. AI in healthcare shifts from hype to infrastructure
   The flashy apps get attention.
   The infrastructure enabling them wins.

4. Health systems consolidate integration vendors
   5+ point solutions → 1 platform.
   IT teams demand simplicity.

5. Interoperability becomes a competitive advantage
   Systems that share data well attract patients.
   Closed ecosystems lose.

What's your prediction for 2026?
```
