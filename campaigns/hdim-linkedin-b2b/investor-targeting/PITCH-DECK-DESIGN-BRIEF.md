# HDIM Pitch Deck - Visual Design Brief
**For Designer/Presentation Specialist**

---

## Overview

Convert the `PITCH-DECK.md` content into a polished investor presentation.

**Target Audience**: Healthcare IT venture capital investors
**Tone**: Professional, credible, confident (not flashy or startup-y)
**Length**: 12 core slides + 5 appendix slides
**Format**: 16:9 widescreen

---

## Brand Direction

### Color Palette

| Use | Color | Hex | Notes |
|-----|-------|-----|-------|
| **Primary** | Deep Healthcare Blue | #1E3A5F | Trust, stability, healthcare |
| **Secondary** | Teal Accent | #00A9A5 | Innovation, technology |
| **Success** | Medical Green | #2E7D32 | Results, growth, success |
| **Alert** | Warm Orange | #E65100 | Urgency, attention |
| **Background** | Clean White | #FFFFFF | Primary background |
| **Text** | Dark Gray | #2C3E50 | Body text |
| **Subtle** | Light Gray | #F5F7FA | Section backgrounds |

### Typography

| Element | Font | Weight | Size |
|---------|------|--------|------|
| **Headlines** | Inter or SF Pro | Bold (700) | 44-56pt |
| **Subheads** | Inter or SF Pro | Semi-Bold (600) | 28-36pt |
| **Body** | Inter or SF Pro | Regular (400) | 18-24pt |
| **Data/Metrics** | Inter or SF Pro | Bold (700) | 64-96pt |
| **Captions** | Inter or SF Pro | Regular (400) | 14-16pt |

### Design Principles

1. **Minimal, clean layouts** - Lots of white space
2. **Data-forward** - Large numbers, clear charts
3. **Enterprise feel** - Not consumer/startup aesthetic
4. **Healthcare credibility** - Professional, trustworthy
5. **One idea per slide** - Clear hierarchy, easy to scan

## Presentation Tips

### Slide Animations

- Use subtle fade or slide transitions
- Avoid flashy effects (not appropriate for enterprise)
- Reveal data points sequentially on Traction slide
- Animate workflow on Product Demo slide

### Speaker Notes

- Include speaker notes on each slide
- 3-5 bullet points maximum
- Key stats and talking points

### Backup Slides

Prepare these appendix slides to pull up during Q&A:
- Technical architecture deep dive
- Security and compliance details
- Customer pipeline breakdown
- Comparable transactions
- Investor-specific positioning

## Designer Notes

1. **Reference Decks**: Look at Pitch (pitch.com) templates for inspiration, particularly their "Enterprise SaaS" and "Healthcare" categories

2. **Avoid**: Startup clichés, rocket ships, overly playful imagery, stock photos of doctors

3. **Embrace**: Data visualization, clean architecture diagrams, professional typography

4. **Test**: View slides on laptop and projector; ensure readability at distance

5. **Accessibility**: Ensure sufficient color contrast, alt text for images
