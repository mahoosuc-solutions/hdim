## Ad Format Strategy

### Format 1: LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms (60% budget)

**Why Lead Gen Forms?**
- 3x higher conversion rate than landing page clicks
- Pre-filled forms (name, title, company) reduce friction
- Leads captured in-platform (no external redirect)
- Mobile-optimized for C-suite on-the-go

**Form Structure**:
- **Headline**: "See How [Hospital Name] Reduced Prior Auth Time by 82%"
- **Description**: "Download our case study and schedule a 15-min demo with our healthcare IT team"
- **Fields**:
  - First Name (pre-filled)
  - Last Name (pre-filled)
  - Work Email (pre-filled)
  - Company (pre-filled)
  - Job Title (pre-filled)
  - Current EHR System (dropdown: Epic, Cerner, AllScripts, Other)
  - Biggest Integration Challenge (custom question)
- **Thank You Message**: "Thanks! Check your email for the case study. Our team will reach out within 24 hours to schedule your demo."

**Expected Performance**:
- Form Open Rate: 12-15%
- Form Completion Rate: 40-50%
- Cost Per Lead: $165-$200

---

### Format 2: Sponsored Content - Single Image (30% budget)

**Why Sponsored Content?**
- Builds brand awareness before lead capture
- Retargeting pool for Lead Gen Forms
- Social proof via likes/comments from industry peers

**Content Types**:
1. **Customer Success Stories** (40% of Sponsored Content budget)
   - "How [Hospital Name] Achieved 80% Faster Prior Authorizations"
   - Visual: Before/After metrics infographic

2. **Problem-Focused** (35% of Sponsored Content budget)
   - "Are you spending $500K/year maintaining point-to-point EHR interfaces?"
   - Visual: Cost breakdown chart

3. **Thought Leadership** (25% of Sponsored Content budget)
   - "The Hidden Cost of Non-FHIR Compliant Integrations"
   - Visual: Industry report cover

**Expected Performance**:
- CTR: 0.50-0.75%
- CPC: $8-12
- Cost Per Landing Page Visit: $10-15

---

### Format 3: Sponsored InMail / Message Ads (10% budget)

**Why Message Ads?**
- Direct outreach to C-suite (CIOs, CMIOs)
- 50-60% open rates (higher than email)
- Personalization at scale

**Message Template**:
```
Subject: [First Name], quick question about [Company]'s prior auth process

Hi [First Name],

I noticed [Company] is using [EHR System]. We recently helped [Similar Hospital]
reduce their prior authorization time from 3.5 days to 8 hours using FHIR-native
integration—without replacing their existing EHR.

Would a 15-minute demo showing how we did it be valuable?

[CTA Button: Book 15-Min Demo]

Best,
[Sales Rep Name]
HDIM Healthcare Integration
```

**Targeting**: C-suite only (CIO, CMIO, CTO)
**Send Frequency**: Max 1 message per 60 days
**Expected Performance**:
- Open Rate: 50-60%
- CTR: 3-5%
- Cost Per Send: $0.50-$1.00
- CPL: $100-$150 (higher quality than Lead Gen Forms)

## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Risk 1: High Cost Per Lead (CPL >$300)
**Likelihood**: Medium
**Impact**: High (budget burn without results)

**Mitigation Strategies**:
1. Start with tight targeting (CIO/CMIO only, 500+ employees)
2. Use Lead Gen Forms (not landing pages) for lower friction
3. Pause ad sets with CPL >$300 after 50 impressions
4. Test 3 creative angles (data, problem, contrarian) to find winner
5. Retarget website visitors (5x cheaper CPL)

**Early Warning Signs**:
- CTR <0.40% after 5,000 impressions
- Form open rate <8%
- Form completion rate <30%

---

### Risk 2: Low Lead Quality (Unqualified Leads)
**Likelihood**: Low-Medium
**Impact**: High (wasted sales time, poor ROI)

**Mitigation Strategies**:
1. Use LinkedIn's job title targeting (not just job function)
2. Add qualifying question to Lead Gen Form: "Current EHR System?"
3. Exclude job seniorities below "Director"
4. Implement lead scoring in CRM (Zoho)
5. Sales team qualifies within 24 hours

**Quality Criteria**:
- Works at hospital/health system with 200+ employees
- Job title: Director level or above
- Manages IT/clinical systems
- Has budget authority or strong influence

---

### Risk 3: Long Sales Cycle (>60 days)
**Likelihood**: Medium
**Impact**: Medium (longer to prove ROI)

**Mitigation Strategies**:
1. Focus messaging on urgent pain points (prior auth delays, compliance deadlines)
2. Offer 15-min "quick demo" (not 60-min sales pitch)
3. Email nurture sequence for leads not ready to demo
4. Retarget with case studies showing fast implementation (30-45 days)

**User indicated 30-60 day sales cycle**: This is a strength, not a risk. Emphasize fast time-to-value in messaging.

---

### Risk 4: LinkedIn Ad Fatigue (Frequency >4)
**Likelihood**: High (in months 2-3)
**Impact**: Medium (declining CTR)

**Mitigation Strategies**:
1. Rotate creative every 30 days (3 variants per audience)
2. Pause ads when frequency >3.5
3. Expand audience after 60 days (include IT Managers)
4. Introduce new ad formats (Document Ads, Video Ads in Month 3)
