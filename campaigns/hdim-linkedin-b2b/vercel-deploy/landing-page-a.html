Frequently Asked Questions

How long does implementation take?

60-90 days typical for full deployment. We start with a 30-day pilot on one workflow to prove value quickly.

Does HDIM replace our EHR?

No - HDIM integrates with your existing EHR system. We connect to Epic, Cerner, MEDITECH, AllScripts, and other FHIR-compliant systems.

What if we're already using Epic App Orchard?

HDIM is complementary. We handle cross-system integrations that App Orchard doesn't cover, especially payer connectivity and prior authorization.

Is HDIM FHIR-compliant?

Yes - built on HL7 FHIR R4 standards. We're fully compliant with the CMS 2026 interoperability mandate.

Is HDIM HIPAA compliant and BAA-ready?

Absolutely. HDIM is fully HIPAA compliant with enterprise-grade security. We can execute a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) within 24 hours. Our platform includes end-to-end encryption, audit logging, and role-based access controls.

Is this a production-ready platform or demo software?

HDIM is 100% production-ready. There are no demo modes or bypasses. Our platform is deployed with distributed tracing, production monitoring, and AI-powered testing infrastructure for enterprise reliability.

What's the pricing model?

Contact us for a custom quote based on your facility size and needs. We offer both subscription and per-transaction models.
