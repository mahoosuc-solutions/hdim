## 📊 Content Breakdown

### Skill 1: Discovery Coach (490 lines)

**5 Customer Personas:**
- CMO / VP Quality (primary buyer, HEDIS focus)
- Quality Coordinator (operational, time-pressed)
- Healthcare Provider (clinical, alert-fatigued)
- CFO / Finance Leader (ROI-focused)
- IT / Analytics Leader (technical, security-focused)

**Per Persona Included:**
- Opening hook (30-second credibility statement)
- 5-8 discovery questions with listen-for guidance
- Red flags (signs of bad qualification)
- Green flags (signs of good qualification)
- Expected objections
- Preferred communication style

**Call Scoring Rubric:**
```
Pain Discovery (0-20):    Did you uncover real pain?
Qualification (0-20):     Is this a real prospect?
Objection Handling (0-15): How well did you handle concerns?
Next Steps (0-20):        Did you get commitment?
Credibility (0-25):       Did they trust you?
────────────────────────
TOTAL (0-100):            Overall call effectiveness
```

**How It Works:**
1. Choose persona
2. Coach asks opening question
3. You respond
4. Coach gives real-time feedback
5. Loop continues for 20-30 min
6. At end: Get score breakdown + coaching

### Skill 2: Objection Handler (465 lines)

**6 Common Objections:**

1. **"Your pricing is too high"** (60% of conversations)
   - 3 reframes: Speed-to-impact, Feature comparison, Hidden costs
   - ROI model: Show quality bonus value
   - Competitive battlecard: Epic, Optum, home-built options

2. **"We could build this ourselves"** (40% of conversations)
   - 3 reframes: Opportunity cost, Hidden infrastructure, Data complexity
   - Build-vs-buy scorecard (timeline, cost, maintenance)
   - Real implementation estimates

3. **"Security & HIPAA concerns"** (80% of conversations)
   - HIPAA verification checklist
   - Data isolation explanation
   - Risk mitigation: Phased pilot approach
   - Compliance documentation references

4. **"Integration is too complex"** (50% of conversations)
   - Pre-built connector story (2-4 week timeline)
   - Data integration decision tree (FHIR/HL7/EDI)
   - Phase 1 test data approach

5. **"We need more time to decide"** (70% of conversations)
   - Cost of delay calculation (monthly opportunity cost)
   - Pilot as path forward (30 days, then decide)
   - Competitive urgency framing

6. **"We don't have budget"** (50% of conversations)
   - ROI-driven reallocation framework
   - Pilot as entry point (smaller initial spend)
   - Phase your deployment (Q1/Q2/Q3 spread)

**Per Objection Included:**
- 3 different reframe options
- Underlying concern analysis
- Competitive positioning
- ROI calculations
- Handling checklist

### Skill 3: Demo Designer (520 lines)

**5 Persona Demos:**

| Persona | Duration | Main Features | Focus |
|---------|----------|---|---|
| **CMO** | 30 min | Predictive gaps, coordinator dashboard, financial tracking | Leadership impact + board visibility |
| **Coordinator** | 20 min | Smart prioritization, provider engagement, workflow tracking | Time savings + provider adoption |
| **Provider** | 10 min | Alert examples, patient context, clinical narratives | Clinical utility + ease of use |
| **CFO** | 20 min | Financial tracking, ROI calculation, competitive scenario | Bottom-line impact + competitive advantage |
| **IT** | 30 min | Architecture, integration, data flow, compliance | Enterprise-readiness + implementation timeline |

**Per Demo Included:**
- Complete flow outline (by minute)
- Opening (establish credibility)
- Problem (show their pain visually)
- Solution (live feature walkthrough)
- Proof (metrics, references)
- Close (next steps)
- Talking points (what to say for each section)
- Interactive elements (what to ask)
- Materials checklist (what to prepare beforehand)

**Demo Lengths:**
- **15-min teaser:** 3-4 core features, no depth (exploratory calls)
- **30-min standard:** 5-6 features with rationale (decision-maker calls)
- **45-min deep-dive:** Full workflow, customization, edge cases (validation)

### Skill 4: Sales Manager (530 lines)

**Strategic Capabilities:**

1. **Deal Scoring Framework** (0-100 scale)
   - Buyer Quality (25 pts): Right person? Budget? Authority?
   - Pain Alignment (25 pts): Does their pain match our solution?
   - Competitive Position (20 pts): Are we the frontrunner?
   - Implementation Readiness (15 pts): Can they deploy in 4 weeks?
   - Momentum (15 pts): Are they moving forward?

   Output: Deal score + priority ranking

2. **Pipeline Analysis**
   - Stage-by-stage breakdown (discovery → demo → scoping → closed)
   - Probability by stage (20% → 40% → 70% → 90%)
   - Expected revenue calculation
   - Red flags to watch

   Output: Pipeline forecast + next steps

3. **Messaging Validation**
   - Take transcripts from 3+ calls
   - Analyze for engagement, differentiation, pain discovery
   - Score messaging effectiveness (0-10)
   - Recommendations for improvement

   Output: Effectiveness score + specific feedback

4. **Series A Readiness Scorecard**
   - Market Validation (40%): TAM, SAM, customer interviews
   - Traction & Revenue (40%): MRR, customers, growth rate
   - Team & Execution (10%): VP Sales, technical, advisors
   - Product & Compliance (10%): HIPAA, SOC2, uptime

   Output: Overall score + gap analysis + timeline to ready

5. **Strategic Coaching**
   - Market positioning: Should we focus on health plans or ACOs?
   - Deal prioritization: Which deals matter most?
   - Execution: What should we do this week?
