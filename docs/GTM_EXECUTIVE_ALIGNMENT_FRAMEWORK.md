## ⚠️ Risk Assessment

### Risk 1: Customer Acquisition Slower Than Projected
**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** High
**Mitigation:**
- Start with lower ACV segments (ACOs, clinical quality orgs) for quick wins
- Pilot program with 2-3 early customers in Week 4
- Focus on reference-ability for future deals

**Contingency:** Extend runway, reduce marketing spend, bootstrap path exists

---

### Risk 2: Competitive Pressure
**Probability:** High | **Impact:** Medium
**Mitigation:**
- Differentiate on pricing (PPPM vs. enterprise licensing)
- Emphasize care gap detection advantage
- Build sales team quickly to secure early relationships

**Contingency:** Accelerate competitive positioning, adjust pricing if needed

---

### Risk 3: VP Sales Hire Takes Longer
**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** Critical
**Mitigation:**
- Begin recruiting NOW, not after approval
- CEO personally involved in sourcing
- Target passive candidates with existing relationships
- Consider interim VP Sales contractor for first 60 days

**Contingency:** Interim VP Sales, CEO leads sales initially

---

### Risk 4: Product Integration Challenges
**Probability:** Low | **Impact:** High
**Mitigation:**
- Pre-built EHR connectors (HL7v2, CDA, FHIR)
- Dedicated integration engineer for first customers
- Extensive API documentation (62 endpoints documented)

**Contingency:** Product team assigned to customer integrations
