## Part 3: Sales & Business Development Strategy

### Sales Approach by Segment

**Segment 1: Health Plans - Enterprise Sales**
```
Target: CMS RADV Programs, Quality Reporting Requirements

Approach:
1. Identify target health plans (Top 20 by enrollment)
2. Build business case: HEDIS compliance + care gap ROI
3. Pilot program: 250K-1M members, 6-month evaluation
4. Demonstrate PPPM economics vs. legacy systems
5. Enterprise contract (3-5 year terms)

Sales Cycle: 12 months average
Contract: $1-3M annually
```

**Segment 2: ACOs - Mid-Market Sales**
```
Target: Shared savings programs, quality measure improvement

Approach:
1. Identify target ACOs (500+ attributed lives)
2. Quality measure assessment and benchmark analysis
3. 90-day pilot with 2-3 key quality measures
4. Results presentation: improved compliance, care gaps identified
5. Multi-year contract with measurement commitments

Sales Cycle: 6 months average
Contract: $500K-$2M annually
```

**Segment 3: Health Systems - Strategic Partnerships**
```
Target: Quality/Medical Affairs departments

Approach:
1. Executive briefing (CMO, Quality VP, CIO)
2. Technical assessment (EHR integration, data readiness)
3. Proof of concept (60-90 days, 2-3 use cases)
4. Enterprise deployment (phased by department)
5. Long-term strategic partnership

Sales Cycle: 12-18 months average
Contract: $2-5M annually
```

**Segment 4: Clinical Quality Organizations - High-Volume Sales**
```
Target: Smaller quality organizations, consultancies

Approach:
1. Freemium/SaaS model (cloud-based)
2. Self-service onboarding (via documentation)
3. Measure library pre-loaded (80+ HEDIS)
4. Quick wins in 30 days
5. Upgrade to enterprise or API-heavy contracts

Sales Cycle: 1-3 months average
Contract: $100K-$500K annually
```
