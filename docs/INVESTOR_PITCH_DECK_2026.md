## SLIDE 1: The Opportunity

### Healthcare Quality Management is a $12.4 Billion Market Growing 15% Annually

**Problem:** Healthcare organizations struggle to:
- Evaluate quality measures (HEDIS, CQL) at scale
- Identify and close care gaps systematically
- Integrate fragmented healthcare data
- Report on quality metrics for value-based contracts

**Solution:** HDIM is a modern, cloud-native platform that:
- Automates quality measure evaluation (80+ HEDIS measures)
- Detects and tracks care gaps in real-time
- Integrates with any healthcare system via FHIR R4
- Generates comprehensive quality reports

**Market Window:** Wide open - incumbents using legacy technology, customers demanding modern solutions
