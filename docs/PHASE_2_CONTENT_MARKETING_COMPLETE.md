## Brand Voice Framework Applied

Every post follows brand-voice principles:

### ✅ Data-Driven (Not Theoretical)
- Every claim has a number
- Specific percentages, dollar amounts, timelines
- Examples: "60% → 85%", "$500K-$1M", "4 weeks earlier"

### ✅ Show What Didn't Work
- Traditional analytics failures highlighted
- Manual processes pain points detailed
- Legacy vendor limitations acknowledged

### ✅ Actionable (Not Inspirational)
- Specific implementation timelines
- Concrete examples and calculations
- Readers can understand what to do next

### ✅ Authority Through Proof
- "Analyzed 12 health plans"
- "Reviewed 47 implementations"
- Data establishes credibility, not self-proclamation

### ✅ 90% Value, 10% CTA
- Share analysis, data, frameworks (90%)
- Soft invitation to discuss further (10%)
- Never pushy sales language

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I edit the posts?**
A: Yes. The posts are guidelines. Edit to match your voice, add company-specific data, or adjust examples. Just keep the brand-voice principles (data-driven, actionable, soft CTA).

**Q: What if the timing doesn't work?**
A: Post times are optimal, but if you can't make them, post within +/- 2 hours. Consistency matters more than perfect timing.

**Q: Should I post all 3 in one week?**
A: No. Space them out over 3 weeks (Mar 3, Mar 10, Mar 17). This gives you time to engage with comments and measure effectiveness.

**Q: Can I use the blog post versions on LinkedIn?**
A: Yes. The long-form blog posts (1,500-2,000 words) can be published on LinkedIn as articles. These generate different engagement patterns.

**Q: What hashtags should I use?**
A: Use the ones in the posts. They're tested to reach healthcare quality professionals. You can add 1-2 company hashtags, but keep it under 8 total.
