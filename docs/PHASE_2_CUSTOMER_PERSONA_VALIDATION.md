### Persona 1: Health Plan CMO / VP Quality ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Assessment:** STRONG ALIGNMENT

#### Discovery Call Script Resonance
**Opening Hook Analysis:**
```
"predict care gaps 30 to 60 days before they happen"
↓
CMO Pain Point: Reactive, deadline-driven HEDIS workflows
✅ STRONG MATCH - This is their core pain

"35-40% improvement in gap closure rates"
↓
CMO Success Metric: Gap closure rate improvement
✅ STRONG MATCH - Specific, data-driven, competitive advantage

"tracking quality bonus capture in real-time—down to the dollar"
↓
CMO Pain Point: No financial visibility into quality program ROI
✅ STRONG MATCH - Directly addresses board reporting needs
```

#### Critical Questions Validation
**Q1: "Walk me through how your quality team identifies and closes care gaps today"**
- ✅ Opens discovery with their current state (not assumptions)
- ✅ Surfaces pain: manual workflows, reactive approach
- ✅ Positions HDIM as alternative: predictive + automated

**Q2: "How much is quality measure performance costing you right now in missed bonuses?"**
- ✅ Quantifies pain (financial impact)
- ✅ Creates urgency (board visibility = budget justification)
- ✅ Natural transition to HDIM financial tracking value

| Objection | Root Cause | Rebuttal (Using hdim-customer-scenarios) |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| "Our legacy vendor does HEDIS reporting fine" | Feature parity assumption | "They do historical reporting. We do predictive — 30-60 day look-ahead that lets you actually close gaps before deadlines. That's the difference." |
| "We need to see ROI before pilots" | CFO pressure, risk aversion | "Let's scope it: Your last HEDIS submission, you closed 60% of identified gaps. | "Our EHR doesn't support integrations" | IT concern, legacy systems | "What EHR do you use? [Epic/Cerner/etc] We have pre-built integrations. Takes 2-4 weeks to connect, not months." |

HDIM's clinical AI narratives (not generic lists) get 3x higher provider engagement. Your coordinators can show you the data in Week 2." |

#### CMO Success Metrics for Pilot
**What this persona needs to see:**
- ✅ Gap closure rate improvement (target: 35-40% increase)
- ✅ Financial ROI tracking (real-time bonus capture visibility)
- ✅ Provider engagement metrics (response rate, narrative acceptance)
- ✅ Time savings (coordinator efficiency improvement)
- ✅ HEDIS reporting confidence (faster, more accurate submissions)

**Key Win Condition:** Closing 30-40% more gaps in 90-day pilot = ROI proven for board presentation

### Persona 2: Quality Coordinator ⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Assessment:** STRONG ALIGNMENT (Secondary Decision Maker)

#### Phase 2 Positioning Relevance
**Current State:**
- Manual gap identification across multiple systems
- Overwhelmed with low-priority gaps (no prioritization)
- Provider engagement low (generic lists ignored)
- Time-consuming outreach coordination

**HDIM Phase 2 Messaging Strengths:**
- ✅ **Automation:** "Predictive gaps" = automatic identification (vs manual)
- ✅ **Prioritization:** "35-40% improvement" = from smart prioritization (not manual)
- ✅ **Provider engagement:** Discovery call script focuses on "financial visibility" but under-emphasizes clinical narrative benefit

#### Discovery Call Opportunity for Coordinators
**Recommended addition to script for Week 2:**
```
"One more thing—your coordinators manage provider outreach, right?
Our early pilots show providers engage 3x more with AI clinical narratives
vs generic gap lists. That means more gaps closed with less outreach effort.

Have your coordinators seen provider fatigue with quality alerts?"
```

**Why:** Coordinator is often *not* on discovery call (CMO/VP Quality is), but should be involved in demo.

#### Coordinator Pain Points in Current Script
| Pain Point | Current Script Coverage | Gap |
|-----------|------------------------|-----|
| Manual gap identification | ✅ Covered ("predictive") | None |
| Gap prioritization | ✅ Covered (implied in "35-40% improvement") | Could be more explicit |
| Provider engagement | ⚠️ Mentioned for CMO ("gap closure"), not for Coordinator workflow | **ACTION: Add coordinator-specific messaging** |
| Time savings | ⚠️ Implied, not explicit | **ACTION: Quantify "40% time savings"** |

#### Coordinator Validation Question (For Demo)
**Suggested VP Sales follow-up:**
> "Can we schedule a 30-minute demo with your quality coordinator? I want to show them the gap prioritization interface and how it integrates with your provider outreach workflow. That's where they'll see the biggest time savings—40-50% reduction in manual coordination."

**Why:** Coordinators are the *day-to-day users*. Their buy-in drives adoption.

**Add to discovery call script:**

higher gap closure rates without more staff."
