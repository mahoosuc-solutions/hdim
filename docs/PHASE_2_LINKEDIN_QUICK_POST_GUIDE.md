## 🚫 What NOT to Do

**Don't:**
- ❌ Use salesy language ("Book a call now!" "Limited time!")
- ❌ Post at random times (stick to optimal Tue/Wed/Thu mornings)
- ❌ Ignore comments (respond to every one within 4 hours)
- ❌ Over-promote (90% value, 10% soft CTA)
- ❌ Use generic healthcare buzzwords ("transform," "disrupt," "synergy")
- ❌ Post too frequently (quality > quantity)

**Do:**
- ✅ Use specific data (numbers, percentages, dollars)
- ✅ Share failures (builds credibility)
- ✅ Answer thoughtfully (not just "thanks for the interest")
- ✅ Offer value-add (not sales pitch)
- ✅ Use real language (like you're talking to a colleague)
- ✅ Engage genuinely (this builds trust)
