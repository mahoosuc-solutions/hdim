✅ Hybrid approach: Gradient boosting + feature engineering + explainability layer

• Claims patterns (traditional triggers)

• 88% accuracy (top quartile for healthcare)
• 5% false positive rate (coordinators actually use the recommendations)
• 92% provider-understandable (they can explain it to patients)

The surprising insight:
Better prediction > higher accuracy number

If they understand it, 3x higher engagement.

• Real-time prediction (< 2 second latency)
• Multi-tenant isolation (HIPAA-compliant data separation)
• FHIR integration (works with your existing EHR)
• Continuous learning (model improves weekly from provider feedback)

For the non-technical folks:
We built a system that predicts care gaps BEFORE they happen,
explains why to the provider, and gets 87% of them closed.

That's a technical achievement, but the business outcome is the real story:
$1M+ additional bonuses per health plan annually.
