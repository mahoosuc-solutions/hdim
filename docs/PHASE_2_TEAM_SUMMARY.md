## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Do I need to read all the documents?**
A: No. Each role has specific "Read These" documents. CEO reads 3, VP Sales reads 2, etc.

**Q: Can I modify the LinkedIn posts?**
A: Yes, customize to match your voice. Keep the data and brand-voice principles (actionable, data-driven, soft CTA).

**Q: What if the posting times don't work for me?**
A: Post within ±2 hours of recommended time. Consistency matters more than perfect timing.

**Q: Should we post all 3 posts in one week?**
A: No. Space them out (Mar 3, 10, 17) to allow time for engagement and measurement.

**Q: When do we refine the messaging?**
A: After first 10 discovery calls (Week 2). Gather data, update script based on what resonates.

**Q: How do we track what's working?**
A: Daily: Share discovery call learnings with team. Weekly: Review LinkedIn metrics, update objection handling based on new questions.
