## CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS FOR PHASE 2

### 1. VP Sales Excellence (BLOCKING)
- **Issue:** VP Sales hire is critical path. Any delay compounds all downstream activities
- **Mitigation:**
  - Pre-hire prep: All collateral ready, targets identified, CRM configured
  - On-day 1: Full onboarding with immediate credibility (CEO + founders visible)
  - Expectation: 50-100 calls/month, 1-2 pilots/month (based on industry norms)
- **Success Metric:** 10 calls/week = on track

### 2. Product Validation (BLOCKING)
- **Issue:** Product must work flawlessly for first pilots or customers lose trust
- **Mitigation:**
  - Engineering team dedicated to pilot support (not new features)
  - Pilot environment = production-grade infrastructure
  - 24-hour response SLA on any issues
  - Daily standup during customer implementations
- **Success Metric:** 99.9% uptime during pilot period, zero critical issues

### 3. Sales Collateral Credibility
- **Issue:** VP Sales needs compelling materials that salespeople trust
- **Mitigation:**
  - Weekly feedback loop: Customer feedback → collateral updates
  - A/B testing: Track which pitches advance to demos most
  - Customer testimonials: Leverage investor testimonials until real customers have results
  - ROI calculator: Specific financial case for healthcare payers
- **Success Metric:** 10%+ demo advancement rate (industry standard 5-15%)

### 4. Marketing-Sales Alignment
- **Issue:** VP Sales may view marketing as slow/ineffective if timeline isn't aligned
- **Mitigation:**
  - Weekly sync: Sales team + marketing team (every Wednesday)
  - Sales requests → marketing creates assets same day/next day
  - Marketing provides warm leads to sales
  - Joint metrics dashboard (sales pipeline + marketing metrics)
- **Success Metric:** Marketing generating 5-10 inbound leads/month by Mar 31

### 5. Customer Success Readiness
- **Issue:** First pilots are existential. Any implementation failure damages brand
- **Mitigation:**
  - Dedicated success manager per pilot (no other responsibilities)
  - Customer on success metrics dashboard (visible success)
  - Weekly check-ins (not just when there's a problem)
  - Success story documented (for Phase 3 case study)
- **Success Metric:** Pilot 1 achieves defined success metrics by Jun 30

## CONTINGENCY PLANS

### If VP Sales Hire Delayed (CRITICAL)
**Scenario:** VP Sales unavailable until mid-March or later

**Contingency:**
- CEO assumes VP Sales role for first 2 weeks
- External recruiter continues search (expedited)
- All sales activities continue with CEO + sales engineer
- Target: Still 20-30 calls by month-end
- Plan: Hire interim VP Sales (contract-based) if permanent hire not ready

---

### If First Pilot Customer Falls Through
**Scenario:** Customer doesn't sign LOI by end of month

**Contingency:**
- Shift focus to Customer 2 & 3 (accelerate closures)
- Target 2 pilots in April (instead of May)
- Extend discovery calls to 40-50 to find alternative pilots
- Offer more favorable pilot terms (discounted, shorter term)
- Plan: Still achieve pilot revenue in Q2

---

### If Website Launch Delays
**Scenario:** Website not ready until April

**Contingency:**
- Launch with MVP (product overview + contact form)
- Sales doesn't wait for website (manual outreach continues)
- Marketing uses LinkedIn + email as primary channels
- Website can be updated quickly during development
- Plan: Full-featured website within 2 weeks of initial launch

---

### If Product Issues Emerge During Pilots
**Scenario:** Pilot customer discovers critical bug in product

**Contingency:**
- Engineering team drops all other work (shift left priorities)
- Product manager owns customer issue resolution
- Daily updates to customer (transparency)
- Workaround provided if possible
- Root cause fix + regression tests
- Plan: Restore customer confidence within 48 hours
