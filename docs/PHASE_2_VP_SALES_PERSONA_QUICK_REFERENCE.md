# VP Sales Quick Reference: Persona-Specific Messaging

**Use this guide during discovery calls to match messaging to your buyer.**

---

## 📊 Quick Persona Identifier

**Look for these titles in the initial intro:**

| Title | Persona | Primary Hook | Success Metric |
|-------|---------|--------------|-----------------|
| CMO, VP Quality, Chief Medical Officer | CMO | "Predictive gaps 30-60 days ahead" | Gap closure +35-40% |
| Quality Manager, Quality Coordinator, Quality Analyst | Coordinator | "Smart prioritization + automation" | Time savings -40% |
| Physician, Nurse, Care Manager | Provider | "Clinical AI narratives providers engage with" | Engagement +3x |
| CFO, VP Finance, Controller | CFO | "Quality bonus tracking down to the dollar" | ROI = $20:1 |
| VP IT, CIO, Analytics Director | IT | "FHIR-native, ECDS-ready, 2-4 week integration" | Timeline + compliance |

## 🎯 Persona-Specific Opening Hooks (30-45 seconds)

### CMO / VP Quality (Primary Buyer) ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
```
"HDIM isn't another HEDIS vendor. We predict gaps 30-60 days ahead
so your team has TIME to close them—not scrambling at deadline.

Early pilots: 35-40% improvement in gap closure rates.
Real ROI: More gaps closed = more quality bonuses captured.

Want to see how this works for a health plan your size?"
```
**Tone:** Confident, consultative
**Listen for:** HEDIS timeline pressure, coordinator overwhelm, board visibility

---

### Quality Coordinator (Secondary Buyer) ⭐⭐⭐⭐
```
"Your coordinators are drowning in generic gap lists, right?

HDIM changes that: Smart prioritization (top 3 gaps per patient),
AI provider narratives they actually read, workflow integration.

Our pilots show coordinators save 40% time on outreach.
Providers engage 3x more. More gaps closed without more staff."
```
**Tone:** Empathetic, practical
**Listen for:** Manual processes, provider engagement issues, time pressure

---

### Healthcare Provider (Tertiary Buyer) ⭐⭐⭐
```
"Your coordinators mention care gaps, but those generic lists are noise, right?

HDIM's different: AI clinical summaries with patient context.
Not 'close this gap.' More like:

'Mrs. Smith: Diabetes, HbA1c 8.2% (target <7%).
Last test 8 months ago. Recommend: Order A1C, adjust meds.'

Providers tell us this is the first quality alert they actually want to read."
```
**Tone:** Clinical, practical, empowering
**Listen for:** Alert fatigue, clinical utility concerns, workflow disruption

---

### CFO / Finance Leader ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
```
"Your quality program costs money. What's the actual ROI?

HDIM quantifies it: Early pilots investing $50K captured $1M+
in additional quality bonuses. Phase 3 adds real-time tracking.

But the real win is Month 1: 35-40% more gaps closed =
```

**Tone:** Numbers-driven, practical, ROI-focused

```
"You're thinking FHIR compliance, integration timeline, security, scale.

(Epic, Cerner) cut implementation from 8-12 weeks (legacy) to 2-4 weeks.

HIPAA/SOC2 compliant. Integration validation in Week 1 of pilot."
```
**Tone:** Technical, precise, architecture-focused

## ⚡ Power Questions by Persona

### For CMO/VP Quality
1. "Walk me through your HEDIS submission timeline. When do gaps get identified?"
2. "How much quality bonus at risk if you miss Star rating targets?"
3. "What percent of identified gaps actually close before deadline?"

**Follow-up:** "That gap closure rate—is that limited by timeline, provider engagement, or something else?"

---

### For Quality Coordinator
1. "What's your biggest workflow pain right now?"
2. "How long does it take from 'gap identified' to 'gap closed'?"
3. "When do your providers typically respond to gap requests?"

**Follow-up:** "What would change if gaps were pre-prioritized for you?"

---

### For Healthcare Provider
1. "How many quality alerts does your team typically get per week?"
2. "When you see a care gap alert, is there enough context to act immediately?"
3. "What would make quality alerts actually useful vs noise?"

**Follow-up:** "Would clinical AI narratives with specific patient data help?"

---

### For CFO/Finance Leader
1. "How much are you currently capturing in quality bonuses vs potential?"
2. "Do you have real-time visibility into quality bonus performance?"
3. "What's the current cost of your quality program vs benefit?"

**Follow-up:** "What if we could quantify exactly which gaps matter financially?"

---

### For IT/Analytics Leader
1. "What EHR systems do you run? Current integration approach?"
2. "What's your timeline for ECDS transition (2030 digital-only HEDIS)?"
3. "How critical is multi-tenant data isolation for your use case?"

**Follow-up:** "How long would typical integrations take with your current vendors?"

## 🚫 Common Objections & Persona-Specific Responses

### "Our legacy vendor already does this"
**CMO/VP Quality Response:**
> "They do historical HEDIS reporting. We predict gaps 30-60 days ahead.
> That's the difference. Historical reporting = reactive. Predictive = proactive."

**IT Response:**
> "What integration timeline did they require? FHIR compliance? We're 2-4 weeks
> FHIR-native vs their traditional ETL approach."

---

### "We need to see ROI before we pilot"
**CFO Response:**
> "Let's do the math: Your plan left roughly $X in quality bonuses last year.
> Even a 20% improvement on gap closure = $Y additional capture.
> Pilot cost is $Z. ROI is clearly positive. Question is: how confident are you
> that 20% improvement is achievable?"

**CMO Response:**
> "I understand. How about this: Your last HEDIS cycle, you closed 60% of gaps.
> We're seeing 85%+ in pilots. On your member size, that's $X in upside.
> Let's scope it at risk—you pay if you don't see at least 30% improvement."

---

### "Our provider network won't use this"
**CMO Response:**
> "That's the real problem, right? Your current gap closure rate reflects
> low provider engagement. HDIM's clinical AI narratives get 3x higher
> provider engagement. Show your coordinators the demo—they'll tell you
> providers will actually read these."

**Coordinator Response:**
> "Your providers ignore generic lists. These aren't generic. Show them
> one AI narrative vs a traditional gap alert. They'll tell you the difference."

---

### "Integration will take too long"
**IT Response:**
> "What's your current vendor integration timeline? Most quoted 8-12 weeks.
> Our pre-built integrations (Epic, Cerner) are 2-4 weeks. We validate in
> Week 1 and you're live. What EHR do you use?"

---

### "We don't have budget for pilots right now"
**CFO Response:**
> "Most pilots pay for themselves in 60-90 days through bonus capture alone.
> What if we structured it: Pilot investment, then pay based on results?
> Your team can quantify gap closure improvement Week 4-6."

## 📋 Discovery Call Closing (What to Ask Before Hanging Up)

**If positive signals (2+ pain points resonated):**
```
"I'd love to get your team across HDIM. How about this:
- 30-minute demo with your quality coordinator (show the workflow)
- 15-minute IT architecture chat with your team (show integration)
- Then we scope a 90-day pilot with defined success metrics

Does next week work? I'll send a calendar link."
```

**If neutral/unclear signals (1 pain point resonated):**
```
"This might not be the right fit, or we need to understand your situation better.
Let's bring your [missing persona] in—I think they'll find it useful.
Can we schedule a follow-up with them?"
```

**If negative signals (no pain resonance):**
```
"Thanks for your time. Keep HDIM in mind if your workflow challenges change.
Can I send you a one-pager for your team?"
```

## 🎬 Demo Pathways by Persona

### For CMO/VP Quality
**Focus:** Predictive gaps → Financial ROI
1. Start with HEDIS timeline visualization
2. Show gap prioritization algorithm (risk × closure likelihood × financial impact)
3. Show financial ROI tracking ("quality bonus captured vs at risk")
4. Show provider engagement metrics (gap closure rate improvement)

### For Quality Coordinator
**Focus:** Daily workflow integration
1. Start with current gap import (auto-populated)
2. Show gap prioritization interface (top 3 by risk/impact)
3. Show AI provider narrative generation
4. Show workflow tracking (closure status dashboard)

### For Healthcare Provider
**Focus:** Clinical utility, time savings
1. Start with "pre-visit summary" showing top 3 gaps with AI narrative
2. Show one-click actions (order A1C, schedule mammogram, etc.)
3. Show integration with existing EHR workflow
4. Time it: "This is your full prep summary in 30 seconds"

### For CFO/Finance Leader
**Focus:** Financial ROI (Phase 3 preview)
1. Show Phase 2: gap closure improvement data
2. Show Phase 3 preview: financial ROI dashboard (coming May/June)
3. Show calculation: "Gap closure +35% = $1M+ bonus capture"
4. Show scalability: "This model applies across entire member population"

### For IT/Analytics Leader
**Focus:** Technical architecture, integration
1. Show FHIR architecture (API-first design)
2. Show pre-built integrations (Epic, Cerner, claims data)
3. Show multi-tenant data isolation (enterprise security)
4. Show integration timeline (2-4 weeks estimated)
