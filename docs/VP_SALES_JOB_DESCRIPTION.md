## 🎯 Role Overview

HDIM is seeking an experienced **VP Sales** to lead go-to-market for a production-ready healthcare platform targeting Series Seed/A funding. You'll build and lead a sales organization from day one, establishing relationships with health plans, ACOs, and health systems to achieve $5M+ ARR in Year 1.

**This is NOT a typical SaaS sales role.** Healthcare IT is different—longer sales cycles, multiple stakeholders, regulatory complexity, complex integrations. We need someone who understands this landscape.

### Why This Role Matters

- **Market Window:** $12.4B TAM, 15% CAGR - we're entering at the right moment
- **Product Ready:** v2.7.0 production-ready, HIPAA verified, 51 microservices, 613+ tests
- **Differentiation:** 7 competitive advantages vs. Optum, Allscripts, Change Healthcare
- **Unit Economics:** 50:1 LTV/CAC (vs 3:1 industry standard), 3.2-month payback period
- **Your Impact:** You'll drive $5M → $45-60M+ ARR over 3 years, build a world-class sales team

## 📋 Key Responsibilities

### Month 1-2: Foundation & Team Building
- **Territory Planning:** Design sales territories (4 geographic regions + segments)
- **Team Hiring:** Recruit 2-3 Account Executives ($80-120K + commission)
- **Sales Infrastructure:** Establish CRM (Salesforce or HubSpot), sales methodology, comp plans
- **Sales Enablement:** Create collateral (pitch decks, ROI calculator, competitive battlecards)
- **Market Assessment:** Validate customer segments, refine targeting (30 health plans minimum)

### Month 2-3: Customer Acquisition
- **Pipeline Generation:** Execute customer discovery outreach (50-100 calls)
- **Pilot Program:** Close 2-3 pilot customers (target: By end of March)
- **Customer Success:** Ensure pilots succeed (become references for future sales)
- **Deal Management:** Lead largest deals personally ($2M+ ACV opportunities)
- **Competitive Response:** Monitor competitors (Optum, Allscripts, Change Healthcare), refine positioning

### Ongoing: Organization Building
- **AE Development:** Mentor and develop Account Executives
- **Quota Management:** Establish $5M Year 1 ARR target, track pipeline and conversion
- **Customer Expansion:** Expand within customers (upsell new modules, additional users)
- **Partner Development:** Identify and manage integration partners (EHR vendors, consultants)
- **Board Reporting:** Monthly sales metrics, quarterly board updates

### Healthcare Market Overview
- **4 customer segments:**
  - Health Plans (800+ targets, $1-3M ACV, 12-month cycle)
  - ACOs (500+ targets, $500K-$2M ACV, 6-month cycle)
  - Health Systems (200+ targets, $2-5M ACV, 12-18 month cycle)
  - Clinical Quality Orgs (1,500+ targets, $100K-$500K ACV, 1-3 month cycle)

### Competitive Landscape
| Competitor | Strength | HDIM Advantage |
|------------|----------|-----------------|
| Optum | Market leader, integrated | Lower cost, better APIs, transparent pricing |
| Allscripts | EHR integrated | Pure-play quality focus, better care gap detection |
| Change Healthcare | Claims data | Real-time FHIR integration, modern stack |
| IBM Watson Health | AI/ML | Transparent pricing, enterprise-ready, easier integration |

### HDIM Differentiation (7 Key Advantages)
1. Modern technology stack (Spring Boot 3, Angular 17, Kubernetes-native)
2. Transparent PPPM pricing (vs. black-box enterprise licensing)
3. Open FHIR-native architecture (200+ APIs, extensible)
4. Superior care gap detection (automated, real-time, integrated)
5. Enterprise-ready compliance (HIPAA verified, 100% API audit)
6. Deployment flexibility (cloud, on-prem, hybrid, air-gapped)
7. Modern development practices (CI/CD, 613+ tests, OpenTelemetry tracing)
