## CONTINGENCY SCENARIOS

### If Behind on Hiring
- **Scenario:** VP Sales hire delayed to April
- **Mitigation:** CEO covers sales for first month, extend Phase 2 timeline
- **Impact:** Phase 3 pushed 2-4 weeks, still achievable

### If Pilot Customer Fails
- **Scenario:** First customer project misses milestones
- **Mitigation:** Daily support, engineering team drops other work, focus on success
- **Impact:** Timeline may slip 4 weeks, but fixable

### If Market Demand Lower
- **Scenario:** Fewer discovery calls than expected (20 instead of 50)
- **Mitigation:** Expand to adjacent markets, increase marketing spend, adjust pricing
- **Impact:** Extended Phase 2, later start to Phase 3

### If Series A Market Deteriorates
- **Scenario:** Investors become cautious, funding harder to raise
- **Mitigation:** Focus on profitability path, bootstrap if needed, smaller Series A
- **Impact:** Year 2 growth slower, but company survives
