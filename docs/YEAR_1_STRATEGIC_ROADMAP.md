## RISK MANAGEMENT

### Critical Risks & Mitigation

#### Risk 1: VP Sales Hiring Delay
**Impact:** Cascading delay to all sales activities
**Probability:** Medium (hiring marketplace competitive)
**Mitigation:**
- Hire search begins immediately (use recruiter)
- CEO ready to fill role for first 4 weeks
- Fallback: Contract VP Sales (if permanent hire delayed)
- **Plan B ARR:** Still achieve $100K+ with interim leadership

#### Risk 2: First Pilot Customer Failure
**Impact:** Damages credibility, slows subsequent sales
**Probability:** Low (customer selected for high probability of success)
**Mitigation:**
- Dedicated success manager per pilot
- Daily standups during critical period
- Weekly check-ins with customer
- Proactive issue resolution
- **Plan B:** Pivot to Customer 2 & 3 (close them faster)

#### Risk 3: Product Stability Issues
**Impact:** Pilot customer loses trust, reputational damage
**Probability:** Low (product proven in testing)
**Mitigation:**
- Engineering team on-call during pilots
- 99.9%+ uptime SLA commitment
- Daily health monitoring
- 24-hour issue response time
- **Plan B:** Rollback capability (revert to previous version)

#### Risk 4: Market Demand Lower Than Expected
**Impact:** Fewer customers, slower revenue growth
**Probability:** Low (validated with customer discovery)
**Mitigation:**
- Continue customer discovery (10 calls/week minimum)
- Adjust messaging based on feedback
- Expand to adjacent markets (ACOs, health systems)
- Aggressive marketing spend increase
- **Plan B:** Extend Phase 2 timeline, lower sales targets

#### Risk 5: Competitive Threat Emerges
**Impact:** Lose first-mover advantage, customer acquisition slows
**Probability:** Medium (healthcare IT attractive market)
**Mitigation:**
- Speed to first customer (Q1-Q2 2026)
- Establish customer relationships early
- Build switching costs (integrations, data)
- Continuous product innovation
- **Plan B:** Emphasize healthcare IT expertise vs generic competitors

#### Risk 6: Funding Unavailable for Series A
**Impact:** Limits growth in Phase 4 Q4, affects Year 2 plans
**Probability:** Low (strong market tailwinds, proven traction)
**Mitigation:**
- Maintain investor relationships throughout Year 1
- Share wins/metrics frequently
- Plan conservative bootstrap scenario
- Series A fundraising begins Sep 2026
- **Plan B:** Bootstrap to profitability (slower growth)
