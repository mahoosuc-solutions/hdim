## Pain Hypothesis
MO-PCA faces a compounding data integration crisis: 28 FQHCs running different EHRs must now submit UDS+ data in FHIR R4 format while simultaneously reporting quality measures to Medicaid MCOs and maintaining MOQuIN improvement metrics. Their existing Azara DRVS platform handles UDS reporting but lacks native FHIR R4 transformation, CQL-based measure evaluation, and cross-payer quality reconciliation. HDIM solves this by providing: (1) FHIR R4 data normalization across heterogeneous EHR sources, (2) automated CQL/HEDIS measure evaluation that maps directly to UDS+ reporting requirements, (3) a unified quality dashboard that consolidates MOQuIN measures, MCO reporting, and federal UDS compliance into a single pane of glass. The new Center of Healthcare Integration & Innovation creates a natural pilot venue for demonstrating HDIM capabilities to all 28 member centers simultaneously.
