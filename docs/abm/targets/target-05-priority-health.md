## Pain Hypothesis
Priority Health operates at the intersection of two pressures: (1) maintaining their 4.5 CMS Stars rating as Corewell Health completes the complex Spectrum Health + Beaumont Health merger integration, and (2) adapting to NCQA's accelerating measure expansion (7 new HEDIS measures + 4 ECDS transitions for MY 2026). As a provider-sponsored plan, they have the advantage of aligned incentives with their provider network but the disadvantage of system integration complexity — merging two health systems' quality measurement infrastructure, clinical workflows, and data pipelines while maintaining top-tier Stars performance. HDIM solves this by providing: (1) CQL-based HEDIS measure evaluation that automatically adapts to new and changed measures without custom development, (2) FHIR R4 data normalization that bridges Epic instances from the pre-merger Spectrum Health and Beaumont environments, (3) care gap identification at the member level that enables proactive outreach to close gaps before measurement periods end, (4) a quality dashboard that provides real-time Stars rating projection so leadership can prioritize interventions. The 240K MA members represent ~$2.4B in premium revenue; each half-star improvement drives ~$120M in quality bonus payments. The financial case for HDIM is measured in tens of millions.
