# Email Sequence: M&A / Expansion

**Trigger:** Organization announces acquisition, merger, health system partnership, or significant network expansion
**Urgency:** Medium — first touch within 30 days of announcement
**Best for archetypes:** 02 (MA Plan), 03 (ACO), 04 (Health System)
**Sequence duration:** 18 days, 3 touches

---

## Personalization Checklist

Before sending, verify each merge field is populated:

- [ ] `[ORG_NAME]` — organization name (confirm exact legal/brand name they use publicly)
- [ ] `[DECISION_MAKER_FIRST]` — recipient first name (verify spelling from LinkedIn)
- [ ] `[TRIGGER_DETAIL]` — specific M&A event (e.g., "your acquisition of Evolent's ACO management business" or "Mass General Brigham's acquisition of Fallon Health")
- [ ] `[PAIN_HYPOTHESIS]` — from research dossier (e.g., "integrating quality measurement across legacy and acquired systems creates a 6-18 month blind spot")
- [ ] `[ARCHETYPE_STAT]` — data point from matching archetype (e.g., "health systems integrating acquisitions typically find a 15-point quality variation between legacy and acquired sites")
- [ ] `[SENDER_NAME]` — Aaron
- [ ] `[ACQUIRED_ORG]` — name of the acquired/merging entity
- [ ] Confirm the deal timeline — announced vs. closed vs. regulatory review
- [ ] Check if the org has discussed integration plans publicly
- [ ] Identify whether EHR consolidation is part of the integration plan
- [ ] Research the quality profiles of both entities (Star ratings, HEDIS, UDS)

>

>

>
> [SENDER_NAME]

## Sequence Notes

- **If they reply at any touch:** Stop the sequence immediately. Move to direct conversation.
- **If they open but do not reply:** Proceed to the next touch on schedule.
- **If no opens after Touch 3:** Move to nurture track (monthly industry insights, no direct outreach for 60 days).
- **Regulatory review timing:** If the deal is in regulatory review (not yet closed), soften language from "navigating" to "planning for." Do not assume the deal will close.
- **Acquirer vs. acquired framing:** Always frame from the acquirer's perspective unless your contact is at the acquired organization. If contacting the acquired side, adjust to their uncertainty: "Regardless of how the integration unfolds, quality visibility across the combined entity will matter."
- **Integration timeline awareness:** Large health system acquisitions take 18-36 months to fully integrate. Quality measurement gaps exist throughout that period. Frame the value as "bridge infrastructure" that provides unified visibility while the underlying systems converge.
