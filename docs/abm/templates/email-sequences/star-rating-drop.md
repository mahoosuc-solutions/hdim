# Email Sequence: Star Rating Drop

**Trigger:** CMS releases Star ratings and organization drops (e.g., 4.0 to 3.5)
**Urgency:** Critical — first touch within 1 week of CMS release
**Best for archetypes:** 02 (MA Plan), also usable for 01 (FQHC) and 03 (ACO)
**Sequence duration:** 18 days, 3 touches

---

## Personalization Checklist

Before sending, verify each merge field is populated:

- [ ] `[ORG_NAME]` — organization name (confirm exact legal/brand name they use publicly)
- [ ] `[DECISION_MAKER_FIRST]` — recipient first name (verify spelling from LinkedIn)
- [ ] `[TRIGGER_DETAIL]` — specific rating change (e.g., "your Star rating dropped from 4.0 to 3.5")
- [ ] `[PAIN_HYPOTHESIS]` — from research dossier (e.g., "your delegated providers submit quality data on different timelines")
- [ ] `[ARCHETYPE_STAT]` — data point from matching archetype (e.g., "plans your size typically find 3,200+ addressable gaps")
- [ ] `[SENDER_NAME]` — Aaron
- [ ] Confirm the rating drop is real — cross-reference CMS public data before sending
- [ ] Check if org has issued a public statement about the rating change
- [ ] Identify 1-2 specific measures likely driving the drop (for Touch 2)

## Sequence Notes

- **If they reply at any touch:** Stop the sequence immediately. Move to direct conversation.
- **If they open but do not reply:** Proceed to the next touch on schedule.
- **If no opens after Touch 3:** Move to nurture track (monthly industry insights, no direct outreach for 60 days).
- **Out-of-office reply:** Pause sequence, resume when they return, adjust day count accordingly.
- **Delegated to someone else:** Start a fresh sequence for the new contact, referencing the original conversation.
