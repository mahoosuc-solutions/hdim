# Email Sequence: VBC Contract Announcement

**Trigger:** Organization announces new value-based care contract, ACO participation, MSSP track change, or Medicaid managed care partnership
**Urgency:** Medium — first touch within 30 days of announcement
**Best for archetypes:** 01 (FQHC), 02 (MA Plan), 03 (ACO), 05 (Specialty Network)
**Sequence duration:** 18 days, 3 touches

---

Before sending, verify each merge field is populated:

- [ ] `[ORG_NAME]` — organization name

- [ ] `[PAIN_HYPOTHESIS]` — from research dossier

## Sequence Notes

- **If they reply at any touch:** Stop the sequence immediately. Move to direct conversation.
- **If they open but do not reply:** Proceed to the next touch on schedule.
- **If no opens after Touch 3:** Move to nurture track (monthly industry insights, no direct outreach for 60 days).
- **First VBC vs. expansion:** If this is their first VBC contract, emphasize the infrastructure gap. If expanding, emphasize scale challenges — more contracts = more quality definitions = more measurement complexity.
- **Contract-specific language:** MSSP = quality gates + shared savings. ACO REACH = performance benchmarks + financial risk. MA Stars = Star Ratings + bonus revenue. Medicaid MCO = access measures + HEDIS reporting. Use the right terminology for their contract type.
