# Loom Walkthrough Script: FQHC — Closing the Gap

**Duration:** 60-75 seconds
**Recording Setup:** Screen share of HDIM demo with FQHC seed data loaded (1,200 patients, 8 sites, 28K population)
**Merge Fields:** [ORG_NAME], [MEASURE_FOCUS], [POPULATION_SIZE]

---

## Pre-Recording Checklist
- [ ] Demo loaded with FQHC seed profile
- [ ] Dashboard showing Monday morning view (68 gaps closed banner, 2 "At Risk" measures)
- [ ] [ORG_NAME] overlay applied (if customizing for specific target)

---

## Script

### Opening Hook (0:00-0:08)
"If you're running quality at a community health center, you probably started this week staring at a 52% diabetes control rate and wondering how to move it before your next UDS report. Let me show you what that Monday morning looks like with HDIM."

### Dashboard Overview (0:08-0:25)
"So here's the population health dashboard — 28,000 patients across 8 sites. First thing you see: 68 care gaps closed over the weekend without a single planned outreach. Twenty-three of those are HbA1c results from patients who came in for Saturday walk-in visits and got labs drawn opportunistically. Your diabetes rate just ticked from 51.6 to 52.1. Small, but that's real patients getting better care."

### The Key Insight (0:25-0:45)
"Now here's where it gets interesting. You've got 847 patients overdue for HbA1c — but they're not all equal. HDIM ranks them by closability. The top 147 already have appointments in the next 30 days — they just need a lab order added. That's a 30-minute workflow for your care coordinator, not a 3-week outreach campaign. And these 203 down here have transportation barriers flagged via SDOH Z-codes — calling them won't work, but your community health workers can reach them."

### Action View (0:45-0:60)
"And then there's this — the equity stratification view. Your overall HbA1c rate is 52%, but for Spanish-speaking patients it's 41%. For patients with no reliable phone number, 38%. HDIM shows you exactly where to invest your community health worker hours. This is your UDS report writing itself in real-time."

### Closing CTA (0:60-0:75)
"This is a demo with sample data. Want to see it with [ORG_NAME]'s actual measures and patient population? I can set that up in about 15 minutes. Just reply to this email and we'll find a time."

---

## Personalization Notes
- If customizing for a specific target, replace [ORG_NAME] with their org name
- Adjust [POPULATION_SIZE] to match their actual scale (15K-40K range)
- Reference their specific trigger event in the opening hook if applicable (e.g., "Your latest UDS report showed diabetes control at 48% — below the national FQHC average")
- If their weakest measure is depression screening (39%) or cervical cancer screening (44%) instead of diabetes, lead with that measure in the opening hook and key insight
- For PCA targets, reframe as "your member health centers" instead of "your sites"
