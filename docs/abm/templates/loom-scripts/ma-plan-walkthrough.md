## Script

### Opening Hook (0:00-0:08)
"If your Star Rating is on the line and you're making decisions with data that's 60 to 90 days old, you're flying blind during the most expensive season of the year. Let me show you what real-time Stars management looks like."

### Dashboard Overview (0:08-0:25)
"Here's the Stars Projection Dashboard. You're at 3.5, trending toward 3.72. Six measures are highlighted in amber — each one within 2 percentage points of the next cut point. And look at this ticker: 297 gaps closed since Friday. 245 of those are medication adherence gaps that closed automatically from weekend pharmacy refill data. Your PDC-DR just moved from 77.8 to 78.1. You're 1.9 points from the 4-star cut point on that measure."

### The Key Insight (0:25-0:45)
"Now here's the play. You've got 3,200 members with open diabetes medication adherence gaps. But HDIM ranked them by closability. The top tier — members who filled 2 of 3 required fills and have a refill due in the next 30 days. One pharmacy call closes the gap. If your outreach team contacts half and converts 60%, that's 960 closures — enough to push PDC-DR past the cut point. Watch this — I drag PDC-DR to 80%, Stars projection jumps to 3.62. Add PDC-RASA to 81%, now it's 3.71. Add blood pressure control to 70% — you're at 3.84."

### Action View (0:45-0:60)
"Three measures. That's the difference between 3.5 and 3.84 Stars. At your membership size, that's the difference between zero quality bonus and $12 million. And your care managers aren't working 40,000 open gaps — they're working the 3,200 that actually move the rating, prioritized, with pharmacy data and preferred contact method attached."

### Closing CTA (0:60-0:75)
"I can set this up with [ORG_NAME]'s actual membership data in about 15 minutes — show you exactly which measures are closest to the cut point and which members to call first. Just reply and we'll find a time."

## Personalization Notes
- If customizing for a specific target, replace [ORG_NAME] with their plan name
- Adjust [POPULATION_SIZE] to match their actual membership (50K-200K range); bonus dollars scale proportionally
- If their trigger was a Star Rating drop, open with: "You dropped from 4.0 to 3.5. That's $12M in quality bonus — gone. Here's the recovery plan."
- If screening measures (BCS, COL) are weaker than medication adherence, pivot the key insight to those measures
