## Emergency Stop Conditions

**PAUSE ALL CAMPAIGNS immediately if**:

🚨 Spend >$500 in first 24 hours (should be ~$100-150)
🚨 Zero impressions after 4 hours (targeting issue)
🚨 Campaign rejected by LinkedIn for policy violation
🚨 Form submissions not syncing to Zoho CRM
🚨 Leads are 100% unqualified (wrong audience targeted)

**Then**: Diagnose issue, fix, resume cautiously
