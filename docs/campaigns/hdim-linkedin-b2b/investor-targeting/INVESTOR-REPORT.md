## Executive Summary

HDIM is positioned at the intersection of three major investment themes driving healthcare VC in 2025:

1. **Healthcare Interoperability** - $6B market (2025) → $24.8B (2035), 15.2% CAGR
2. **Prior Authorization Automation** - $2.18B (2024) → $11.3B (2033), 18.4% CAGR
3. **AI in Healthcare** - Explosive growth with regulatory tailwinds (CMS 2026 FHIR mandate)

**Key Regulatory Driver**: CMS Interoperability and Prior Authorization Final Rule (CMS-0057-F) requires all payers to implement FHIR APIs by January 1, 2026 - creating massive market pull.

### Investment Terms Recommendation

For angel round, consider:

**SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity)**
- Valuation Cap: $8M - $12M (pre-revenue healthcare IT typical)
- Discount: 20% (standard)
- Pro-rata rights: Yes for $100K+ checks

**Alternative: FAST (Founder/Advisor Stock)**
- For strategic advisors who can open doors
- 0.25% - 1.0% for advisor-level involvement
