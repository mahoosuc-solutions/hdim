## Budget Reallocation Triggers

### Scale UP Budget When:

| Condition | Action | Budget Increase |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| CPL <$150 for 7+ days | Increase daily budget by 20% | +$20-25/day |
| Lead-to-Demo rate >35% | Shift budget from Sponsored Content to Lead Gen | +10% to Lead Gen |
| InMail open rate >60% | Double InMail budget | +$10/day to InMail |
| Retargeting CPL <$100 | Increase retargeting budget | +$15/day to retargeting |

### Scale DOWN Budget When:

| Condition | Action | Budget Decrease |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| CPL >$300 for 5+ days | Reduce daily budget by 30% | -$30/day |
| CTR <0.40% for 7+ days | Pause ad set, reallocate | -50% from that ad set |
| Lead quality <60% | Tighten targeting, reduce budget | -20% until quality improves |
| Frequency >4 | Pause creative, rotate new | Pause until frequency <3 |

## Emergency Budget Preservation

**If early performance is poor** (CPL >$350 after Week 2):

### Contingency Plan:
1. **Pause all campaigns** immediately
2. **Diagnostic analysis**:
   - Review ad creative quality
   - Check targeting (too broad?)
   - Analyze landing page (is form too long?)
   - Check sales follow-up (are leads going cold?)
3. **Strategic pivot**:
   - Reduce budget to $50/day (preservation mode)
   - Test 1 ad variant at a time
   - Focus on retargeting only (cheapest CPL)
4. **Preserve remaining budget** for relaunch once issues fixed

**Budget Burn Rate Protection**: Never spend >40% of total budget in Month 1 without achieving target CPL.
