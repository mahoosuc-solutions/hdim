## FAQ

### Is HDIM open source?

HDIM is source-available under Business Source License 1.1.  
BSL 1.1 is not an OSI-approved open-source license.

### When do I need a commercial license?

You need a commercial license for production use that is not covered by the
Additional Use Grant in the repository LICENSE, including hosted or managed
service scenarios.

### Can we evaluate before buying?

Yes. Non-production evaluation and development use are available under BSL 1.1.

### Do you offer annual and multi-year terms?

Yes. Contract structure can support annual and multi-year agreements.

### Do you sign BAAs?

BAA availability depends on deployment model, scope, and responsibilities.  
Discuss this during commercial review.

### Is pricing public?

Not initially. Early pricing is quote-based while packages are finalized with
launch customers.

### How are packages and pricing decided?

We align package and pricing to your rights model, deployment responsibility,
support needs, and primary usage grouping.

### Can we change package later?

Yes. Most teams start with a pilot-oriented package and expand as scope grows.
