# HDIM AI Pricing + Local Impact Policy (BSL Launch)

## Why This Exists
HDIM was built with AI-assisted methodology and is priced with AI-assisted economics.
Our commercial model is designed to scale delivery while hard-wiring community benefit into every contract.

This policy defines the launch model for:
- AI-based pricing structure
- Tiered social allocation
- Local economy routing
- Governance and reporting commitments

## AI-Priced, Humanity-First Model

### Year 1 Commercial Formula
1. `Implementation Fee = Effective Billable Points x Point Rate`
2. `Annual Subscription = Platform Base + Integration Tier + Intelligence Tier`
3. `Local Impact Allocation = (Implementation Fee + Annual Subscription) x Social Allocation %`
4. `Year 1 Total = Implementation Fee + Annual Subscription + Local Impact Allocation`

### Social Allocation Tiers (By Customer Size)
| Customer Size | Allocation % |
|---------------|--------------|
| Growth / SMB | 1% |
| Mid-market | 2% |
| Enterprise | 3% |

### What "Local" Means
- Primary: customer MSA/county
- Fallback: state-level pool when MSA/county execution is unavailable

## Buyer-Type Forecast Segmentation
Launch forecasts are maintained by buyer type:
- Payer
- Provider / Health System
- ACO / FQHC
- Vendor / Partner

Within each buyer type, deals are modeled by:
- Size tier (Growth, Mid-market, Enterprise)
- Deployment model (cloud, hybrid, on-prem)
- AI leverage factor (ALF) and story-point profile

## Local Impact Fund Operating Standard
The Local Impact Allocation is administered through a dedicated restricted fund.

### Fund Flow
1. Allocation amount is tagged at quote and contract execution.
2. Allocation is booked against the customer's local routing target.
3. Disbursement cycles run quarterly.
4. Reporting package includes amount allocated, amount disbursed, and local initiative summary.

### Accountability Roles
- Sales Operations: quote tagging and tier validation
- Finance: restricted-fund accounting and reconciliation
- Impact Operations: local partner disbursement records
- Compliance: audit trail retention and evidence readiness

## Contract Language Template (Commercial Clause)
Use this language in order forms, SOWs, or commercial appendices:

> Local Impact Allocation. A Local Impact Allocation is included in the commercial pricing for this engagement. The allocation amount is calculated as a percentage of Year 1 commercial value based on customer size tier. Funds are designated for local economic reinvestment in the customer's MSA/county where feasible, with state-level fallback where direct local routing is unavailable.

## Discount and Approval Guardrails
- Local Impact Allocation is mandatory and is not an optional add-on.
- Standard discounting cannot remove the allocation line.
- Any exception requires executive approval and written rationale in the deal record.

## Evidence and Reporting Cadence
At minimum, each deal record must include:
- Buyer type
- Customer size tier
- Social allocation percentage and amount
- Local routing target (MSA/county or state fallback)
- Quarterly disbursement evidence reference

Quarterly reporting outputs should support both:
- Customer-facing trust reporting
- Internal audit and board-level review
