3. **Real-Time**: Continuous measure calculation, not batch processing

## Objection Handling

### "We already use [Competitor]"

> "That's great—[Competitor] is solid for [their strength]. We've found organizations use HDIM alongside existing tools when they need [specific capability we have that they don't]. Would you be open to seeing how they complement each other?"

### "We don't have budget for new tools"

> "I understand. Many of our customers started with a pilot focused on one high-value measure—like reducing HbA1c gaps or improving Star Ratings on one metric. The ROI from closing those gaps often funds broader implementation. Would a focused pilot make sense?"

### "We're too small / too busy"

> "Actually, smaller organizations are exactly who we built this for. The national plans have 50-person analytics teams. We help organizations like yours get similar capabilities without that investment. Could we do a quick 15-minute overview to see if it's worth a deeper look?"

### "We need to see the data security approach"

> "Absolutely—we're HIPAA-compliant with 5-minute PHI cache TTLs, encrypted at rest and in transit, and full audit logging. We're also in the process of SOC2 Type I certification. I can share our security documentation ahead of any demo."
