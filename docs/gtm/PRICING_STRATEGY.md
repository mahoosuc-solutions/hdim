# HDIM Pricing Strategy

## Executive Summary

HDIM's pricing strategy positions us as the **value leader** in healthcare data platforms - offering enterprise-grade quality automation at a fraction of competitor costs. Our transparent, tiered pricing removes the complexity and sticker shock common in healthcare IT procurement.

---

## Pricing Philosophy

### Core Principles
1. **Transparency** - Published pricing, no hidden fees
2. **Value-Based** - Pricing aligned with customer outcomes
3. **Scalability** - Grows with customer needs
4. **Accessibility** - Entry points for all organization sizes

## Pricing Objections

### "That seems too cheap - what's the catch?"
**Response:** "No catch. We've built HDIM on modern cloud-native architecture that's dramatically more efficient than legacy platforms. Lower infrastructure costs = lower pricing. Plus, we don't have enterprise sales teams and marketing budgets to fund."

### "We need enterprise support"
**Response:** "Enterprise tier includes dedicated support with 4-hour response SLA, dedicated success manager, and security audit support. Custom SLAs available."

### "Can you match competitor pricing?"
**Response:** "We're already 60-80% below competitors. If budget is the concern, we can discuss longer-term commitments for additional discounts, or start with a smaller scope and expand."

### "We need per-user pricing like Salesforce"
**Response:** "Per-user pricing scales poorly - a 50-user deployment at $325/user costs $195K/year. HDIM's flat pricing gives you unlimited users at the platform level. Better economics as you grow."

## Sales Guidelines

### Qualification Questions for Pricing
1. "What's your approximate patient/member population?"
2. "How many users need access to quality dashboards?"
3. "Do you have on-premise requirements?"
4. "What's your budget range for quality improvement technology?"
5. "Are you considering multi-year commitments?"

### Discounting Authority

| Role | Maximum Discount |
|------|------------------|
| Sales Rep | 10% |
| Sales Manager | 20% |
| VP Sales | 30% |
| CEO | Unlimited |

### Non-Negotiables
- No free pilots beyond 30 days
- No custom pricing below Tier 1
- Implementation services required for Tier 3+
- Annual billing for all tiers (monthly available at +10%)

---

## Pricing Updates

- Pricing reviewed quarterly
- Existing customers grandfathered for 12 months after changes
- New features may be added to higher tiers only
- Volume thresholds may be adjusted based on market conditions
