# Target SMB Healthcare Organizations for HDIM Outreach

*Generated: December 29, 2025*

## Executive Summary

This document identifies 10 target SMB healthcare organizations for HDIM initial outreach based on market research on value-based care adoption, quality measure challenges, and technology needs in 2024-2025.

**Target Profile:**
- Organizations struggling with quality measure reporting (HEDIS, CQM, UDS)
- Limited technology budgets forcing reliance on manual processes
- Participation in value-based care programs (MSSP, ACO REACH, Medicare Advantage)
- Serving underserved populations with complex care needs
- Annual revenue: $10M-$500M

## Category 1: Small ACOs in Medicare Shared Savings Program

### 1. Greater Regional Health ACO (Creston, Iowa)
**Type:** Critical Access Hospital-based ACO
**Size:** ~25-bed hospital, 5 rural clinic locations
**Why Target:**
- Documented MSSP success ($870K shared savings 2019-2022) but limited technology infrastructure
- Relies heavily on care coordinators for manual gap closure
- Rural population with social determinant challenges (food insecurity, transportation)
- Critical access hospitals often lack enterprise quality measurement tools

**Pain Points:** Manual wellness visit tracking, chronic care management coordination, SDOH documentation

**HDIM Value:** Automated care gap identification, CQL-based measure evaluation, FHIR interoperability for EHR data extraction

**Sources:** [HFMA ACO Best Practices](https://www.hfma.org/payment-reimbursement-and-managed-care/accountable-care-organizations/recent-updates-and-emerging-best-practices-for-acos-in-the-medicare-shared-savings-program/)

### 6. Regional Not-for-Profit MA Plan (Generic Profile)
**Type:** Regional Medicare Advantage plan
**Size:** 50,000-200,000 members
**Why Target:**

- Star Rating drops from 4.0 to 3.5 = ~5% revenue loss ($millions)
- 2025 cut point increases requiring higher performance
- Limited analytics infrastructure vs. national competitors

- Regional Blues plans in smaller markets
- Provider-sponsored health plans at regional health systems

**Pain Points:** Star Rating improvement, HEDIS measure gaps, competing with better-funded national plans

**HDIM Value:** Real-time HEDIS gap identification, predictive analytics for at-risk members, automated measure calculation

## Key Messages by Segment

### For State PCAs/HCCNs:
> "HDIM provides the same enterprise-grade CQL measure evaluation used by large health systems, now accessible for community health centers. Automate UDS+ reporting, identify care gaps in real-time, and demonstrate quality improvement to HRSA and payers."

### For Small ACOs:
> "The 2025 APP Plus quality measures add 6 new metrics to track. HDIM automates CQL-based measure calculation across your EHR data, so your care coordinators can focus on closing gaps instead of generating reports."

### For Regional MA Plans:
> "National MA plans have analytics teams of 50+. HDIM gives you equivalent HEDIS automation and Star Rating optimization at a fraction of the cost, helping you compete for the 4+ star bonus payments."

### For IPAs/CINs:
> "Aggregating quality data across independent practices with different EHRs is painful. HDIM's FHIR-based integration normalizes data and calculates measures consistently, giving you network-wide visibility for value-based contracts."
