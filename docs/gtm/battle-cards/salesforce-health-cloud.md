| **Implementation** | 90 days | 6-12 months |

## Objection Handling

### "We're already invested in Salesforce ecosystem"
**Response:** "HDIM integrates with Salesforce via APIs. Many customers use Salesforce for patient engagement and CRM while HDIM handles clinical quality - they complement each other."

### "Salesforce has Einstein AI"
**Response:** "Einstein is great for sales/service AI. For clinical quality, HDIM offers purpose-built risk stratification, predictive analytics, and real-time measure monitoring designed for HEDIS and Star ratings."

### "Salesforce is trusted by 600+ healthcare organizations"
**Response:** "For CRM, absolutely. But how many use it for HEDIS automation? Ask for references specifically using Health Cloud for quality measure management."
