# Case Study: Integrated Behavioral Health Partners

## Closing the Mental Health Quality Gap

---

## Executive Summary

**Organization:** Integrated Behavioral Health Partners (IBHP)
**Size:** 28 primary care practices, 6 behavioral health centers, 145,000 patients
**Location:** Pacific Northwest
**Focus:** Primary care behavioral health integration

**Results After 12 Months:**
- **PHQ-9 screening rate** increased from 31% to 89%

## The Challenge

### The Mental Health Quality Crisis

IBHP was missing critical quality opportunities in mental health:

**Screening Gaps:**
- Only 31% of eligible patients screened for depression (PHQ-9)
- Anxiety screening (GAD-7) at 18%
- Paper-based screenings not captured in EHR
- No systematic follow-up for positive screens

**Care Coordination Failures:**
- 60% of patients with positive screens lost to follow-up
- Behavioral health referrals taking 6+ weeks
- No visibility into treatment outcomes
- Primary care and behavioral health operating in silos

**Financial Impact:**
- Missing HEDIS AHQ (Antidepressant Medication Management) targets
- Not capturing behavioral health quality bonuses
- High ED utilization for behavioral health crises
- Readmission penalties from psychiatric co-morbidities

**The Statistic That Changed Everything:**
> "50% of patients with depression are undiagnosed, and 50% of those diagnosed don't receive adequate treatment. We were contributing to that problem."
> — *Dr. Amanda Reyes, IBHP Chief Medical Officer*

## Screening Workflow

### Before HDIM
```
Patient → Paper PHQ-9 → Manual scoring → Maybe entered in EHR → Often no follow-up
         (if remembered)
```

### After HDIM
```
Patient → Tablet PHQ-9 → Automatic scoring → EHR documentation →
  ↓
  Score 0-4: Documented, no action needed
  Score 5-9: Care coordinator follow-up scheduled
  Score 10+: Same-day provider notification, urgent referral triggered
  Score 15+: Crisis protocol activated, same-day intervention
```

## Lessons Learned

1. **Start with PHQ-9** - It's the most impactful measure with clearest ROI
2. **Tablet > Paper** - Digital screening completion rates are 3x higher
3. **Automate Follow-Up** - Don't rely on humans to remember
4. **Track Outcomes** - Screening is meaningless without treatment response
5. **Integrate BH Staff** - Co-located behavioral health accelerates care
