# Accelerator & Incubator Applications

**Created:** February 2, 2026
**Purpose:** Track accelerator applications as parallel fundraising path
**Strategy:** Apply to 5-8 programs to maximize odds

---

## Why Accelerators?

1. **Credibility signal** - YC/Techstars badge opens doors
2. **Structured fundraising** - Demo Day creates urgency
3. **Network access** - Alumni, mentors, investors
4. **Standard terms** - Less negotiation, faster close
5. **Backup plan** - If direct raise is slow

## Application Materials Checklist

### Required for Most Programs

- [ ] Company one-liner (< 10 words)
- [ ] Problem statement (1 paragraph)
- [ ] Solution description (1 paragraph)
- [ ] Market size (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- [ ] Traction metrics (pilots, revenue, users)
- [ ] Team bios and LinkedIn profiles
- [ ] 1-minute video (YC requires this)
- [ ] Demo video or live demo access
- [ ] Pitch deck (10-15 slides)

### HDIM-Specific Talking Points

**One-liner:**
> Real-time HEDIS quality measurement for health systems

**Problem (for applications):**
> Health systems make quality decisions with 6-month-old data. By the time they identify care gaps, the measurement year is closing. This costs them millions in missed quality bonuses and avoidable hospitalizations.

**Solution (for applications):**
> HDIM calculates HEDIS measures in real-time (<2 seconds) using FHIR R4 event streams. Health systems see care gaps the day they happen - not 6 months later.

**Traction (update as you progress):**
> - [X] pilots active with [Y] hospitals
> - [Z] discovery calls completed
> - Platform processes [N] patients in demo environment

**Market Size:**
> - TAM: $15B (healthcare quality analytics)
> - SAM: $3B (real-time quality measurement)
> - SOM: $300M (mid-size health systems, year 1 addressable)
