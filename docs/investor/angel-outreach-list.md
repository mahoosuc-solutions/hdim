**Strategy:** Advice-first approach (not pitching yet)

---

## Why Angels First?

1. **Faster decisions** - 2-4 weeks vs. 8-12 for VCs
2. **Smaller checks, less dilution** - $25K-100K typical
3. **Warm intro source** - Angels know VCs
4. **Domain expertise** - Healthcare operators give better advice
5. **Social proof** - Angel commits make VC raises easier

## Notes

- **Don't pitch** - ask for advice
- **Be specific** - vague asks get ignored
- **Reference their work** - shows you did homework
- **Make it easy** - offer to meet their schedule/location
- **Follow up** - busy people miss emails
