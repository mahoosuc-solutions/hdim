# HDIM Customer Target List

**Created:** February 2, 2026
**Purpose:** Week 1 outreach for pilot customers
**Target:** 50 mid-size health systems and ACOs

---

## Target Profile

**Ideal Customer:**
- Mid-size health systems (5-20 hospitals)
- Active value-based care contracts (MSSP, ACO REACH, commercial)
- HEDIS/quality measure reporting requirements
- FHIR R4 capable EHR (Epic, Cerner, Meditech)
- Decision-making agility (not 12-month procurement cycles)

**Why Mid-Size:**
- Large enough to validate market ($50K-100K ACV)
- Small enough to move fast (30-60 day sales cycle)
- Often underserved by enterprise vendors

## Research Notes by Organization

### Geisinger
- **Why target:** Pioneer of ProvenHealth Navigator model, published extensively on quality
- **Recent news:** Expanding ACO partnerships
- **Pain point likely:** Scaling quality measurement across growing network
- **Approach:** Reference their published work, offer to validate approach

### Intermountain Health
- **Why target:** Created the healthcare quality improvement methodology many use
- **Recent news:** Merger with SCL Health, expanding to 33 hospitals
- **Pain point likely:** Integrating quality measurement across merged systems
- **Approach:** Acknowledge their quality leadership, position as amplifier

### Aledade
- **Why target:** Fast-growing ACO enabler, tech-forward culture
- **Recent news:** $300M Series E, expanding rapidly
- **Pain point likely:** Scaling quality measurement across hundreds of practices
- **Approach:** Emphasize real-time capabilities for distributed network

### Privia Health
- **Why target:** Physician enablement platform, national scale
- **Recent news:** Strong growth in Medicare Advantage
- **Pain point likely:** Consistent quality measurement across diverse practices
- **Approach:** Position as infrastructure for quality at scale

## Disqualification Criteria

Remove from list if:
- [ ] No value-based care contracts (pure fee-for-service)
- [ ] EHR not FHIR R4 capable
- [ ] Recently signed competing vendor (Arcadia, Innovaccer, etc.)
- [ ] Known 12+ month procurement process
- [ ] No quality team or dedicated quality leadership
