## Conference Strategy

### Before the Event
- [ ] Register early (better pricing, meeting scheduler access)
- [ ] Review attendee list (if available)
- [ ] Pre-book 10-15 meetings via event app or LinkedIn
- [ ] Prepare 2-minute pitch for hallway conversations
- [ ] Bring demo on laptop (no dependencies)

### At the Event
- [ ] Attend sessions where target personas speak
- [ ] Work the hallways and networking events
- [ ] Collect business cards / LinkedIn connections
- [ ] Take notes on conversations immediately
- [ ] Send same-day follow-up emails

### After the Event
- [ ] Send follow-up within 48 hours
- [ ] Connect on LinkedIn with personalized note
- [ ] Add to CRM with conversation notes
- [ ] Schedule discovery calls with warm leads
