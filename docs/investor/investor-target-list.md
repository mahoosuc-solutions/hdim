## Outreach Sequence

### Phase 1: Warm-Up (Weeks 1-4)
- Research partners, find intro paths
- Engage on social media (like, comment, share)
- Request advice meetings (not pitches)
- Add to monthly update list

### Phase 2: Active Outreach (Weeks 5-8)
- Send 15-20 emails in week 5
- Use warm intros where available
- Cold email tier 2 and 3

### Phase 3: Close (Weeks 9-12)
- Focus on engaged investors
- Partner meetings
- Diligence and term sheet

### Status Definitions
- **Research:** Identifying intro path
- **Intro Requested:** Asked connection for intro
- **Intro Sent:** Connection made intro
- **Meeting Scheduled:** First meeting on calendar
- **First Meeting:** Had first meeting
- **Partner Meeting:** Meeting with full partnership
- **Diligence:** In due diligence
- **Term Sheet:** Received or negotiating term sheet
- **Passed:** Declined to invest

## Disqualification Criteria

Remove from active list if:
- [ ] Portfolio conflict (invested in direct competitor)
- [ ] Check size mismatch (only does >$10M or <$1M)
- [ ] Stage mismatch (only Series B+ or only pre-seed)
- [ ] Geographic mismatch (only invests in specific region we're not in)
- [ ] Clear "no" after meeting
