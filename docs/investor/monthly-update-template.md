# Template Instructions (Delete Before Sending)

## When to Send
- First week of each month
- Same day each month (builds trust)
- Even when news is bad (especially when news is bad)

## Who Gets It
- All investors (angels, VCs)
- Advisors
- Warm contacts who've taken meetings
- People who said "keep me posted"

## Tone Guidelines
- Honest but optimistic
- Specific over vague
- Numbers over adjectives
- Short over long

## What NOT to Include
- Vanity metrics that don't matter
- Excuses without plans
- Asks without specificity
- Walls of text

## Subject Line Options
- "HDIM Update - [Month Year]"
- "[Company] Monthly Update - [Month]"
- "February Update: [One Key Highlight]"

## Pro Tips
1. Write the TL;DR first - it forces clarity
2. Limit to 1 page when printed
3. Use bullet points liberally
4. Include at least one "ask" - people want to help
5. Send via BCC to protect privacy
6. Track opens if you can (Superhuman, Mailtrack)
