# HDIM Outreach Templates

**Quick Reference:** Copy-paste templates for customer and investor outreach.

---

## Customer Outreach

### LinkedIn Connection Request (300 characters max)

```
Hi [Name] - I'm working on real-time HEDIS gap detection for health systems.
Saw [hospital] is focused on value-based care. Would love to hear how you're
handling quality measure reporting today. Open to connecting?
```

**Personalization tips:**
- Replace `[hospital]` with their actual organization name
- Check their profile for recent posts about quality, value-based care, HEDIS
- If they posted something relevant, reference it: "Saw your post about [topic]"

---

### Cold Email to Prospects

**Subject:** Real-time HEDIS gaps at [Hospital Name]?

```
Hi [Name],

Quick question: How long does it take [Hospital] to identify HEDIS care gaps
after a patient visit?

I ask because most health systems I've talked to are working with data that's
3-12 months stale. By the time they find gaps, the measurement year is closing.

I built a platform that calculates gaps in real-time (<2 seconds after
a FHIR event). Two health systems are piloting it now.

Worth a 15-minute call to see if this matches a pain point for you?

[Your name]
[One-line credential]
```

**Subject line alternatives:**
- "Question about HEDIS reporting at [Hospital]"
- "Real-time care gaps - relevant to [Hospital]?"
- "[Mutual connection] suggested I reach out"

---

### Discovery Call Script

**Opening (2 min)**
> "Thanks for taking the time. I'm trying to understand how health systems
> handle quality measurement - not pitching today, just learning. Mind if
> I ask a few questions?"

**Discovery Questions (10 min)**
1. "How do you currently track HEDIS measures throughout the year?"
2. "What's the typical lag between a care event and seeing it in dashboards?"
3. "When you find gaps, how do you close the loop with care teams?"
4. "What does quality reporting cost annually - vendors, FTEs, manual work?"
5. "If you could change one thing about quality measurement, what would it be?"

**Transition (2 min)**
> "That's helpful. Based on what you're describing, what we've built might
> be relevant. Would you be open to a 15-minute demo next week?"

**If Yes:**
> "Great. We're looking for 2-3 design partners for a 30-day free pilot.
> If the demo resonates, would that be something [hospital] could consider?"

## Quick Tips

1. **Personalize everything** - Generic templates get ignored
2. **Keep it short** - Busy people don't read walls of text
3. **Lead with their pain** - Not your features
4. **Follow up 3x** - Most responses come after the 2nd or 3rd touch
5. **Track everything** - Know who you've contacted and when
