## Search Tips

### Finding the Right Person

1. **LinkedIn search:** "[title] [organization]"
2. **Check company page:** LinkedIn > Company > People > Filter by title
3. **Google backup:** "[name] [organization] LinkedIn"
4. **Conference speakers:** HIMSS, IHI, AMGA speaker lists

### Verification

Before connecting, verify:
- [ ] Current role (not former)
- [ ] Correct organization (not subsidiary)
- [ ] Quality/pop health focus (not unrelated role)

### Common Title Variations

| Standard Title | Variations to Search |
|---------------|---------------------|
| Chief Quality Officer | CQO, VP Quality, SVP Quality |
| VP Population Health | VP Pop Health, VP Value-Based Care |
| CMIO | Chief Medical Information Officer, VP Clinical Informatics |
| Director Quality | Quality Director, Director Clinical Quality |
