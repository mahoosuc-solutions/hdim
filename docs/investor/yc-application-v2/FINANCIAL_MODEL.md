## 1. Revenue Model

### 1.1 Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Monthly Price | Annual Price | Target Customer | Patient Limit |
|------|---------------|--------------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Starter** | $80 | $800 | Small clinics, solo practices | <1,000 |
| **Pro** | $500 | $5,000 | FQHCs, small ACOs | <10,000 |
| **Enterprise** | $2,000 | $20,000 | Mid-size ACOs, IPAs | <50,000 |
| **Custom** | $5,000+ | $50,000+ | Large ACOs, health systems | Unlimited |

### 1.2 Average Revenue Per Account (ARPA)

| Year | Starter Mix | Pro Mix | Enterprise Mix | Custom Mix | Blended ARPA |
|------|-------------|---------|----------------|------------|--------------|
| 2025 | 60% | 30% | 10% | 0% | $500/mo |
| 2026 | 50% | 35% | 12% | 3% | $625/mo |
| 2027 | 40% | 40% | 15% | 5% | $625/mo |
| 2028 | 35% | 40% | 18% | 7% | $667/mo |
| 2029 | 30% | 40% | 20% | 10% | $694/mo |

**ARPA Growth Drivers:**
- Upsell from Starter → Pro as clinics grow
- Enterprise adoption increases over time
- Custom tier for large health systems (2026+)
- Annual price increases (3-5% per year)

### 2.1 Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

| Component | 2025 | 2026 | 2027 | 2028 | 2029 |
|-----------|------|------|------|------|------|
| Sales Salaries | $60K | $150K | $360K | $720K | $1.2M |

| Events/Travel | $5K | $20K | $50K | $100K | $150K |

| New Customers | 55 | 175 | 450 | 1,000 | 1,700 |
| **CAC** | **$1,455** | **$1,486** | **$1,422** | **$1,270** | **$1,191** |

**CAC Trend:** Decreases over time due to:
- Brand awareness reducing marketing needs
- Inbound leads from content marketing
- Referrals from existing customers
- Channel partnerships reducing direct sales cost

### 2.2 Lifetime Value (LTV)

| Component | Calculation | Value |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Average ARPA | Year 3 average | $625/month |
| Gross Margin | | 85% |
| Monthly Contribution | $625 × 85% | $531/month |
| Average Lifespan | 1 / 8% churn | 12.5 years |
| **LTV** | $531 × 12 × 12.5 | **$79,650** |

**Conservative LTV (5-year horizon):**
| Component | Calculation | Value |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Monthly Contribution | | $531/month |
| 5-Year Revenue | $531 × 60 months | $31,860 |
| Discount Rate | 10% | 0.62 factor |
| **5-Year LTV** | $31,860 × 0.62 | **$19,753** |

### 2.3 LTV:CAC Ratio

| Year | LTV | CAC | LTV:CAC | Payback (months) |
|------|-----|-----|---------|------------------|
| 2025 | $19,753 | $1,455 | 13.6x | 2.7 |
| 2026 | $19,753 | $1,486 | 13.3x | 2.8 |
| 2027 | $19,753 | $1,422 | 13.9x | 2.7 |
| 2028 | $19,753 | $1,270 | 15.6x | 2.4 |
| 2029 | $19,753 | $1,191 | 16.6x | 2.2 |

**Benchmarks:**
- LTV:CAC > 3x is healthy for SaaS
- HDIM maintains 13-17x throughout projections
- Payback < 12 months is excellent
- HDIM payback is < 3 months

### 2.4 Churn Analysis

| Type | 2025 | 2026 | 2027 | 2028 | 2029 |
|------|------|------|------|------|------|

| **Revenue Churn** | 8% | 10% | 6% | 5% | 5% |

**Churn Mitigation Strategies:**
- 2025: Higher churn expected (new product, finding PMF)
