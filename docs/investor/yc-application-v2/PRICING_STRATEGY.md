# HDIM Pricing Strategy

**Value-Based Pricing Aligned to Customer Segments**

*Version 1.0 | December 2025*

---

## Executive Summary

HDIM pricing is designed around three principles:
1. **Affordable for underserved markets** — 625x cheaper than enterprise alternatives
2. **Value-aligned** — Price tied to organization size and value received
3. **Sustainable margins** — 85%+ gross margin at all tiers

### 1.2 Willingness to Pay Analysis

**What drives WTP in healthcare quality:**

| Value Driver | Impact | How HDIM Delivers |
|--------------|--------|-------------------|
| **Quality bonuses** | $50-500K/year | Improve scores → more bonus |
| **Penalty avoidance** | $100K-1M/year | Avoid CMS penalties |
| **Staff time savings** | 10-40 hrs/week | Automate manual tracking |
| **Revenue from gaps closed** | $200-2,000/gap | Real-time = more gaps closed |
| **Reduced chart chase** | $25-50/chart | Automated data aggregation |

**Rule of thumb:** Customers will pay 5-10% of the value they receive.

## 5. Pricing Psychology

### 5.1 Anchoring Strategy

**Lead with the problem cost, not the solution cost:**

> "Small ACOs spend $50,000-100,000/year on quality reporting staff—just to manage spreadsheets. HDIM automates 80% of that work for $3,588/year."

> "Missing quality bonuses costs the average FQHC $75,000/year. HDIM pays for itself if you close just 24 additional care gaps."

### 5.2 Price Point Psychology

| Tier | Price | Psychology |
|------|-------|------------|
| Community $49 | Under $50 | "Less than a coffee per day" |
| Professional $299 | Under $300 | "Less than one staff hour per day" |
| Enterprise $999 | Under $1,000 | "Less than one FTE for the whole org" |

### 5.3 Annual vs Monthly

**Encourage annual billing:**
- 15% discount for annual prepay
- Reduces churn (commitment)
- Improves cash flow
- Simplifies budgeting for customers
