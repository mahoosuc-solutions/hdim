## Team Culture

### Our Values

**1. Patients First**
Every decision filters through: "Does this help patients get better care?" We're not building software for software's sake—we're improving healthcare outcomes.

**2. Radically Simple**
Healthcare is already complex. We fight complexity with simplicity. If a customer needs a 50-page implementation guide, we've failed.

**3. Fast and Right**
We move quickly without sacrificing quality. Speed without accuracy is worthless in healthcare. We do both.

**4. Transparent by Default**
We share context, decisions, and challenges openly. Customers know our roadmap. Team knows our metrics. Trust is earned through transparency.

**5. Always Learning**
Healthcare and technology evolve constantly. We stay curious, embrace feedback, and improve continuously.
