## Subject Line Testing

### Best Performing Patterns

| Pattern | Example | Open Rate |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Question | "What's your MIPS score?" | 35-40% |
| Number + Benefit | "40% higher quality scores" | 30-35% |
| Pain Point | "Still using spreadsheets?" | 32-38% |
| Social Proof | "How [Company] achieved..." | 28-32% |
| Curiosity Gap | "The quality metric you're missing" | 30-35% |

### A/B Testing Protocol

1. Test 2-3 subject lines per email
2. Send to 20% of list, split evenly
3. Wait 4 hours for results
4. Send winner to remaining 80%

### Subject Lines to Avoid

- ALL CAPS
- Excessive punctuation!!!
- Spammy words (Free, Guaranteed, Act Now)
- Misleading subject lines
- Over 60 characters
