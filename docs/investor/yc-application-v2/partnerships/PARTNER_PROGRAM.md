# HDIM Partner Program

> Partner tier definitions, revenue share models, co-marketing opportunities, and onboarding processes.

---

## Executive Summary

**Vision:** Build an ecosystem of partners who extend HDIM's reach and capabilities, creating mutual value for partners, HDIM, and healthcare organizations.

**Program Goals:**
- Expand market reach through channel partnerships
- Enhance product value through technology integrations
- Reduce customer acquisition costs
- Accelerate time-to-value for customers

**Target Partner Mix (Year 1):**
- Referral Partners: 30-50
- Reseller Partners: 5-10
- Technology Partners: 10-20

## Partner Tiers

### Tier 1: Referral Partners

**Description:** Organizations that refer qualified leads to HDIM in exchange for commission.

**Ideal Partner Profile:**
- Healthcare consultants and advisors
- Practice management consultants
- Revenue cycle management companies
- Healthcare IT consultants
- Industry associations
- Healthcare attorneys and compliance experts

**Requirements:**
- Signed referral partner agreement
- Complete partner onboarding training (1 hour)
- Minimum 2 qualified referrals per quarter to remain active

**Benefits:**

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Referral commission | 15% of first year contract value |
| Partner portal access | Track referrals, commissions, resources |
| Co-branded materials | One-pagers, email templates |
| Partner newsletter | Monthly updates, product news |
| Sales support | Access to HDIM sales team for joint calls |

**Commission Structure:**

| Tier | Referrals/Year | Commission Rate | Bonus |
|------|----------------|-----------------|-------|
| Bronze | 1-5 | 15% | - |
| Silver | 6-15 | 18% | $500 per 5 closed |
| Gold | 16+ | 20% | $1,000 per 5 closed |

**Referral Qualification Criteria:**
- Healthcare organization (practice, FQHC, ACO, health system)
- Decision-maker contact provided
- Active interest in quality measurement
- Not currently in HDIM pipeline

**Commission Payment:**
- Paid 30 days after customer first invoice
- Paid via ACH or check
- 1099 issued annually for US partners

---

### Tier 2: Reseller Partners

**Description:** Organizations that sell HDIM directly to customers, handling sales and potentially implementation.

**Ideal Partner Profile:**
- Healthcare IT value-added resellers (VARs)
- EHR implementation partners
- Healthcare consulting firms with technology practice
- Regional healthcare IT companies
- Practice management companies

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 2 years healthcare IT experience
- Demonstrated sales capability (3+ deals/year minimum)
- Completion of HDIM certification program (8 hours)
- Dedicated account manager assigned to HDIM
- Quarterly business reviews

**Benefits:**

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Reseller margin | 25-35% discount on list price |
| Lead sharing | Access to HDIM-generated leads in territory |
| Partner portal | Full access including deal registration |
| Co-marketing fund | 3% of revenue for marketing activities |
| Technical support | Priority access to support team |
| Training | Unlimited seats in certification program |
| NFR licenses | 2 internal use licenses for demos |
| Quarterly incentives | Bonuses for exceeding targets |

**Margin Structure:**

| Tier | Annual Revenue | Margin | MDF |
|------|----------------|--------|-----|
| Authorized | $0-$50K | 25% | 2% |
| Premier | $50K-$150K | 30% | 3% |
| Elite | $150K+ | 35% | 4% |

**Territory Options:**
- Geographic (state, region)
- Segment (FQHC, solo practice, etc.)
- Named account (specific prospects)
- Non-exclusive (standard)
- Exclusive (requires commitment)

**Deal Registration:**
- Registered deals receive 90-day protection
- Protected deals cannot be direct-sold by HDIM
- Registration requires prospect name, contact, timeline
- Approval within 24 hours

**Description:** Software vendors that integrate with HDIM to provide enhanced solutions.

**Ideal Partner Profile:**
- EHR vendors (Epic, Cerner, athena, NextGen, etc.)

- Analytics and BI tools

**Requirements:**
- Technical integration completed and certified
- Mutual customer or prospect interest
- Commitment to ongoing integration maintenance

**Benefits:**

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|

| Sandbox environment | Dedicated test environment |
| Technical support | Direct access to integration team |
| Joint marketing | Co-branded materials, joint webinars |
| Listing in marketplace | Featured in HDIM partner directory |
| Lead sharing | Referrals from HDIM customers |
| Revenue share | For customer referrals (10-15%) |

**Integration Tiers:**

| Tier | Requirements | Benefits |
|------|--------------|----------|
| **Compatible** | Tested data exchange | Directory listing |

**Certification Process:**
1. Submit integration proposal
2. Receive API access and sandbox
3. Complete development and testing
4. HDIM validation and certification
5. Go-to-market planning
6. Public launch

## Revenue Share Models

### Referral Partner Revenue Share

```
Example: Referral Partner → Small Practice Customer

Customer Contract: $299/month × 12 months = $3,588
Partner Commission: $3,588 × 15% = $538.20

Paid to partner after customer's first payment.
```

### Reseller Partner Revenue Share

```
Example: Premier Reseller → FQHC Customer

List Price: $999/month × 12 months = $11,988
Reseller Cost: $11,988 × 70% = $8,392
Reseller Margin: $11,988 - $8,392 = $3,596 (30%)

Reseller invoices customer directly.
HDIM invoices reseller at discounted rate.
```

### Technology Partner Revenue Share

```
Example: EHR Vendor Refers Customer

Customer Contract: $999/month × 12 months = $11,988
Technology Partner Commission: $11,988 × 10% = $1,199

Paid annually based on referred customer revenue.
```

### Marketing Development Fund (MDF)

**Eligible Activities:**
- Co-branded advertising
- Event sponsorships
- Content development
- Lead generation campaigns
- Product demonstrations
- Customer events

**MDF Rules:**
- Available to Reseller and Premier Technology Partners
- Accrued as percentage of revenue (2-4%)
- Requires pre-approval for expenditure
- 50% match (partner contributes 50%)
- Use within 12 months of accrual
- Reporting required within 30 days of activity

**Example MDF Calculation:**

```
Premier Reseller generates $100K in annual revenue
MDF accrual: $100K × 3% = $3,000

Partner proposes $4,000 co-branded webinar campaign
HDIM MDF contribution: $2,000 (50% match from $3,000 balance)
Partner contribution: $2,000
Total campaign budget: $4,000
```

## Partner Onboarding Process

### Referral Partner Onboarding (1 Week)

| Day | Activity | Owner |
|-----|----------|-------|
| 1 | Agreement signed | Partner |
| 1-2 | Welcome email, portal access | HDIM |
| 2-3 | Onboarding training (1 hour) | Partner |
| 3-5 | Review co-branded materials | Partner |
| 5-7 | First check-in call | HDIM |
| 7+ | Active partner, ready to refer | Partner |

**Onboarding Training Agenda (1 Hour):**
1. HDIM product overview (15 min)
2. Target customer profiles (10 min)
3. Qualification criteria (10 min)
4. Referral submission process (10 min)
5. Partner portal walkthrough (10 min)
6. Q&A (5 min)

---

### Reseller Partner Onboarding (2-4 Weeks)

| Week | Activity | Owner |
|------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Agreement negotiation and signing | Both |
| 1 | Account manager assigned | HDIM |
| 1-2 | Sales certification training (4 hours) | Partner |
| 2 | Technical certification training (4 hours) | Partner |
| 2-3 | Demo environment setup | HDIM |
| 3 | First joint pipeline review | Both |
| 3-4 | Co-marketing planning | Both |
| 4 | Active partner, ready to sell | Partner |

**Sales Certification Curriculum (4 Hours):**
1. Healthcare quality measurement market (30 min)
2. HDIM product deep dive (60 min)
3. Competitive positioning (30 min)
4. Sales methodology and talk tracks (45 min)
5. Pricing and packaging (30 min)
6. Demo best practices (45 min)
7. Certification exam (30 min)

**Technical Certification Curriculum (4 Hours):**
1. HDIM architecture overview (30 min)
2. Integration options (FHIR, CSV, API) (60 min)
3. Implementation methodology (45 min)
4. Common troubleshooting (30 min)
5. Customer success handoff (30 min)
6. Support processes (30 min)
7. Certification exam (30 min)

---

| Week | Activity | Owner |
