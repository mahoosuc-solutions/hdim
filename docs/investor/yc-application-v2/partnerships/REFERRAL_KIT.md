## How to Make a Referral

### Step 1: Qualify the Opportunity

Ensure the prospect meets basic criteria:
- [ ] Healthcare organization (practice, FQHC, ACO, health system)
- [ ] Decision-maker or influencer contact available
- [ ] Expressed interest in quality measurement
- [ ] Not currently an HDIM customer
- [ ] Not already in HDIM sales pipeline

### Step 2: Introduce HDIM

**Option A: Warm Introduction**
- Connect prospect directly with your HDIM contact
- Three-way email introduction
- Joint call with HDIM sales

**Option B: Direct Referral**
- Submit referral through partner portal
- HDIM sales reaches out directly
- You stay informed via portal

### Step 3: Submit the Referral

**Via Partner Portal:**
1. Log in to partners.healthdatainmotion.com
2. Click "Submit Referral"
3. Complete referral form
4. Receive confirmation within 24 hours

**Via Email:**
Send to partners@healthdatainmotion.com with:
- Organization name
- Contact name, title, email, phone
- Organization type and size
- Key pain points discussed
- How you know this prospect
- Any timeline or budget information

### Step 4: Track and Follow Up

- Monitor referral status in partner portal
- Receive updates at key milestones
- Participate in sales process if requested
- Get notified when deal closes

## Partner FAQ

### About the Program

**Q: How long is the partner agreement?**
A: 12 months, auto-renewing. Either party can cancel with 30 days notice.

**Q: Can I work with competing vendors?**
A: Yes. Our program is non-exclusive. We believe you should recommend the best solution for each customer.

**Q: What's the minimum commitment?**
A: 2 qualified referrals per quarter to remain an active partner. No referrals = inactive status (can reactivate anytime).

**Q: Do I need healthcare experience?**
A: Not required, but helpful. Our training covers everything you need to know about HDIM.

### About Referrals

**Q: What makes a referral "qualified"?**
A: A qualified referral includes a decision-maker or influencer contact who has expressed interest in quality measurement and is not already in our pipeline.

**Q: What if the prospect is already talking to HDIM?**
A: We'll let you know within 24 hours if the referral is already in our system. If it is, no commission applies.

**Q: Can I submit referrals from my existing clients?**
A: Yes! Referrals from consulting clients, past employers, or any other source are welcome.

**Q: What if the deal takes a long time to close?**
A: Referrals are valid for 12 months. If the deal closes within that window, you earn commission.

**Q: What if the customer cancels?**
A: Commissions are non-refundable once earned. We only pay after the customer's first payment.

### About Commission

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Within 30 days of the customer's first payment. Most customers pay at contract signing.

**Q: How do I track my referrals?**
A: Log in to the partner portal at partners.healthdatainmotion.com. You'll see all referrals, statuses, and commissions.

**Q: Is there a cap on commissions?**
A: No cap! The more you refer, the more you earn—and you get bonus tiers at higher volumes.

### About Support

**Q: What if I need help with a specific opportunity?**
A: Contact your partner manager or email partners@healthdatainmotion.com. We can join calls, provide custom materials, or answer prospect questions.

**Q: Can HDIM sales work directly with my prospects?**
A: Yes. You can either make an introduction or submit the referral and have us reach out directly. Your choice.

**Q: What if my referral has a question I can't answer?**
A: Either connect them with us directly or send the question to partners@healthdatainmotion.com. We'll get you an answer within 24 hours.
