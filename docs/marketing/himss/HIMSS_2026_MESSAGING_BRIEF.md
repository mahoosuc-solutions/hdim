# HIMSS 2026 Messaging Brief

## Purpose
Single-page framing for customer and investor meetings focused on four pillars:
1. Accelerator
2. Platform
3. Validation implemented
4. Performance implemented

## Conference Talk Track
"HDIM is not just a product demo. It is an operating model: domain-led architecture, AI-accelerated implementation,
proof-linked governance, and measurable outcomes."

## Messaging Pillars

### 1) Accelerator
- Story-point delivery model with explicit scope and assumptions.
- AI leverage increases speed while keeping architecture and safety human-led.
- Repeatable integration and governance patterns reduce delivery risk.

### 2) Platform
- Event-driven quality measurement platform with FHIR-native pathways.
- Buyer-specific paths for CIO/CISO, clinical leaders, and procurement.
- Flexible deployment posture aligned with enterprise requirements.

### 3) Validation Implemented
- Claim-to-proof mapping via trust center and evidence room.
- Release evidence, control traceability, and manual approval workflow in place.
- Evidence freshness and observed/modeled status now explicit in the buyer flow.

### 4) Performance Implemented
- Performance and metrics views connected to governance and release evidence.
- ROI method references anchored in defensibility packs and pilot outcome methodology.
- Conversion analytics instrumented across evidence request workflows.
