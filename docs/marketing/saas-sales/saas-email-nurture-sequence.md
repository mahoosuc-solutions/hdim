## Email Sequence Best Practices

### Timing

**Send Times:**
- **B2B:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM or 2-4 PM (recipient's timezone)
- **Technical buyers:** Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM-12 PM
- **Business buyers:** Tuesday-Thursday, 2-4 PM

**Frequency:**
- Email 1: Day 0 (immediate)
- Email 2: Day 3 (3 days later)
- Email 3: Day 7 (4 days later)
- Email 4: Day 10 (3 days later)
- Email 5: Day 14 (4 days later)
- Email 6: Day 17 (3 days later)
- Email 7: Day 21 (4 days later)

### Personalization

**Variables to Replace:**
- `[First Name]` - Recipient's first name
- `[Your Name]` - Sender's name
- `[Your Title]` - Sender's title
- `[Company Name]` - Recipient's company (if known)

**Customization:**
- Reference specific pain points mentioned in discovery
- Include relevant use cases for their organization type
- Mention mutual connections or shared interests

### A/B Testing

**Subject Lines:**
- Test different angles (problem, solution, benefit)
- Test length (short vs. descriptive)
- Test personalization (name vs. company)

**CTAs:**
- Test button text ("Download" vs. "Get Started")
- Test placement (top vs. bottom)
- Test urgency ("Download Now" vs. "Download")

### Tracking

**Metrics to Monitor:**
- Open rate (target: 25-35%)
- Click-through rate (target: 5-10%)
- Conversion rate (target: 2-5%)
- Unsubscribe rate (target: <0.5%)

**Optimization:**
- Test subject lines for higher open rates
- Test CTAs for higher click rates
- Test content for higher engagement
- Test timing for better performance
