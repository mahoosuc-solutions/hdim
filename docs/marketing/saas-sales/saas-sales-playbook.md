# HDIM Platform: SaaS Sales Playbook
## Complete Sales Process for Technical & Business Buyers

**Purpose:** Step-by-step sales process with discovery questions, objection handling, and closing strategies  
**Audience:** Sales team, Account Executives, Sales Engineers  
**Format:** Comprehensive playbook with scripts, questions, and frameworks

---

## Sales Process Overview

### 5-Stage Sales Process

```
1. Discovery → 2. Qualification → 3. Demo → 4. Proposal → 5. Close
```

**Timeline:** 60-90 days (typical B2B SaaS sales cycle)

**Key Stages:**
- **Discovery:** Understand pain points, needs, budget
- **Qualification:** BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
- **Demo:** Show HDIM capabilities, address technical questions
- **Proposal:** Customized solution, pricing, implementation plan
- **Close:** Negotiation, contract, implementation kickoff

---

## Stage 1: Discovery

### Purpose

Understand the prospect's:
- Current situation
- Pain points
- Goals and objectives
- Technical requirements
- Business requirements

### Discovery Framework

**BANT Qualification:**
- **Budget:** Do they have budget allocated?
- **Authority:** Who makes the decision?
- **Need:** Do they have a real need?
- **Timeline:** When do they need to solve this?

### Discovery Questions

#### Current State Questions

**1. Current Platform/Systems:**
- "What healthcare platforms or systems are you currently using?"
- "How long have you been using [current system]?"
- "What's working well with your current setup?"
- "What's not working well?"

**2. Integration Challenges:**
- "How do you currently integrate with EHRs and other systems?"
- "How long does a typical integration take?"
- "What's the cost of maintaining custom integrations?"
- "How many custom interfaces do you have?"

**3. Quality Measurement:**
- "How do you currently measure quality (HEDIS, Star Ratings)?"
- "What's the latency of your quality data? (Real-time, daily, monthly?)"
- "How do you identify care gaps?"
- "What's the process for closing care gaps?"

**4. Technical Infrastructure:**
- "What's your current architecture? (Monolithic, microservices, cloud, on-premise?)"
- "What APIs do you currently use? (REST, SOAP, proprietary?)"
- "How do you handle real-time data? (Batch, streaming, polling?)"
- "What's your current scalability? (Events/second, concurrent users?)"

#### Pain Point Questions

**1. Integration Pain:**
- "What's the biggest challenge with your current integrations?"
- "How much time does your team spend on integration maintenance?"
- "What's the cost of custom integrations? (One-time, annual?)"
- "How long does it take to add a new system integration?"

**2. Quality Measurement Pain:**
- "What's the impact of delayed quality data? (Revenue, compliance, patient care?)"
- "How much manual work is involved in quality measurement?"
- "What's the cost of manual quality reporting? (FTEs, consultants?)"
- "How do delayed quality measures affect your Star Ratings?"

**3. Technical Pain:**
- "What's the biggest technical challenge you're facing?"
- "How does your current architecture limit you?"
- "What's the cost of maintaining legacy systems?"
- "How long does it take to deploy new features?"

**4. Business Pain:**
- "What's the revenue impact of lower Star Ratings?"
- "What's the cost of missed care gaps?"
- "How does delayed quality data affect value-based contracts?"
- "What's the opportunity cost of slow deployments?"

#### Goal Questions

**1. Business Goals:**
- "What are your top 3 business objectives for the next 12 months?"
- "What would success look like for a new platform?"
- "What's the revenue impact of improving Star Ratings?"
- "What's the cost savings target for automation?"

**2. Technical Goals:**
- "What are your top 3 technical objectives?"
- "What architecture do you want to move toward? (Microservices, API-first, cloud-native?)"
- "What's your target deployment timeline?"
- "What's your scalability requirement?"

**3. Healthcare Goals:**
- "What quality measures are most important to you?"
- "What's your care gap closure target?"
- "What's your Star Ratings improvement goal?"
- "What value-based contracts are you pursuing?"

#### Requirements Questions

**1. Technical Requirements:**
- "What APIs do you need? (REST, GraphQL, WebSocket?)"
- "What's your performance requirement? (Latency, throughput?)"
- "What's your scalability requirement?"
- "What compliance requirements do you have? (HIPAA, SOC 2, HITRUST?)"

**2. Integration Requirements:**
- "What systems do you need to integrate with?"
- "What data formats do you need to support? (FHIR, HL7 v2, X12?)"
- "What's your integration timeline?"
- "What's your integration budget?"

**3. Business Requirements:**
- "What's your budget range?"
- "Who's involved in the decision?"
- "What's your timeline?"
- "What's your success criteria?"

### Discovery Script

**Opening:**
"Thanks for taking the time to speak with me today. I'd like to understand your current situation and see if HDIM might be a good fit. Let's start with understanding what you're currently using and what challenges you're facing."

**Discovery Flow:**
1. Current state questions (10 minutes)
2. Pain point questions (10 minutes)
3. Goal questions (10 minutes)
4. Requirements questions (10 minutes)
5. Summary and next steps (5 minutes)

**Closing:**
"Based on what you've shared, it sounds like HDIM could be a good fit. Let me summarize what I heard and propose next steps..."

## Stage 2: Qualification

### Purpose

Qualify the opportunity using BANT:
- **Budget:** Confirmed budget allocated
- **Authority:** Decision maker identified and engaged
- **Need:** Clear pain points and requirements
- **Timeline:** Defined timeline and urgency

### Qualification Framework

**BANT Scorecard:**

| Criteria | Score | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| **Budget** | 0-3 | 0=No budget, 1=Exploring, 2=Budget allocated, 3=Budget confirmed |
| **Authority** | 0-3 | 0=No decision maker, 1=Influencer, 2=Decision maker engaged, 3=Champion |
| **Need** | 0-3 | 0=No need, 1=Nice to have, 2=Real need, 3=Urgent need |
| **Timeline** | 0-3 | 0=No timeline, 1=12+ months, 2=6-12 months, 3=0-6 months |

**Qualification Threshold:** 8+ total score (out of 12)

### Qualification Questions

#### Budget Questions

**1. Budget Allocation:**
- "Do you have budget allocated for this initiative?"
- "What's your budget range?"
- "When was the budget approved?"
- "Who approved the budget?"

**2. Budget Process:**
- "What's your budget approval process?"
- "What's the timeline for budget approval?"
- "What documentation do you need for budget approval?"

**3. Budget Constraints:**
- "Are there any budget constraints we should be aware of?"
- "What's the maximum budget you can allocate?"
- "Are there competing priorities for this budget?"

#### Authority Questions

**1. Decision Makers:**
- "Who's involved in the decision-making process?"
- "Who has final approval authority?"
- "Who's the technical decision maker?"
- "Who's the business decision maker?"

**2. Decision Process:**
- "What's your decision-making process?"
- "What's the timeline for a decision?"
- "What criteria will you use to evaluate solutions?"
- "Who else needs to be involved?"

**3. Champion:**
- "Who's your internal champion for this initiative?"
- "What's their role and influence?"
- "How can we support them?"

#### Need Questions

**1. Pain Severity:**
- "How critical is solving this problem?"
- "What's the impact of not solving this?"
- "What's the cost of the current situation?"
- "What's the opportunity cost?"

**2. Urgency:**
- "How urgent is this need?"
- "What's driving the urgency?"
- "What happens if you don't solve this soon?"

**3. Alternatives:**
- "What alternatives are you considering?"
- "What's the status of other solutions?"
- "Why haven't you solved this already?"

#### Timeline Questions

**1. Timeline:**
- "What's your target timeline for implementation?"
- "What's driving the timeline?"
- "What are the key milestones?"
- "What's the go-live target?"

**2. Urgency:**
- "How urgent is this timeline?"
- "What happens if you miss the timeline?"
- "What's the cost of delay?"

**3. Dependencies:**
- "What are the dependencies for this timeline?"
- "What could delay the timeline?"
- "What needs to happen before you can start?"

### Qualification Script

**Opening:**
"Based on our discovery conversation, I'd like to understand a bit more about your budget, decision process, and timeline to see if we're a good fit for each other."

**Qualification Flow:**
1. Budget questions (5 minutes)
2. Authority questions (5 minutes)
3. Need questions (5 minutes)
4. Timeline questions (5 minutes)
5. Qualification summary and next steps (5 minutes)

**Closing:**
"Based on what you've shared, it sounds like we're a good fit. Let me propose next steps..."

## Stage 3: Demo

### Purpose

Show HDIM capabilities:
- Architecture and technology
- Healthcare workflows
- Integration capabilities
- Performance and scalability

### Demo Framework

**Demo Structure:**
1. **Opening (5 min):** Set context, understand what they want to see
2. **Architecture Overview (10 min):** Microservices, APIs, event streaming
3. **Healthcare Workflows (15 min):** Quality measures, care gaps, risk stratification
4. **Integration Demo (10 min):** FHIR R4 APIs, real-time data flow
5. **Performance Demo (5 min):** Scalability, latency, throughput
6. **Q&A (10 min):** Address questions, objections
7. **Next Steps (5 min):** Proposal, trial, reference call

### Demo Script

**Opening:**
"Thanks for taking the time for this demo. I'd like to show you HDIM's architecture, healthcare workflows, and integration capabilities. Before we start, what are you most interested in seeing?"

**Architecture Overview:**
"Let me start with HDIM's architecture. HDIM is built like modern SaaS with microservices, API-first design, and real-time event streaming. Here's how it works..."

**Healthcare Workflows:**
"Now let me show you HDIM's healthcare workflows. HDIM provides real-time quality measures, automated care gap detection, and AI-powered risk stratification..."

**Integration Demo:**
"Let me show you how HDIM integrates with your systems. HDIM uses FHIR R4 standard APIs, so there's no custom code needed..."

**Performance Demo:**
"Let me show you HDIM's performance. HDIM handles 10,000+ events per second with sub-second latency..."

**Q&A:**
"What questions do you have about HDIM's capabilities?"

**Next Steps:**
"Based on what you've seen, I'd like to propose next steps. Would you like to see a customized proposal, schedule a trial, or speak with a customer reference?"

## Stage 4: Proposal

### Purpose

Present customized solution:
- Solution overview
- Implementation plan
- Pricing
- ROI analysis

### Proposal Framework

**Proposal Structure:**
1. **Executive Summary:** Problem, solution, benefits
2. **Solution Overview:** HDIM capabilities, architecture
3. **Implementation Plan:** Timeline, phases, deliverables
4. **Pricing:** Subscription, usage, total cost
5. **ROI Analysis:** Cost savings, revenue impact, payback
6. **Next Steps:** Timeline, decision process, contract

### Proposal Script

**Opening:**
"Thanks for your interest in HDIM. I've prepared a customized proposal based on our conversations. Let me walk you through it."

**Executive Summary:**
"Based on our discussions, your main challenges are [pain points]. HDIM addresses these with [solution]. The benefits are [benefits]."

**Solution Overview:**
"HDIM provides [capabilities] with [architecture]. Here's how it addresses your requirements..."

**Implementation Plan:**
"Here's the implementation timeline. Phase 1 is [description] in [timeline]. Phase 2 is [description] in [timeline]..."

**Pricing:**
"Here's the pricing. The base subscription is [amount] with [usage-based pricing]. The total first-year cost is [amount]."

**ROI Analysis:**
"Here's the ROI. Based on your situation, HDIM delivers [cost savings] in cost savings and [revenue impact] in revenue impact. The payback period is [timeframe]."

**Next Steps:**
"Here are the next steps. We need [decision] by [date] to meet your [timeline]. The contract process is [process]."

## Stage 5: Close

### Purpose

Finalize the deal:
- Negotiate terms
- Address final objections
- Sign contract
- Kick off implementation

### Closing Framework

**Closing Structure:**
1. **Final Objections:** Address any remaining concerns
2. **Negotiation:** Terms, pricing, timeline
3. **Contract:** Review, sign, execute
4. **Implementation Kickoff:** Timeline, team, next steps

### Closing Script

**Opening:**
"Thanks for your interest in moving forward with HDIM. Let's finalize the details and get started."

**Final Objections:**
"Before we proceed, are there any remaining concerns or questions?"

**Negotiation:**
"Here are the terms we discussed. [Terms]. Are these acceptable, or would you like to discuss any adjustments?"

**Contract:**
"Here's the contract. Let's review the key terms: [Terms]. Once you sign, we'll kick off implementation."

**Implementation Kickoff:**
"Great! Here's the implementation timeline. We'll start with [phase] on [date]. Your implementation team is [team]. Next steps are [steps]."

## Objection Handling

### Common Objections

#### "It's too expensive."

**Response:**
"I understand cost is a concern. Let me show you the ROI. HDIM delivers [cost savings] in cost savings and [revenue impact] in revenue impact. The payback period is [timeframe], which means HDIM pays for itself in [timeframe]. Plus, HDIM eliminates [costs], saving you [amount] annually. Would you like to see the detailed ROI analysis?"

#### "We need to see more customer references."

**Response:**
"Absolutely. I'd be happy to connect you with [similar organization] who's using HDIM for [use case]. They've achieved [results]. Would you like to schedule a reference call?"

#### "We're not ready to make a decision yet."

**Response:**
"I understand. What would help you make a decision? Would a trial help? Or a more detailed technical review? Or customer references? Let's identify what you need and create a plan to get there."

#### "We're evaluating other solutions."

**Response:**
"That makes sense. What other solutions are you evaluating? I'd be happy to provide a competitive comparison. HDIM's key differentiators are [differentiators]. What criteria are you using to evaluate solutions?"

#### "We need custom features."

**Response:**
"HDIM provides APIs for customization. What specific features do you need? Let's see if HDIM can support them via APIs, or if we need to discuss custom development. HDIM's API-first design makes customization easier than traditional platforms."
