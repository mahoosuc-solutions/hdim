Controlled-content guardrails

  • Do not distribute NCQA-owned HEDIS specifications or licensed code-system datasets in public artifacts.
  • Do not redistribute HL7 specification text beyond permitted usage.
  • Keep customer-specific and licensed measure packs outside public repositories and public web bundles.
  • Route commercial rights and service commitments through executed MSA/BAA agreements.
