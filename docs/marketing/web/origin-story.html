Origin Story

The moment architecture pressure turned into an AI solutioning operating model.

What Broke

The team was delivering healthcare workflows with growing integration complexity, inconsistent implementation quality, and long lead times for every cross-service change.

What Changed

The approach shifted from prompt-driven coding to specification-first engineering. AI assistants were directed by complete architectural specifications, interface contracts, and test definitions.

Why It Matters

This changed delivery from incremental coding to orchestrated system construction: faster timelines, broader service coverage, and higher consistency across artifacts.
