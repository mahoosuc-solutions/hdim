**Target close window:** April 7-21, 2026

## Scoring Guidance

Use this conversion for each category:

- `1` = weak evidence / no proof
- `2` = partial relevance / limited proof
- `3` = acceptable baseline proof
- `4` = strong proof and repeatability
- `5` = exceptional proof with direct relevance to MA pilot commercialization

Weighted points formula:

`(Score / 5) * Weight`

## Gate Criteria

- `>= 85`: partner-track candidate (priority for deep diligence)
- `75-84`: advance to structured interview
- `< 75`: do not advance unless strategic exception approved

## Hard Disqualifiers

Disqualify regardless of numeric score if any are true:

1. Refuses weekly operator ownership.
2. Seeks title/equity only, without execution commitment.
3. Cannot provide verifiable deployment outcomes.
4. Demonstrates inconsistent ethics, communication, or reference feedback.
5. Requires full-time cash comp pre-funding outside agreed constraints.

## Top-20 Selection Rule

When selecting top 20 from longlist:

1. Sort by weighted score.
2. Enforce diversity across payer/provider backgrounds.
3. Keep at least 5 backup candidates above 75.
4. Prioritize candidates with near-term availability over prestige-only profiles.
