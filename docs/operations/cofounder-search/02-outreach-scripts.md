# Outreach Scripts: Medical Cofounder Search

## Positioning Principles

- Keep message operator-first, not advisor-first.
- Anchor to defined 90-day mandate.
- Be explicit on equity-heavy structure plus milestone stipend.
- Emphasize national MA deployment opportunity.

## Script 1: Warm Introduction Request (to mutual contact)

Subject: Intro request: clinical operator role (national MA deployment)

Hi [Mutual Contact Name],

I am building HDIM through Mahoosuc Solutions and looking for a medically credible operating partner, specifically a CMIO/informatics physician profile.

This is not a passive advisory role. I need someone who can lead clinical deployment, shape pilot outcomes, and operate weekly with me.

Could you introduce me to [Candidate Name]? I am proposing a `Chair of Clinical Strategy` role with a 90-day mandate and a defined path to cofounder/equity alignment based on execution.

If helpful, I can send a one-page brief before the intro.

Thanks,
[Your Name]

## Objection Handling Snippets

### Objection: "I can only advise, not operate weekly."
Understood. This role requires weekly execution ownership. If that is not feasible now, we can explore a separate advisory track, but not this cofounder path.

### Objection: "I need full-time cash comp first."
We are pre-funding and structuring this as equity-heavy with milestone stipend. The model is designed around aligned build-phase risk and upside.

### Objection: "Why not wait until after funding?"
The clinical operator is a force multiplier for pilot design, MA credibility, and deployment success. Delaying this role increases go-to-market risk.

## Communication Cadence

- Day 0: Initial outreach.
- Day 5-7: Follow-up 1.
- Day 12-14: Follow-up 2 with one-page role brief.
- Day 21: Close loop and move candidate to nurture if no response.
