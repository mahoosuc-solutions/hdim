# Chair of Clinical Strategy: 90-Day Charter

**Role type:** Operating leadership mandate  
**Term:** 90 days (initial)  
**Start target:** Within 6-8 weeks from February 24, 2026  
**Reporting line:** Direct partnership with Founder

## Role Purpose

Lead clinical deployment strategy and execution for MA pilot commercialization, translating clinical credibility into measurable pilot outcomes and repeatable deployment playbooks.

## Time Commitment

- Target: 8-12 hours/week minimum during 90-day term.
- Required: Weekly operating meeting + milestone ownership.
- Optional: Customer-facing executive calls and conference representation.

## Compensation Framework

- Equity-heavy structure with milestone-based stipend.
- Cofounder/equity expansion considered only after milestone performance and mutual fit validation.

## Core Responsibilities

1. Pilot design ownership
- Define pilot clinical scope, inclusion criteria, and success definitions.

2. Stakeholder orchestration
- Build alignment with CMO/VP Quality, CFO, and IT stakeholders.

3. Deployment governance
- Establish 30/60/90-day cadence, check-ins, and escalation procedures.

4. Clinical narrative leadership
- Serve as credible clinical face for executive and field conversations.

5. Repeatability design
- Build version 1 deployment playbook for multi-plan replication.

## 90-Day Milestones

### Milestone 1 (By Day 30): Clinical Scope and Governance Locked

Deliverables:

1. Pilot scope document with measurable outcomes.
2. Stakeholder map (clinical, financial, technical).
3. Weekly governance cadence and decision rights matrix.

Success criteria:

- Scope and measures approved by Founder.
- At least one MA-aligned pilot pathway defined.

### Milestone 2 (By Day 60): Market Activation and Credibility Expansion

Deliverables:

1. Two qualified MA clinical stakeholder relationships activated.
2. Executive-ready clinical + financial narrative deck.
3. Objection-handling framework for clinical procurement path.

Success criteria:

- At least two high-quality deployment discussions advanced.
- Stakeholder feedback confirms clinical credibility and clarity.

### Milestone 3 (By Day 90): Repeatable Deployment Asset Complete

Deliverables:

1. Version 1 MA pilot deployment playbook.
2. Post-pilot expansion decision framework.
3. Role conversion recommendation memo.

Success criteria:

- Founder confirms readiness to replicate deployment model.
- Clear recommendation on cofounder conversion with evidence.

## Weekly Operating Cadence

- 45-minute Monday execution sync.
- Friday written update (wins, blockers, next-week priorities).
- Shared tracker updates on candidate introductions, stakeholder progress, and pilot readiness.

## KPI Set

1. Number of MA clinical stakeholder conversations advanced.
2. Pilot scope approval cycle time.
3. Stakeholder alignment quality (CMO/CFO/IT consistency).
4. Readiness score of deployment playbook artifacts.

## Risks and Mitigations

- Risk: advisor-style posture instead of operator behavior.
- Mitigation: weekly deliverable accountability and hard milestone gates.

- Risk: high credibility, low availability.
- Mitigation: minimum time commitment and missed-milestone reset clause.

- Risk: unclear authority boundaries.
- Mitigation: documented decision rights matrix in first 30 days.

## Exit Conditions

Any of the following may end charter early:

1. Two consecutive missed milestone commitments without remediation.
2. Persistent communication or accountability failures.
3. Strategic misalignment on operating model.

## End-of-Term Outcomes

At day 90, choose one path:

1. Convert to cofounder-equity track.
2. Extend Chair role for another term with revised goals.
3. Close role and transition knowledge.
