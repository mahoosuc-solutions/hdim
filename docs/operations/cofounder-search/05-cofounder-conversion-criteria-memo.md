# Cofounder Conversion Criteria Memo

**Purpose:** Define objective criteria and decision process for converting `Chair of Clinical Strategy` into cofounder-equity role.

## Conversion Decision Date

Default: End of 90-day Chair term.

## Conversion Prerequisites (All Required)

1. Milestone execution
- Completed Day 30, Day 60, and Day 90 deliverables at acceptable quality.

2. Operator behavior
- Demonstrated weekly ownership, follow-through, and execution leadership.

3. Commercial impact evidence
- Advanced clinically credible MA stakeholder opportunities with measurable momentum.

4. Founder compatibility
- High trust, aligned pace, and constructive conflict management in real decisions.

5. Reference validation
- Positive confirmation from executive, clinical, and implementation references.

## Evaluation Grid

| **Total** | **100** |  |  |

Decision threshold:

- `>=85`: convert to cofounder track.
- `75-84`: extend Chair term with targeted remediation goals.
- `<75`: do not convert.

## Conversion Paths

### Path A: Full Conversion

When threshold is met and both parties aligned:

1. Confirm role scope (clinical strategy, deployment leadership, market-facing responsibilities).
2. Finalize equity framework with vesting and performance alignment.
3. Publish internal operating model and decision rights.

### Path B: Extended Trial

When partial alignment exists:

1. Extend 60-90 days.
2. Define 2-3 hard remediation goals.
3. Re-evaluate with same scorecard.

### Path C: No Conversion

When fit or performance is insufficient:

1. Close role respectfully.
2. Capture knowledge transfer assets.
3. Continue candidate pipeline with backups.

## Governance and Risk Controls

1. No cofounder title before evidence-based conversion criteria are met.
2. Do not grant irreversible strategic control during trial phase.
3. Document all milestone reviews and decision rationale.
