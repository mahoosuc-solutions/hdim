# Medical Cofounder Search Operating Pack

**Last updated:** February 24, 2026  
**Owner:** Founder (Mahoosuc Solutions)  
**Objective:** Identify and close a clinically credible, nationally connected medical cofounder in 6-8 weeks.

## Scope

This pack implements the national CMIO/informatics cofounder search plan with an operator-first pathway:

1. `Chair of Clinical Strategy` 90-day operating mandate.
2. Conversion to cofounder/equity role based on milestone execution and founder fit.

## Files

1. `01-candidate-scorecard.md`
- Weighted scoring model with pass/fail gates.
- Hard disqualifiers and interview sequencing.

2. `02-outreach-scripts.md`
- Warm intro request script.
- Direct outbound founder note.
- Follow-up sequence and objection handling.

3. `03-structured-interview-rubric.md`
- Stage-by-stage interview flow.
- Standardized scoring and debrief format.

4. `04-chair-of-clinical-strategy-90-day-charter.md`
- Role charter, cadence, deliverables, and success metrics.

5. `05-cofounder-conversion-criteria-memo.md`
- Conversion triggers, decision framework, and term-shaping guardrails.

6. `06-targeted-messaging-don-liss-joe-kimura.md`
- Candidate-specific outreach messaging for Dr. Don Liss and Dr. Joe Kimura.

7. `07-yc-cofounder-profile.md`
- YC Cofounder profile draft with role definition, skills, and 90-day scope.

8. `candidate-pipeline-template.csv`
- Track 60-name longlist and top-20 active outreach funnel.

## Execution Cadence

- Week 1: Build and score longlist (60 candidates), select top 20.
- Week 2: Outbound + intro calls.
- Week 3: Structured interviews and scenario evaluation.
- Week 4: References and diligence on top 3.
- Week 5-6: Offer `Chair of Clinical Strategy` role to primary candidate.
- Week 7-8: Confirm operating rhythm and maintain backup pipeline.

## Operating Rules

- Do not advance candidates who cannot commit to weekly execution ownership.
- Prioritize national MA access over local-only network strength.
- Preserve equity discipline: no cofounder title grant before execution evidence.
