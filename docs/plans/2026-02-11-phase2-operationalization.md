### Task 3: Pilot Customer Playbook (Days 7-9)
**Owner:** Customer Success Lead + VP Sales
**Deliverables:**

1. **Pilot Qualification Criteria**
   - ✅ 10,000-30,000 members (sweet spot for impact)
   - ✅ Forward-thinking leadership (CMO/VP Quality with AI interest)
   - ✅ Value-based contract exposure ($500K+ quality bonuses at risk)
   - ✅ Willing to be public reference + co-marketing partner
   - ✅ Existing analytics/data science capability
   - 🔴 Red flags: Legacy IT, resistant to change, publicly traded (compliance delays)

2. **Pilot Discovery Call Template**
   - "What's your biggest challenge with quality measures?"
   - "How much is quality performance costing you monthly?"
   - "What if we could close 35% more gaps without more headcount?"
   - "Would you be willing to be a co-marketing partner for an AI innovation pilot?"

3. **Pilot Onboarding Checklist**
   - Week 1: Data integration (patient list, care gap CQL)
   - Week 2: AI model training (2 months historical data for prediction accuracy)
   - Week 3: Provider training (AI dashboard, clinical summary workflow)
   - Week 4: Go-live (predictive gaps + clinical summaries in production)

4. **Pilot Success Metrics Framework**
   - ✅ Gap closure rate: 35% improvement (target: 60% → 95%)
   - ✅ Provider time savings: 40% reduction in pre-visit planning
   - ✅ Quality measure improvement: +5-10% on targeted measures (BCS, CDC, COL)
   - ✅ Patient satisfaction: +15% in gap closure outreach response rate
   - ✅ Financial impact: $X captured in quality bonuses (tracked via Phase 3 system)

5. **Case Study Framework**
   - Customer profile: Industry, size, challenges
   - Problem statement: "Stuck at 3.5 Stars, struggling with quality reporting workload"
   - Solution: "AI-powered predictive gaps + clinical summaries"
   - Results: Quantified metrics (gap closure %, time saved, $ captured)
   - Quote: Customer testimonial about AI-first approach
   - Deployment: 90-day timeline, go-live process
