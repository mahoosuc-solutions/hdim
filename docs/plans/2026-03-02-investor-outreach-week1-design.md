## Strategy: Two Parallel Tracks

### Track 1 — Healthix Warm Path (this week, highest priority)

**Why:** The Healthix CEO/CIO are credible references in the NYC health IT ecosystem. Their endorsement or introductions carry far more weight than any cold email. A single warm intro from them is worth 20 cold emails.

**The ask:** A 20-minute validation call — ask for honest feedback on what you're building, not for favors. This preserves the relationship and naturally leads to intros, advisory interest, or pilot conversations.

**Expected outcomes:**
- Validation of the problem from someone who lived it
- Potential pilot customer (Healthix or their member health systems)
- Warm intros to 1-2 angels or VCs they know
- Advisory relationship that adds credibility to the pitch

---

### Track 2 — LinkedIn Angel Warm-Up (this week, parallel)

**Why:** Cold outreach to Glen Tullman, Farzad Mostashari, and John Halamka with zero context gets a ~2% response rate. Thoughtful LinkedIn engagement for 2-3 weeks before outreach meaningfully improves that.

**The action:** Like and leave substantive comments (2-3 sentences, adds value) on 1-2 posts per day from the Tier 1 angels. No pitching, no DMs yet — just build name recognition.

**Target profiles to engage this week:**

## Key Principles

1. **Never pitch on first contact** — ask for feedback, advice, or reaction
2. **Lead with Healthix** — it's your strongest credibility signal in this ecosystem
3. **Be specific** — vague asks get ignored; name the exact question you want answered
4. **Follow up exactly twice** — Day 4 and Day 10, then move on
5. **Log everything** — responses, tone, what resonated; patterns emerge after 10 contacts
