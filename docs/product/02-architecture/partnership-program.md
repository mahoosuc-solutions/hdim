# Partnership & Channel Program

## Executive Summary

HealthData in Motion partners with **system integrators, resellers, consultants, and technology vendors** to expand market reach and deliver comprehensive healthcare IT solutions. Our partnership program offers revenue sharing, technical support, and go-to-market resources.

**Partner Types**:
- System Integrators (implementation partners)
- Resellers (sales partners)
- Technology Vendors (integration partners)
- Consultants (advisory partners)
- Health Plans/Health Systems (referral partners)

## Partner Program Tiers

### Tier 1: Technology Partner

**Requirements**:
- Technical integration with HealthData
- Shared customer opportunity
- Co-marketing commitment

**Benefits**:
- Joint GTM resources
- Co-marketing funds ($5K-$10K annually)
- Integration certification
- Dedicated integration engineer

**Examples**: EHR vendors, pharmacy vendors, lab systems

### Tier 2: Implementation Partner

**Requirements**:
- Implementation certification
- 5+ successful implementations annually
- Support team trained

**Benefits**:
- 20% implementation margin
- Deal registration
- Marketing development fund ($25K-$50K)
- Dedicated account manager
- Priority support

**Examples**: Consulting firms, system integrators

### Tier 3: Reseller Partner

**Requirements**:
- Sales certification
- 3-5 customer commitments annually
- Contract sales team

**Benefits**:
- 30% sales commission (recurring)
- Deal registration
- Marketing development fund ($50K-$100K)
- Co-branded marketing materials
- Dedicated sales engineer

**Examples**: Healthcare consultants, IT vendors

### Tier 4: Strategic Partner

**For large, strategic partners**:
- Custom arrangements
- Dedicated teams
- Significant MDFs ($100K+)
- Executive relationship

**Examples**: Major EHR vendors, large consultancies

## Revenue Models

### Reseller Model

**Structure**:
- Partner sells to customer
- Partner implements solution
- Partner provides ongoing support
- HealthData provides back-end services

**Revenue Sharing**:
- Year 1: 30% of ACV (annual contract value)
- Year 2-3: 20% recurring
- Implementations: $100-$200/patient margin

### Integration Model

**Structure**:
- HealthData integrates with partner technology
- Joint customers leverage both solutions
- Partner benefits from HealthData validation

**Revenue**:
- Co-marketing funds
- Lead sharing agreements
- Joint go-to-market

### Referral Model

**Structure**:
- Partner refers customers to HealthData
- HealthData closes deal
- Partner receives referral revenue

**Revenue**: 10% of first year ACV

## Conflict Resolution

**Partner-to-Partner**:
- Deal registration prevents overlap
- Territory protection (for large deals)
- Channel partner preference (equal opportunity)

**Direct vs. Partner**:
- Direct sales avoid partner territory
- HealthData rarely sells direct to SMB
- Enterprise/national accounts: negotiated

## Partner Agreements

**Standard Terms**:
- 1-3 year partnership agreements
- 90-day termination for cause
- Non-exclusive (with territory for large partners)
- Confidentiality requirements
- Brand usage guidelines

**Payment Terms**:
- Net 30 invoicing
- Quarterly reconciliation
- Performance metrics tracked

## Success Metrics

### Partner Performance

**Tracked**:
- Customer implementations completed
- Customer retention rate
- Customer satisfaction (CSAT)
- Customer expansion revenue
- Marketing activity and leads
- Support ticket resolution time

**Tiers Evaluated Annually**:
- Promotion if exceeding metrics
- Probation if below metrics
- Termination if continued underperformance
