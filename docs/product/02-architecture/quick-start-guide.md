# Quick Start & Getting Started Guide

## Getting Started in 30 Minutes

### Step 1: Create Your Account (5 minutes)

1. Navigate to login.hdim.com
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a strong password (minimum 12 characters, mixed case, numbers, symbols)
4. Verify your email (check your inbox)
5. You're logged in!

**First Login Experience**:
- Personalization questions (role, organization, focus areas)
- Role-appropriate dashboard customization
- Optional tutorial (recommended for new users)

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile (5 minutes)

1. Click on your name in top right → Profile
2. Add photo (optional)
3. Select your specialty/department
4. Set your timezone
5. Configure notification preferences
6. Save changes

**Notification Preferences**:
- In-platform alerts: Real-time
- Email notifications: Daily digest (customizable)
- SMS alerts: Critical only (opt-in)

### Step 3: Explore Your Dashboard (10 minutes)

**Your Personalized Dashboard Shows**:
- Patient panel summary
- Open care gaps for your patients
- Quality metrics and performance
- Upcoming alerts and tasks
- Recent patient interactions
- Team messages and updates

**Navigation Tips**:
- Click any number to drill into details
- Use filters to narrow results
- Customize dashboard (add/remove widgets)
- Save frequent searches

### Step 4: Take Your First Action (10 minutes)

**Try This Workflow**:
1. Click on "Care Gaps" in left menu
2. Select a patient from the list
3. Click "View Details" to see the gap
4. Review clinical evidence and recommendations
5. Click "Recommend Action"
6. Document your action and save
7. Click "View Dashboard" to see it updated

**Congratulations!** You've completed your first care gap action!

## Key Concepts in 5 Minutes

### What is a Care Gap?
A **care gap** is when a patient doesn't meet a clinical quality measure criteria. Examples:
- Diabetes patient without HbA1c check in past year
- Hypertension patient with BP reading >140/90
- Patient overdue for preventive screening

### What is a Quality Measure?
A **quality measure** is a metric tracking how well healthcare is being delivered. Example:
- Percentage of diabetic patients with controlled HbA1c
- Target: >70% of patients meeting criteria

### What is a Care Plan?
A **care plan** is a comprehensive document outlining patient goals, interventions, and follow-up. It involves:
- Patient and care team input
- Specific goals and timelines
- Assigned interventions
- Scheduled follow-up appointments

### What is a Care Manager?
A **care manager** proactively manages high-risk patients through:
- Regular patient contact and engagement
- Care plan development and monitoring
- Referral management
- Outreach and intervention coordination

## Common Workflows

### Workflow 1: Managing a Care Gap (5 minutes)

1. **Identify**: Review open gaps on your dashboard
2. **Assess**: Click gap to see patient info and clinical data
3. **Plan**: Determine action (treat, refer, educate)
4. **Act**: Document your action in the system
5. **Follow-up**: Schedule review and track outcome
6. **Close**: Mark gap resolved when criteria met

**Expected Time**: 2-3 minutes per gap

### Workflow 2: Creating a Care Plan (15 minutes)

1. **Select Patient**: Click "New Care Plan"
2. **Assessment**: Review patient health status and risks
3. **Goals**: Define SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound)
4. **Interventions**: Add specific care activities and timelines
5. **Team**: Assign team members and responsibilities
6. **Schedule**: Plan follow-up appointments
7. **Share**: Send to team members for collaboration
8. **Monitor**: Review and adjust monthly

**Care Plan Template**: Choose from 20+ condition-specific templates

### Workflow 3: Viewing Your Dashboard (3 minutes)

**Patient Panel Overview**:
- Total patients in your panel
- Number with active care plans
- Number with open care gaps
- Percentage meeting quality criteria

**My Patients**:
- List of all your assigned patients
- Sortable by name, health score, risk level
- Click patient name to see detail

**Open Care Gaps** (by priority):
- RED (Critical): Action needed immediately
- YELLOW (High): Action needed within 1 week
- GREEN (Standard): Action needed within 1 month

**Quality Metrics**:
- Your performance vs benchmark
- Trending over time
- Top improvement opportunities

### Workflow 4: Collaborating with Your Team (5 minutes)

1. **Share Patient Info**: Click patient name → "Share"
2. **Send Message**: Click "Team" tab on patient record
3. **Assign Task**: Click "Create Task" and assign to team member
4. **View Updates**: Team messages appear on patient record
5. **Track Status**: Task status shows completion progress

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How often is my patient data updated?**
A: Data refreshes 4 times daily. Most data available within 2-4 hours of occurrence.

**Q: Can I export patient data?**
A: Yes, use Reports or direct export (respects HIPAA requirements).

**Q: How long does training take?**
A: Role-based training is 4-8 hours depending on your role. Most users productive within 1 week.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page. Reset link sent to your email.

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Call 1-800-XXX-XXXX or email support@hdim.com. Available 24/7 for critical issues.

**Q: Can I customize my dashboard?**
A: Yes, click "Customize" on your dashboard. Add, remove, or rearrange widgets.

**Q: How do I view my team's activity?**
A: Click "Team Dashboard" in left menu. See activity, productivity, and performance.

**Q: How often should I review care gaps?**
A: Daily for high-risk patients, weekly for moderate-risk, monthly for standard-risk.
