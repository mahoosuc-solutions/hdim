# Training & Customer Support Program

## Executive Summary

HealthData in Motion provides **comprehensive training and support services** to ensure successful adoption and long-term value realization. Our customer success model combines proactive education, responsive support, and continuous optimization to drive measurable outcomes.

**Support Commitment**:
- 24/7 critical issue support
- <1 hour response time for critical issues
- Role-based training (in-person and online)
- Ongoing webinars and learning resources
- Dedicated customer success team
- Quarterly business reviews

## Training Programs

### Initial Onboarding Training

**Program Structure**:
- **Pre-Go-Live**: 4-6 weeks before system activation
- **Duration**: 20-40 hours depending on role
- **Format**: Mix of classroom, online, and one-on-one
- **Target**: 100% of users trained before go-live

**Training Components**:

1. **System Overview Training** (2 hours, all users)
   - Product benefits and features
   - How HealthData fits into organizational workflows
   - Key terminology and concepts
   - Security and compliance requirements
   - How to access support resources

2. **Role-Based Classroom Training** (4-8 hours, by role)

   **Physicians/Providers** (6 hours):
   - Patient panel overview and navigation
   - Care gap identification and management
   - Care plan creation and review
   - Patient communication and documentation
   - Quality metrics and performance tracking
   - Hands-on practice with real patient data

   **Care Managers** (8 hours):
   - Patient identification and enrollment
   - Comprehensive assessment and risk evaluation
   - Care plan development and implementation
   - Outreach and engagement workflows
   - Documentation and compliance requirements
   - Gap closure procedures
   - Reporting and metric tracking
   - Hands-on practice with outreach workflows

   **Administrative Users** (6 hours):
   - User management and provisioning
   - System configuration and customization
   - Report generation and scheduling
   - Data quality monitoring
   - Workflow configuration
   - System administration and troubleshooting

   **IT Support Staff** (4 hours):
   - System architecture overview
   - Troubleshooting common issues
   - Password resets and access management
   - Integration and data exchange issues
   - System performance monitoring
   - Escalation procedures

3. **Hands-On Practice Sessions** (4-6 hours)
   - Live system walkthrough with instructor
   - Guided exercises with real patient data
   - Scenario-based practice activities
   - Q&A and discussion

4. **Superuser/Champion Training** (12-16 hours)
   - Deep-dive on all features
   - Administrator-level functions
   - Troubleshooting and problem-solving
   - Train-the-trainer preparation
   - Peer coaching and mentoring skills

**Training Methods**:
- **Classroom**: Groups of 15-20 users (preferred for initial training)
- **Webinars**: Live instructor-led, recordable for asynchronous viewing
- **Virtual One-on-One**: For advanced topics or struggling users
- **Online Self-Paced**: Modules for flexible learning

**Training Materials Provided**:
- Participant workbooks with activities
- Video tutorials (30-60 min per role)
- Quick reference job aids (laminated cards)
- FAQ documents and troubleshooting guides
- Glossary of terms and acronyms
- Workflow flowcharts and process maps

## Customer Support Model

### Support Tiers & SLAs

**Critical Issues** (System down, patient safety risk):
- **Response SLA**: 15 minutes
- **Resolution SLA**: 4 hours
- **Support Level**: VP+ escalation authority
- **Availability**: 24/7

**High Issues** (Functionality significantly impaired, workaround unclear):
- **Response SLA**: 1 hour
- **Resolution SLA**: 8 hours
- **Support Level**: Senior support engineer
- **Availability**: Business hours + on-call

**Medium Issues** (Functionality impaired but workaround available):
- **Response SLA**: 4 hours
- **Resolution SLA**: 1-2 business days
- **Support Level**: Support specialist
- **Availability**: Business hours

**Low Issues** (Minor bugs, questions, feature requests):
- **Response SLA**: 1 business day
- **Resolution SLA**: 5 business days
- **Support Level**: Support team member
- **Availability**: Business hours

### Support Channels

**Phone Support**:
- Toll-free number (1-800-XXX-XXXX)
- Available 24/7 for critical issues
- Business hours for non-critical issues
- Average hold time: <2 minutes
- Direct routing to specialists by queue

**Email Support**:
- support@hdim.com
- Tracked in ticketing system
- Auto-response with ticket number
- Response within SLA timeframe

**Online Ticketing**:
- Web-based portal for submitting tickets
- Self-service for password resets, known issues
- Ticket status tracking
- Integrated knowledge base search

**Live Chat**:
- In-app chat during business hours
- Average wait time: <5 minutes
- Agent can view your screen (with permission)
- Chat history available in account

**Knowledge Base**:
- 500+ searchable articles
- Search engine and category browsing
- Video tutorials (100+ videos)
- Frequently asked questions
- Troubleshooting guides
- Available 24/7

### Support Team Structure

**Tier 1: Support Specialists** (Front-line support)
- Handle common questions and known issues
- Password resets and access issues
- Training and product education
- Ticket categorization and routing
- Resolution time: 24 hours for 60% of issues

**Tier 2: Support Engineers** (Complex issues)
- Database and configuration issues
- Integration and data exchange problems
- System performance and optimization
- Custom report and dashboard issues
- Resolution time: 24-48 hours for 80% of issues

**Tier 3: Product Engineering** (Development team)
- Software bugs and defects
- Architecture and design issues
- Complex customization requests
- Performance optimization
- Resolution time: 48-72 hours or backlog

**Customer Success Manager** (Proactive support)
- Assigned to each customer
- Quarterly business reviews
- Adoption tracking and coaching
- Performance trending
- Optimization recommendations
- Early access to new features

### Issue Resolution Process

**Step 1: Intake & Categorization** (0-15 min)
- Gather basic information
- Categorize by severity
- Assign severity level
- Route to appropriate team

**Step 2: Diagnosis** (varies by issue)
- Reproduce the issue
- Gather diagnostic data
- Review logs and system state
- Identify root cause

**Step 3: Resolution** (varies by issue)
- Implement fix (configuration, bug patch, workaround)
- Verify resolution with customer
- Document solution
- Close ticket

**Step 4: Verification & Closure**
- Confirm issue is resolved
- Schedule any necessary follow-up
- Add to knowledge base if applicable
- Request satisfaction rating

**Step 5: Follow-Up** (within 1 week)
- Confirm long-term resolution
- Check for recurrence
- Offer optimization recommendations

### Escalation Procedures

**Escalation Triggers**:
- Issue not resolved within SLA
- Customer satisfaction rating <3/5
- Impact to critical business process
- Patient safety concern
- Data integrity or security issue

**Escalation Path**:
1. **Tier 1 → Tier 2**: Escalate at 24 hours
2. **Tier 2 → Tier 3**: Escalate at 48 hours or if engineering-level issue
3. **Tier 3 → VP Customer Success**: Escalate at 72 hours
4. **VP → VP Product**: Escalate for critical or urgent issues
5. **VP Product → Executive Sponsor**: Escalate for business-critical issues
