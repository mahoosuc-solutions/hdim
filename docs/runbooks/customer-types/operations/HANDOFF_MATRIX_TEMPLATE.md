# Handoff Matrix Template

Purpose: formalize cross-functional handoffs to avoid dropped ownership.

## Handoff Table
| From Function | To Function | Trigger | Input Artifact | Output Artifact | SLA |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sales | Solution Architecture | Discovery complete | Discovery summary | Architecture assumptions | 1 business day |
| Solution Architecture | Engineering | Topology and controls approved | Deployment decision + architecture notes | Integration plan | 1 business day |
| Engineering | Customer Success | UAT complete | UAT evidence | Go-live readiness brief | 1 business day |
| Customer Success | Executive Sponsors | Weekly steering complete | Weekly update | Decision log and escalations | 1 business day |

## Escalation Rule
- If SLA is missed, escalate to functional leads within 24 hours.
