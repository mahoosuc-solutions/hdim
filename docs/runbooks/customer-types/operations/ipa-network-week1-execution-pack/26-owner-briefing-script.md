# Owner Briefing Script (10 Minutes)

Purpose: align internal owners before customer workshop.

## Opening (1 minute)
"This week we must clear G1 by end of Week 1. Our focus is deployment decision, security ownership, and complete evidence."

## Role Call and Commitments (4 minutes)

1. Sales Lead
- Confirm customer security approver and backup.
- Ensure procurement and sponsor are in workshop.

2. Solution Architect
- Lead workshop and decision capture.
- Own G1 evidence completion and status updates.

3. Engineering Lead
- Prepare interface inventory and integration assumptions.
- Flag technical blockers quickly.

4. Customer Success Lead
- Drive action log, steering cadence, and communication follow-through.

## Today’s Critical Actions (3 minutes)

1. Send workshop invite and pre-read.
2. Confirm attendee list and decision owners.
3. Update gate status and open items log by end of day.

## Close (2 minutes)
"No kickoff until G1 is PASS or approved exception is documented. We will run daily command-center checks until closure."
