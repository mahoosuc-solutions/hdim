# Week 2 Transition Plan

Purpose: move from Week 1 decisioning into controlled execution for G2/G3.

## Entry Criteria (from Week 1)

- [ ] G1 is PASS, or Conditional Go with approved exception and dated remediation.
- [ ] Deployment model decision is documented.
- [ ] Security approver and backup are confirmed.
- [ ] Owner roster, action log, and risk register are current.

## Week 2 Objectives

1. Complete integration readiness and UAT scope definition (G2 path).
2. Finalize pilot operations readiness and governance rhythm (G3 path).
3. Freeze baseline KPI definitions and data ownership.

## G2 Workstream (Integration + UAT)

- [ ] Finalize source-system interface inventory.
- [ ] Complete mapping of required pilot data elements.
- [ ] Confirm test payload exchange success.
- [ ] Approve UAT acceptance criteria and test owners.

Outputs:

- Updated `02-gate-status-snapshot.md`
- Updated `04-action-log.md`
- UAT criteria attached to gate evidence package

## G3 Workstream (Pilot Readiness)

- [ ] Confirm weekly steering attendees and recurring cadence.
- [ ] Confirm escalation path ownership and SLA.
- [ ] Confirm KPI baseline values and reporting owner.
- [ ] Confirm first 2 weeks of steering agenda.

Outputs:

- Updated `06-weekly-steering-update-week1.md` (or Week 2 version)
- Updated `08-runbook-readiness-memo.md`

## Week 2 Exit Criteria

- [ ] G2 is PASS or AT RISK with approved remediation plan.
- [ ] G3 is PASS or AT RISK with approved remediation plan.
- [ ] No unresolved blocker without named owner and due date.
