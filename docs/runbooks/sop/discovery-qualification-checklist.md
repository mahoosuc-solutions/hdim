# SOP: Discovery and Qualification Checklist

## Objective
Confirm customer fit, pilot feasibility, and sponsor alignment.

## Checklist
- [ ] Confirm customer type (MA/ACO/IPA)
- [ ] Confirm pilot size (200-500 discharges for MA transitions pilot)
- [ ] Confirm executive sponsor and clinical champion
- [ ] Confirm baseline KPI availability
- [ ] Confirm legal/compliance participation
- [ ] Confirm decision timeline and contracting path

## Outputs
- Discovery summary
- Stakeholder map
- Initial risk list
