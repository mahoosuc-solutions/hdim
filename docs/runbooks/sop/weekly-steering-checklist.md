# SOP: Weekly Steering Checklist

## Objective
Run a consistent weekly governance cadence across all pilot customers.

## Checklist
- [ ] Publish KPI snapshot
- [ ] Review prior week decisions and actions
- [ ] Review risk register deltas
- [ ] Confirm blockers and owners
- [ ] Confirm decisions due this week
- [ ] Capture meeting record and distribute

## Outputs
- Weekly steering update
- Updated risk register
- Action log
