1. Fixed implementation and pilot fee at contracted scope.

1. Customer provides timely access to integration endpoints and SMEs.
2. Pilot cohort and KPI definitions are jointly finalized in Week 1.
3. Epic MyChart write-back is excluded from Phase 1 build scope.
4. Material legal/compliance changes may require re-estimation.
