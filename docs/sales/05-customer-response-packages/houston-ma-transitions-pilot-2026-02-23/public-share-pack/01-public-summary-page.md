# AI Care Transitions Pilot - Public Summary

## What This Is

A 90-day pilot model for Medicare Advantage and risk-bearing organizations focused on improving post-discharge patient engagement and understanding.

## Core Problem

Patients often leave acute care with unresolved confusion about medications, follow-up steps, and warning signs. This creates avoidable engagement failures and care-transition risk.

## Proposed Solution

1. Context-aware, plain-language patient guidance.
2. Secure escalation workflow for care-team follow-up.
3. Weekly KPI governance focused on measurable outcomes.

## Security and Trust Position

1. Minimum-necessary PHI handling.
2. Zero-retention AI pattern for request-level processing.
3. Tenant-aware controls and auditability.

## Deployment Models

1. Vendor-hosted.
2. Customer-hosted cloud.
3. On-prem/private data center (including air-gapped variant).

## Pilot Envelope

1. Duration: 90 days.
2. Typical discharge cohort: 200-500 cases.
3. Focus KPIs: engagement, comprehension, response time, escalation closure.

## Commercial Range (High-Level)

1. Hosted baseline pilot envelope: approximately $277,000-$383,000 total.
2. Customer-cloud and on-prem variants add cost and timeline based on control/operating requirements.
