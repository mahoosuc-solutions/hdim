# Public Pilot Brief

## Pilot Objective

Demonstrate measurable improvement in post-discharge patient engagement and comprehension using secure AI-assisted communication workflows.

## Scope (Public)

In scope:

1. Patient-facing plain-language summaries and guidance.
2. Secure escalation to care teams.
3. KPI instrumentation and weekly governance.

Out of scope (Phase 1):

1. Broad enterprise rollouts beyond pilot cohort.
2. Production-grade bidirectional EHR write-back.

## Success Metrics

1. Improvement in 7-day engagement.
2. Improvement in comprehension confirmation.
3. Faster first-response and escalation closure times.

## Delivery Pattern

1. Week 1: architecture/security workshop and decision gate.
2. Weeks 2-3: integration and readiness validation.
3. Weeks 4-10: pilot operation and weekly optimization.
4. Weeks 11-13: outcomes synthesis and scale recommendation.

## Governance

1. Weekly cross-functional steering.
2. Gate-based progression with evidence.
3. Clear escalation ownership.

## Commercial Summary (Public)

1. Hosted baseline pilot envelope: $277,000-$383,000.
2. Alternate deployment models priced with additive adjustments.

## Intended Audience

1. Health plan and provider executives.
2. Clinical operations leaders.
3. IT/security and procurement stakeholders.
