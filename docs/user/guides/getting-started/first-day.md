## Step 1: Receive Your Credentials (5 minutes)

### What You'll Get
Your HealthData in Motion account includes:
- **Email address**: Your work email (used for login)
- **Temporary password**: Sent to your email
- **Portal URL**: https://portal.healthdata.com
- **Quick start guide**: PDF with key resources

### Check Your Email
1. Look for email from "HealthData Support" (support@healthdata.com)
2. Subject line: "Welcome to HealthData in Motion"
3. Email contains:
   - Portal login URL
   - Temporary password
   - Quick start checklist
   - IT support contact information

**Not receiving email?** [Contact IT Support](#getting-help)

---

## Step 2: Log In & Change Your Password (10 minutes)

### First-Time Login

**Go to**: https://portal.healthdata.com

**Enter**:
- **Email**: Your work email address
- **Password**: Temporary password from email

**Click**: "Sign In"

### Change Your Password (Required)

You'll be prompted to change your temporary password on first login.

**Create a strong password**:
- At least 12 characters
- Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Unique (not used on other accounts)
- Example: `HealthData2025!Secure`

**Password Tips**:
- ✓ Use a passphrase (easier to remember and secure)
- ✓ Use a password manager (LastPass, 1Password, Bitwarden)
- ✗ Don't use: birthdate, patient names, simple patterns
- ✗ Don't share: with anyone, in emails, in text messages

### Save Your Login Credentials

Store your login information securely:
- **Password manager** (recommended)
- **Secure note** (never in email or chat)
- **Remember this device** (only on secure, personal device)

**Bookmark the login URL**: Save https://portal.healthdata.com in your browser

## Step 3: Complete Your Profile (5 minutes)

After first login, you'll see the **"Complete Your Profile"** prompt.

### Profile Information to Update

1. **Full Name** (already filled from directory)
2. **Phone Number**
   - Preferred contact number
   - Format: (555) 555-5555
3. **Specialty** (if applicable for clinical staff)
   - Family Medicine, Internal Medicine, Cardiology, etc.
   - Used for care team assignment
4. **Location/Department**
   - Where you primarily work
   - Used for team organization
5. **Manager** (if applicable)
   - Your direct supervisor
   - Used for performance reporting
6. **Photo** (optional)
   - Click "Upload Photo" to add professional headshot
   - Helps team members recognize you

### Communication Preferences

**Email Notifications**: Choose what you receive emails about
- **Daily Summary**: Overview of important items (recommended for most)
- **Immediate Alerts**: Urgent items only (recommended for clinical staff)
- **Weekly Digest**: Summary of the week (optional)

**System Notifications**: In-app notifications
- **On**: Pop-up alerts for important items
- **Off**: Only view when you log in

**Recommend**: Enable daily summary + system notifications for best experience

## Common First-Day Questions

### Q: I can't remember my password
**A**: Click **"Forgot Password"** on login page
- Enter your email
- Click link in password reset email
- Create new password
- Log in with new password

### Q: I'm not seeing my patients in the system
**A**: Your patient population is assigned by administrators
- Contact your manager to confirm patient assignment
- Patients appear within 1-2 hours of assignment
- Refresh your browser if already logged in

### Q: Where do I find the button for [specific task]?
**A**: Use the **Search Help** feature:
- Click **"Help"** (bottom left)
- Type what you're looking for
- Browse results
- Contact support if you need more assistance

### Q: How do I change my password?
**A**: Click your **profile icon** (top right)
- Select **"Account Settings"**
- Click **"Change Password"**
- Enter current and new password
- Click **"Update"**

### Q: Can I access the platform from my phone?
**A**: Yes, use the mobile app
- Download "HealthData" from App Store or Google Play
- Log in with your credentials
- Mobile features are role-specific
- Contact IT support for mobile access issues
