## Overview: The 5 User Roles

HealthData in Motion has 5 main user roles. Your role determines:
- **What you see**: Available menus and features
- **What you can do**: Available actions and workflows
- **What data you can access**: Patient information visibility
- **Your responsibilities**: Assigned tasks and workflows

### Quick Role Summary

| Role | Primary Responsibility | Team % | Key Features |
|------|----------------------|--------|--------------|
| **Physician** | Clinical care decisions | 25% | Patient dashboards, care gaps, quality metrics |
| **Care Manager** | Care coordination | 25% | Patient lists, care plans, outreach |
| **Clinical Staff** | Care delivery support | 20% | Task management, documentation, alerts |
| **Administrator** | Team oversight | 15% | Reports, user management, data quality |
| **IT Support** | System operations | 15% | Configuration, monitoring, integration |

## Role 1: Physician/Provider

**Primary Focus**: Clinical decision-making and patient care quality

### Your Responsibilities

- **Clinical Decisions**: Reviewing care gaps and ordering care
- **Quality Metrics**: Understanding and improving quality performance
- **Patient Management**: Managing your patient panel
- **Care Coordination**: Working with care team
- **Documentation**: Recording clinical decisions and outcomes

### Your Key Features

**Dashboard**:
- 📊 Patient alerts and critical items
- 📈 Your quality metrics
- 👥 Your patient panel summary
- ⚠️ Clinical escalations

**Patient Management**:
- 🔍 Search and view your patients
- 📋 Patient history and encounters
- 💊 Medications and allergies
- 🧪 Lab results and imaging
- 📅 Upcoming appointments

**Care Gap Management**:
- 📌 View assigned care gaps
- ✅ Document gap closure (treat, refer, educate)
- 📊 See gap closure tracking
- 🎯 View quality improvement metrics

**Quality Metrics**:
- 📈 Your performance dashboard
- 🎯 Metrics vs. targets
- 🏆 Benchmarking vs. peers
- 💡 Improvement opportunities

**Clinical Features**:
- ⚡ Clinical alerts (safety warnings)
- 📝 Clinical documentation
- 💬 Secure messaging with team
- 📞 Schedule callbacks/follow-ups

### What You CANNOT Do

- ❌ Add/remove users
- ❌ View other physicians' data (unless care coordination)
- ❌ Change system settings
- ❌ Access administrative reports
- ❌ Modify user permissions

### Privacy & Security

- You can only see your own patients
- You can see shared patients (care team)
- You cannot see other physicians' patients
- All access is logged for compliance
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest

**HIPAA Compliance**: Viewing is restricted to patients in your care. Violating this could result in account suspension.

## Checking Your Role

### Find Your Role in Settings

1. Click your **profile icon** (top right)
2. Select **"Account Settings"**
3. See your **"Role"** listed
4. See **"Department"** and **"Manager"**

### Your Role Determines

- **Left Menu Items**: You see features for your role
- **Available Actions**: Buttons and options you can use
- **Data Access**: Patients and information you can see
- **Reports Available**: Reports for your role

### Role-Specific Menu

**In Left Sidebar**:
- Each role has different menu items
- Menu items match your responsibilities
- Items change if your role changes
- Contact admin if menu seems wrong

## Requesting Access to New Features

### Your Feature Is Hidden

**Possible Reasons**:
- Feature requires different role
- Feature not enabled for your organization
- You don't have permission yet
- Feature is being rolled out gradually

### Request Access

1. Click **"Help"** (bottom left)
2. Select **"Request Access"**
3. Choose feature
4. Provide business justification
5. Submit request

**Process**:
- Your request goes to your manager
- Manager reviews and approves/denies
- Admin implements if approved
- You're notified when complete
- Timeline: 1-3 business days

### Escalate Request

If denied:
1. Discuss with your manager
2. Manager can escalate to IT director
3. IT director reviews business case
4. Final decision made by director
5. You're notified of outcome

## What Happens When Your Role Changes

### Role Change Process

1. **Notification**: You receive email of role change
2. **Immediate**: New role takes effect
3. **Access**: New menu items appear, old ones disappear
4. **Training**: You receive training for new role
5. **Questions**: Contact IT support for questions

### Access Removal

When moving roles:
- **Old data**: Still exists but not visible
- **Old access**: Removed immediately
- **Patient data**: Restricted appropriately
- **Audit trail**: Preserved for compliance

## Permissions FAQ

### Q: Can I see another provider's patients?
**A**: Only if:
- You're on the shared care team
- Patient is referred to your department
- Otherwise, no - access is restricted

### Q: Can I download all patient data?
**A**: Depends on role:
- Physicians: Your patients (via reports)
- Care Managers: Your patient list
- Admins: Team data (no individual clinical records)
- Downloads are encrypted and tracked

### Q: What if I need access I don't have?
**A**: Follow the access request process above:
- Click Help → Request Access
- Manager approves
- Admin implements (1-3 days)

### Q: Is my access logged?
**A**: Yes, completely:
- Every login is recorded
- Every patient view is logged
- All downloads are tracked
- All admin actions are audited
- Logs kept for 7 years per HIPAA

### Q: What if I access data I shouldn't?
**A**:
- Unauthorized access is audited
- First violation: warning + retraining
- Repeat: suspension or termination
- Serious: legal action
- Compliance is required
