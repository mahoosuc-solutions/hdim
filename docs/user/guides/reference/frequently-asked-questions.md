# Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about the platform.

## Getting Started

**Q: How do I log in for the first time?**
A: Use your email and temporary password from IT. System prompts you to create new password. Set up multi-factor authentication if required.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page. Enter email, check email for reset link, create new password.

**Q: How do I change my notification settings?**
A: Go to Settings > Notifications. Toggle notification types on/off and set quiet hours.

**Q: How do I see which patients are assigned to me?**
A: Go to Patients > My Panel. Lists all assigned patients with status indicators.

## Patient Management

**Q: How do I find a specific patient?**
A: Use search at top of page. Search by name, MRN, date of birth, or phone number.

**Q: How do I view patient's complete health record?**
A: Click patient name > Health Overview. Shows summary of medications, conditions, appointments, labs.

**Q: Can I see if a patient is assigned to another provider?**
A: Yes. Patient card shows primary provider and care team members assigned.

**Q: How do I request access to a patient not currently assigned?**
A: Contact your supervisor. They can assign patient or update permissions.

**Q: How often does patient data update?**
A: EHR data syncs every 1-4 hours typically. Use refresh to see most recent.

## Care Gaps and Quality Measures

**Q: What does a care gap mean?**
A: Patient missing a recommended care, preventive service, or treatment per quality standards.

**Q: How do I close a care gap?**
A: Perform the recommended care (screening, medication, referral). Platform tracks and closes automatically when detected.

**Q: Can I mark a gap as "will not address"?**
A: Yes. Document reason (patient declined, contraindicated, etc.). Requires supervisor approval for some gaps.

**Q: What if my quality metrics aren't improving?**
A: Review patient workflows, ensure timely documentation, verify data accuracy, and follow best practices guidance.

**Q: How are quality scores calculated?**
A: Scores based on HEDIS, preventive care, chronic disease management standards. See Quality Metrics guide for details.

## Orders and Referrals

**Q: How do I order a lab test?**
A: Go to patient > Orders > New Lab. Select tests, enter special instructions if needed, verify patient info, submit.

**Q: How long does a lab result take?**
A: Most results 24-72 hours. Some tests longer. Contact lab directly if significantly delayed.

**Q: How do I make a specialist referral?**
A: Go to patient > Referrals > New Referral. Select specialty, enter clinical information, choose target specialist or group, submit.

**Q: Can I order imaging (X-ray, CT, MRI)?**
A: Yes. Orders > New Imaging. Follow same process as lab orders.

**Q: How do I track order status?**
A: Orders tab shows all submitted orders with status (pending, completed, reviewed, etc.).

## Reports and Analytics

**Q: How do I generate a report?**
A: Go to Reports > New Report. Select report type, set filters and date range, click Generate.

**Q: Can I export report data?**
A: Yes. Report page has Export button. Choose format (Excel, CSV, PDF) and download.

**Q: How do I schedule a regular report?**
A: Report page > Schedule. Set frequency (daily, weekly, monthly) and recipients.

**Q: Why is my report showing no data?**
A: Check date range, verify filters applied, clear filters to see all data. See Report Generation Issues guide.

**Q: Can I compare performance with other providers?**
A: Yes. Scorecard dashboards show benchmarking against peer providers and organization averages.

## Technical and Access

**Q: Why can't I access a certain feature?**
A: Feature requires specific role/permissions. Contact supervisor or IT to request access.

**Q: What if I forget my login information?**
A: Use "Forgot Password" on login page. If still stuck, contact IT.

**Q: Can I use the platform on my phone?**
A: Yes. Download mobile app from App Store or Play Store. Works iOS and Android.

**Q: Is the platform accessible from outside the organization?**
A: Yes. Secure login from any location with internet and approved device.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions). Avoid Internet Explorer.

## Communication and Collaboration

**Q: How do I message a colleague?**
A: Click message icon or colleague name > Send Message. Messages encrypted and HIPAA-secure.

**Q: Can I share patient information with patients?**
A: Depends on organization policy. Some systems allow patient portal access to visit notes and records.

**Q: How do I assign a task to someone?**
A: Click task or patient > Assign Task. Select person and due date.

**Q: How do I know if someone assigned me a task?**
A: Notification email sent and task appears in Tasks inbox. Check Notifications settings.

**Q: Can I schedule a video call with a patient?**
A: Some systems integrated with telehealth. Check with your IT team.

## Data and Quality

**Q: Why is there a discrepancy between EHR and platform?**
A: EHR is source system. Platform syncs every 1-4 hours. If discrepancy persists, contact IT.

**Q: Can I edit patient demographics?**
A: Edits made in EHR first, then sync to platform. Don't edit in platform.

**Q: What if I find a duplicate patient record?**
A: Report to IT with both patient MRNs. IT will merge records.

**Q: How do I correct incorrect patient information?**
A: Correct in EHR (source system). Platform will sync correction within 1-4 hours.

**Q: What if patient data is missing?**
A: Check if data exists in EHR. If in EHR but not synced, contact IT. If not in EHR, add there first.

## Best Practices

**Q: When should I use the mobile app vs. web?**
A: Mobile for quick lookups on rounds. Web for detailed work, documentation, complex tasks.

**Q: How often should I refresh my view?**
A: Every 2-4 hours during work day to see latest data.

**Q: What's the best way to organize my workflow?**
A: See Workflows guides for your role. Follow same process consistently.

**Q: How do I ensure data quality in my entries?**
A: Double-check before saving. Use standard formats. Correct errors promptly. Review entries weekly.

**Q: When should I contact IT vs. my supervisor?**
A: Supervisor for access requests and role questions. IT for technical problems and system errors.
