## Scene-by-Scene Script

### Scene 1: The Problem (0:00 - 0:30)

**Visual:** Start with a simple text slide or statistics graphic

**On-Screen Text:**
```
40% of quality measure opportunities are missed every year

Each missed screening is a patient at risk
Each overdue test is a care gap

These aren't just statistics. They're patients falling through the cracks.
```

**Voiceover:**
> "Healthcare organizations miss 40% of quality measure opportunities every year. Each missed screening, each overdue test represents a patient at risk—and lost quality bonuses worth millions.
>
> For Maria Garcia, a 57-year-old woman, her colonoscopy was 4 years overdue. For Robert Chen, uncontrolled diabetes was silently progressing. These aren't just statistics. They're patients falling through the cracks."

**Action:** Transition to Clinical Portal

---

### Scene 2: Meet the Patients (0:30 - 1:00)

**Visual:** Clinical Portal → Patients page

**Actions:**
1. Click on "Patients" in navigation
2. Show patient list with 5 demo patients visible
3. Click on Maria Garcia to view details
4. Highlight her care gap indicator

**Voiceover:**
> "HDIM gives you instant visibility into every patient's care status. Here's our patient list with real clinical data.
>
> Let's look at Maria Garcia—57 years old, her last colonoscopy was in 2019. That's over 4 years ago. Her colorectal cancer screening is HIGH priority.
>
> Without a system like HDIM, Maria would be invisible. One of thousands of patients quietly falling out of compliance."

**Key Moments to Capture:**
- Patient list showing all 5 demo patients
- Maria Garcia's profile with care gap badge
- Date of last colonoscopy visible

---

### Scene 3: Gap Detection Dashboard (1:00 - 1:30)

**Visual:** Care Gaps page

**Actions:**
1. Click "Care Gaps" in navigation
2. Show the dashboard with summary cards (Total, High, Medium, Low urgency)
3. Show the intervention recommendations panel
4. Filter by "High Urgency"

**Voiceover:**
> "Our care gap dashboard surfaces every patient who needs attention—automatically. No hunting through spreadsheets. No manual chart reviews.
>
> Right now we have 13 care gaps across our patient population. 6 are HIGH urgency—patients like Maria who need immediate outreach.
>
> And look at these intervention recommendations—each one shows proven success rates and ROI. Care coordinator calls close 67% of gaps. Provider alerts are free and close 48%."

**Key Moments to Capture:**
- Summary statistics (13 total, 6 high, 5 medium, 2 low)
- Intervention cards with success rates (67%, 48%, 32%, 28%)
- Filter controls

### Scene 4: Take Action - The Intervention (1:30 - 2:15)

**Visual:** Care gap row → Actions menu

**Actions:**
1. Find Maria Garcia's row in the care gap table
2. Click the "More actions" menu (three dots)
3. Show intervention options
4. Click to log an intervention (if dialog available)
5. Show the intervention types with ROI metrics

**Voiceover:**
> "One click to take action. See Maria's colonoscopy gap here—HIGH urgency, 4 years overdue.
>
> I can send an outreach letter... schedule a care coordinator call... or alert her primary care provider directly. Each intervention has proven success rates—care coordinator calls close 67% of gaps with a 5.8x ROI.
>
> For Maria, we sent a letter, followed up with a call, and scheduled her colonoscopy. The system tracks every touchpoint."

**Key Moments to Capture:**
- Maria's row highlighted with COL gap
- Actions menu or intervention dialog
- ROI metrics visible

**Voiceover:**

>
> Look at Angela Williams—her mammogram gap was 28 months overdue. After our outreach, she got screened... and we detected early-stage breast cancer. Stage 1A, highly treatable, excellent prognosis. Without closing that gap, her cancer could have progressed for months.
>
> Robert Chen? We closed 3 diabetes care gaps in 4 months—HbA1c control, eye exam, and nephropathy screening. His eye exam actually caught early diabetic retinopathy. Early detection. Better outcomes."

**Key Moments to Capture:**
- Angela Williams with CLOSED BCS gap

## Recording Workflow

1. **Prep (15 min)**
   - Run `scripts/create-demo-patients-historical.sh`
   - Verify patients appear in Clinical Portal
   - Log in, arrange windows

2. **Record Screen (10 min)**
   - Follow scene-by-scene guide
   - Capture each scene as separate clip
   - Don't worry about mistakes—re-record individual scenes

3. **Record Voiceover (10 min)**
   - Read script naturally, conversational tone
   - Match timing to screen actions
   - Record in quiet environment

4. **Edit (30 min)**
   - Sync voiceover to screen recording
   - Add transitions between scenes
   - Add text overlays for key stats
   - Add music bed
   - Export at 1080p

5. **Review & Publish**
   - Watch full video
   - Check audio levels
   - Verify all text is readable
   - Export final version
