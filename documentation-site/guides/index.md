# Role-Based Guides

Welcome to the HDIM Clinical Portal role-based guides. Each guide is tailored to your specific role and responsibilities within the healthcare quality improvement workflow.

## Available Guides

<div class="role-cards">

### [Provider Guide](./provider.md)
For **Physicians, NPs, and PAs** who need to:
- Review patient quality metrics during visits
- Address care gaps at point of care
- Monitor quality measure performance
- Sign off on clinical results

### [RN Guide](./rn.md)
For **Registered Nurses** who need to:
- Coordinate patient outreach campaigns
- Manage care gap workflows
- Monitor population health metrics
- Support quality improvement initiatives

### [MA Guide](./ma.md)
For **Medical Assistants** who need to:
- Prepare patients for visits
- Document interventions
- Schedule follow-up appointments
- Support care gap closure

### [Administrator Guide](./admin.md)
For **System Administrators** who need to:
- Manage users and permissions
- Configure quality measures
- Monitor system health
- Generate compliance reports

### [Quality Analyst Guide](./quality-analyst.md)
For **Quality Analysts** who need to:
- Run population evaluations
- Generate quality reports
- Track compliance trends
- Configure measure parameters

</div>

## Getting Started

### First-Time Login

1. Navigate to your organization's Clinical Portal URL
2. Enter your username and password
3. Select your organization (if applicable)
4. Complete multi-factor authentication (if enabled)

### Navigating the Portal

The main navigation includes:

- **Dashboard** - Role-specific metrics and tasks
- **Patients** - Patient list and search
- **Evaluations** - Quality measure evaluations
- **Care Gaps** - Care gap management
- **Reports** - Quality reports
- **Admin** - System configuration (Admin only)

### Common Tasks

Regardless of role, all users can:

1. **Search for patients** using the global search bar
2. **View patient details** including demographics, conditions, and medications
3. **Run quality evaluations** for individual patients
4. **Export data** in CSV or Excel format
