## Platform Overview

The HDIM Clinical Portal is a comprehensive healthcare quality management platform designed to help healthcare organizations:

- **Improve Quality Scores** - Track and improve HEDIS, MIPS, and custom quality measures
- **Close Care Gaps** - Identify and resolve patient care gaps efficiently
- **Streamline Workflows** - Role-specific dashboards and workflows for clinical teams
- **Demonstrate Value** - Generate reports for payers, regulators, and stakeholders
