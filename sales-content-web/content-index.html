🎯 Sales Motion by Prospect Type

Medical Director / CMO Path

Decision Driver: Quality metrics (Star rating, HEDIS scores) and reputation
  • Week 1: Send One-Pager (focus on Star rating improvement)
  • Week 2: Email #2-3 (competitive pressure + gap closure impact)
  • Week 3: Working session with Case Study (real quality metrics improvement)
  • Week 4: ROI calculator showing quality bonus revenue impact
  • Close: Emphasize clinical outcomes, Star rating, provider relationships

Finance / CFO Path

Decision Driver: ROI, payback period, financial return
  • Week 1: Email #1 (hook with $250K+ opportunity)
  • Week 2: Email #5 (detailed ROI with transparent assumptions)
  • Week 3: Working session with ROI Calculator (customize for org size)
  • Week 4: Proposal with 3-year financial model
  • Close: Emphasize net benefit, payback period, conservative assumptions

Compliance / Chief Privacy Officer Path

Decision Driver: Compliance, audit readiness, policy enforcement
  • Week 1: One-Pager (highlight compliance/audit benefits)
  • Week 2: Email #2 (risk/compliance angle, audit efficiency)
  • Week 3: Working session (deep dive on policy enforcement, audit trail)
  • Week 4: Case Study (compliance/audit section)
  • Close: Emphasize zero-trust compliance, policy enforcement, audit proof

IT / CIO Path

Decision Driver: Integration effort, data governance, scalability
  • Week 1: Blog: FHIR-Native Architecture (technical credibility)
  • Week 2: Invite to webinar Segment 3 replay (automation architecture + dashboards)
  • Week 3: Technical deep dive (integration checklist, security posture)
  • Week 4: Implementation Summary + ROI calculator (IT resourcing + payback)
  • Close: Emphasize API-first build, low-lift deployment, governance controls

Care Coordination Director Path

Decision Driver: Operational efficiency, coordinator time/satisfaction
  • Week 1: One-Pager (emphasize time savings, workflow integration)
  • Week 2: Share webinar replay chapters covering automation + coordinator task lists
  • Week 3: Working session with product demo (workflow walkthrough)
  • Week 4: Case Study (coordinator quote and time savings data)
  • Close: Emphasize labor savings, coordinator adoption, workflow simplicity

✅ Team Preparation Checklist

Sales Team

  • Read Clinical Sales Strategy (understand why)
  • Memorize 30-second pitch from Quick Reference
  • Print laminated copy of Quick Reference for desk
  • Review all 11 discovery questions
  • Read objection responses (7 common)
  • Work through Sales Training Toolkit battle cards + drills
  • Review Case Study for proof points
  • Participate in team training session

Marketing Team

  • Configure Email Sequence in marketing automation
  • Load templates from Email Template Library into campaigns/cadences
  • Create test sends to verify template rendering
  • Set up gated landing pages for downloads
  • Configure tracking (UTM parameters, tracking pixels)
  • Set up ROI calculator deployment
  • Establish reporting dashboard for metrics
  • Plan content roadmap (video, additional assets)
  • Follow Marketing Implementation Guide for 90-day cadence

Leadership

  • Review and validate ROI model assumptions
  • Approve messaging and positioning
  • Read Marketing Completion Summary for program status
  • Set expectations for sales cycle and close rate
  • Brief team on importance and commitment
  • Monitor metrics monthly (opens, demos, closes)
  • Support asset adoption and team training
