📋 About This Suite

A complete, production-ready sales content suite specifically designed to sell Health Data In Motion to busy clinical users. Everything you need to close deals—from initial outreach to proposal.

✓ What's Included

21 comprehensive documents + webinar assets + 4 thought leadership blogs: Sales strategy, case study, one-pager, email sequence, quick reference playbook, 2 specialized collateral (mental health + risk stratification), webinar outline & speaker plan, sales training toolkit, 15-template email library, full webinar speaker notes, marketing implementation guide, completion summary, plus 4 production-ready blog posts targeting CMOs, CIOs, COOs, and clinical leaders. All ready to deploy immediately.

💡 Key Insight

Busy clinical users care about one thing: time and money. Our messaging focuses on freeing 2-3 hours/week per care coordinator and generating $300K+ annual revenue. Everything flows from that value prop.

Investor Library Deployment Checklist

Sync the new investor pages to production once reviewed:

  1. Run npm run build to ensure static assets compile cleanly.
  2. Execute ./deploy-production.sh sales-content-web to publish the refreshed HTML bundle.
  3. Spot-check /investor-overview, /investor-metrics-factsheet, and /investor-faq routes on the CDN before notifying investors.

If you only need to stage the changes, swap in deploy-rhel7.sh or docker-compose.local.yml as appropriate.
