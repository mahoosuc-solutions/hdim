Investor Overview – Health Data In Motion

Date: November 20, 2025
Stage: Late Seed / pre-Series A
Purpose: Equip investors with a concise narrative covering vision, traction, unit economics, and near-term growth priorities.

1. Vision & Mission

Health Data In Motion (HDIM) turns raw clinical data into real-time quality and revenue insights so care teams close more gaps with less manual work. We believe quality measurement should be autonomous, not retrospective. Our platform combines a FHIR-native data layer with care coordination workflows so every eligible patient is screened, routed, and documented in under five minutes.

Why now:

  • CMS is tying 5-8% of reimbursement to quality performance and mental health integration.

  • Health systems face a staffing crisis; coordinators spend 40% of their week hunting for data instead of helping patients.

  • FHIR adoption and API-first EHR roadmaps finally make real-time automation feasible.

3. Product & Differentiation

  1. FHIR-Native Data Platform – Direct APIs into Epic, Cerner, Athena; implementation in 8-12 weeks instead of 18-24 months.

  2. Quality Automation Engine – Auto-creates and routes depression, diabetes, hypertension, and colorectal screening gaps with configurable rules.

  3. Risk Stratification Layer – Combines behavioral + clinical indicators to surface the top 10% of cost-driving patients.

  4. Care Team Workflow – Coordinators receive ranked tasks, evidence packets, and “last best action” guidance; adoption hits 85%+ by week 4.

  5. Executive Reporting – Real-time Star rating forecast, ROI calculator outputs, and compliance-ready audit trails.

Data Consistency Plan: HDIM’s ingestion roadmap now includes an AI-driven integration layer that reads directly from common Clinical Data Repositories (Epic Clarity, Cerner HealtheEDW, Meditech Data Repository) and auto-generates SQL mapping templates. Large language models detect schema drift, reconcile code sets to our canonical FHIR store, and produce deterministic SQL views before data ever hits downstream services. This guarantees consistent measures regardless of the originating EMR, shortens implementation to under 8 weeks, and keeps investor diligence focused on growth rather than data quality concerns.

Competitive moat:

  • Built on FHIR from day one vs. legacy vendors translating HL7 → proprietary schemas.

  • Mental health + quality workflow in a single experience (no competitor offers this pairing).

  • Library of 50+ pre-modeled measures plus customer-tunable templates.
