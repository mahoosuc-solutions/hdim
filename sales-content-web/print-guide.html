Print Instructions by Document

📋 For Reading/Reference (Print as PDF)

Recommended: Clinical Sales Strategy, Case Study, Implementation Summary, Content Index

  1. Open the document link above
  2. Click the "Print as PDF" button at the top
  3. Save to your computer
  4. Open and read in PDF viewer
  5. Optional: Print to physical paper

📄 For Daily Sales Use (Print & Laminate)

Recommended: Sales Quick Reference

  1. Open Sales Quick Reference
  2. Click "Print as PDF"
  3. Print to color paper
  4. Laminate (optional but recommended)
  5. Keep on desk or in folder during sales calls

✉️ For Email Campaigns (Copy-Paste)

Recommended: Email Nurture Sequence, One-Pager (as PDF attachment), Case Study (as PDF attachment)

  1. Open Email Nurture Sequence
  2. Copy each email body (Ctrl+C / Cmd+C)
  3. Paste into your email marketing platform (HubSpot, Marketo, etc.)
  4. Personalize with prospect name/company
  5. Attach One-Pager or Case Study as PDF
  6. Schedule send sequence

👥 For Team Training (Project on Screen or Print)

Recommended: Clinical Sales Strategy, Sales Quick Reference

  1. Open Clinical Sales Strategy on projector
  2. Walk through sections: pain points, ROI, competitive positioning, personas
  3. Hand out printed Sales Quick Reference
  4. Walk through discovery questions and objection responses
  5. Role-play with sample scenarios

🎯 For Prospect Presentations (One-Pager + Case Study)

Recommended: Clinical Overview One-Pager, Case Study

  1. Print one-pager (2 pages) for each prospect meeting
  2. Have case study ready (physical or digital)
  3. Use as conversation starters during discovery/working session
  4. Leave behind one-pager + case study after meeting

Chrome/Safari Print Settings (Optimized)

For Best Results:

  1. Destination: Save as PDF
  2. Margins: Default (or custom 0.5")
  3. Scale: 100%
  4. Paper size: Letter (8.5" × 11")
  5. Background graphics: ON (to preserve blue theme)
  6. Headers/Footers: OFF

Storage & Organization Tips

  • Digital: Keep all PDFs in a shared folder (Google Drive, OneDrive, etc.). Easy to email or share links.
  • Physical: Print copies of One-Pager (25+) and Quick Reference (10+). Keep in desk organizer.
  • Laminated: Laminate 1-2 copies of Sales Quick Reference for durability and daily use.
  • Color: Print all PDFs in color for visual impact. Blue theme is part of brand identity.
  • Backup: Keep digital versions always accessible online in case you run out of prints.

Customization Notes

Some documents reference "15,000-patient organization" as baseline. When customizing for specific prospects:

  • One-Pager: Keep generic (works for 10K-30K patient orgs)
  • Case Study: Reference your case study if available, or this generic one
  • ROI Numbers: Adjust conservative numbers based on prospect's specific size (scaling guide in Clinical Sales Strategy)
  • Quick Reference: No customization needed (universal talking points)

Questions or Issues?

Print Quality Issues?

• Check your printer color settings
• Ensure "Background Graphics" is enabled in print dialog
• Try saving to PDF first, then printing from PDF viewer
