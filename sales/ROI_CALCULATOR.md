## ROI Calculation Formulas

### 1. Time Savings Calculation

```
Annual FTE Hours on Quality Reporting = Current FTEs x 2,080 hours/year

Hours Saved with HDIM = Annual FTE Hours x Time Savings %

FTE Equivalent Savings = Hours Saved / 2,080

Annual Cost Savings = FTE Equivalent Savings x Average FTE Cost
```

**Time Savings Percentages by Function**:
| Function | Automation Rate |
|----------|-----------------|
| Manual measure calculation | 90-95% |
| Data aggregation/preparation | 85-90% |
| Report generation | 95-98% |
| Audit preparation | 70-80% |
| Gap list management | 80-85% |

### 2. Quality Improvement Revenue (Star Rating)

```
Per-Star Value = $50 - $100 per member per year (varies by market)

Star Rating Revenue Increase = Member Count x Star Improvement x Per-Star Value

For Medicare Advantage Plans:
  - 4-star threshold bonus: Additional 5% quality bonus payments
  - 5-star bonus: Highest rebate percentages
```

**Star Rating Value Table**:
| Current Rating | After HDIM | Revenue Impact/Member |
|----------------|------------|----------------------|
| 3.0 stars | 3.5 stars | $25 - $50 |
| 3.5 stars | 4.0 stars | $50 - $100 + 5% bonus |
| 4.0 stars | 4.5 stars | $50 - $75 |
| 4.5 stars | 5.0 stars | $50 - $75 + max rebate |

### 3. Care Gap Closure Revenue

```
Current Gaps Closed = Open Care Gaps x Current Closure Rate

New Gaps Closed with HDIM = Open Care Gaps x (Current Closure Rate + Improvement %)

Additional Gaps Closed = New Gaps Closed - Current Gaps Closed

Revenue per Gap (varies by type):
  - Preventive screenings: $150 - $250
  - Chronic disease management: $300 - $500
  - High-value interventions: $500 - $1,000

Annual Gap Closure Revenue = Additional Gaps Closed x Average Revenue per Gap
```

**Gap Value Reference**:
| Gap Type | Average Revenue | Volume (% of total) |
|----------|-----------------|---------------------|
| Mammography | $175 | 15% |
| Colorectal Screening | $200 | 10% |
| A1C Testing | $150 | 20% |
| Blood Pressure Control | $250 | 15% |
| Medication Adherence | $400 | 25% |
| Annual Wellness Visit | $300 | 15% |

### 4. Risk Adjustment (HCC) Revenue

```
Current HCC Revenue = Member Count x Average Risk Payment

HCC Capture Improvement Value = Current HCC Revenue x Capture Improvement %

Annual Additional HCC Revenue = HCC Capture Improvement Value
```

**HCC Improvement Factors**:
| Improvement Area | Typical Lift |
|------------------|--------------|
| Chronic condition documentation | 3-5% |
| Suspect condition identification | 2-4% |
| Retrospective chart review | 1-3% |
| Real-time coding alerts | 2-4% |

### 5. Compliance Cost Avoidance

```
HIPAA Violation Risk Reduction:
  - Average HIPAA fine: $100,000 - $1,500,000
  - Risk reduction factor: 50-80%
  - Annual risk-adjusted savings: Probability x Average Fine x Reduction %

Audit Preparation Savings:
  - Current audit prep hours x FTE hourly rate x 70% reduction
  - Typical: 500-2,000 hours/year for mid-size plans
```

### 6. Total ROI Calculation

```
TOTAL ANNUAL BENEFITS:
  A = Time Savings (FTE cost reduction)
  B = Star Rating Revenue Improvement
  C = Care Gap Closure Revenue
  D = HCC Revenue Improvement
  E = Compliance Cost Avoidance

Total Annual Benefits = A + B + C + D + E

TOTAL COSTS:
  Year 1 = Annual License + Implementation + Support
  Year 2+ = Annual License + Support

Net Annual Value = Total Benefits - Total Costs

ROI % = (Net Annual Value / Total Costs) x 100

Payback Period (months) = (Year 1 Total Costs / Monthly Benefits)

3-Year Total Value = (Year 1 Benefits - Year 1 Costs) +
                     (Year 2 Benefits - Year 2 Costs) +
                     (Year 3 Benefits - Year 3 Costs)
```

## Talking Points

### Opening Value Statement

"Based on your organization's profile, HDIM could deliver [X] in annual value - that's a [Y]% ROI in the first year alone, with full payback in [Z] months."

### Key Differentiators to Emphasize

1. **Automated HEDIS Calculation**: "We reduce manual measure calculation by 90%, freeing your quality team to focus on interventions rather than spreadsheets."

2. **Real-Time Care Gaps**: "Instead of quarterly gap lists, your care coordinators get daily prioritized worklists - that's why our clients see 15-25% improvement in gap closure rates."

3. **HCC Coding Optimization**: "Our AI-powered suspect condition identification captures 5-10% more HCC codes that are clinically valid but undocumented."

4. **Compliance Built-In**: "Every PHI access is audited, every cache expires in under 5 minutes, and every report is HIPAA-compliant by design."

### Objection Handlers

**"These numbers seem too good to be true."**

Response: "Let me walk you through the conservative scenario - even at the lowest assumptions, you're looking at [X]% ROI. And these aren't theoretical - they're based on real results from organizations like [reference customer]."

**"We've invested heavily in our current systems."**

Response: "HDIM integrates with your existing EHR and data warehouse investments. We're not replacing your core systems - we're adding an intelligence layer that makes them more valuable. Most of your current quality reporting processes can be retired, but your data assets become more productive."

**"What about implementation risk?"**

Response: "We've built a phased implementation approach with go-live gates. You start seeing ROI on Phase 1 capabilities within 90 days - automated measure calculation and gap identification. Full platform deployment typically completes in 6-9 months with your team fully trained."

### Closing Questions

- "Based on this analysis, which benefit area resonates most with your organization's priorities right now?"
- "If we could deliver even half of these projected benefits, would that justify moving forward with a pilot?"
- "What would it mean for your team if they could reclaim [X] hours per week from manual quality reporting?"
