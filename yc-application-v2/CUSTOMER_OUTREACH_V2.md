## Target Customer Profiles

### Tier 1: Mid-Market ACOs (Primary Target)
- **Size:** 5,000-50,000 attributed lives
- **IT Budget:** $200K-2M annually
- **Current State:** Legacy batch processing or manual reporting
- **Pain Level:** High (struggling with value-based contracts)
- **Target ACV:** $50-150K

### Tier 2: Regional Health Systems
- **Size:** 50,000-200,000 lives
- **IT Budget:** $2M-10M annually
- **Current State:** Epic/Cerner tools, dissatisfied with speed
- **Pain Level:** Medium (want real-time, stuck with batch)
- **Target ACV:** $100-250K

### Tier 3: Progressive FQHCs
- **Size:** 5,000-25,000 patients
- **IT Budget:** $100K-500K annually
- **Current State:** Mostly manual, some Azara
- **Pain Level:** High (can't afford enterprise tools)
- **Target ACV:** $30-80K

## Call Scripts

### Discovery Call (15 minutes)

**Opening (1 min):**
> "Thanks for taking the time. Before I show you anything, I'd love to understand your current setup. What tools are you using for quality measurement today?"

**Discovery Questions (5 min):**
1. "How long does it take to see measure results after a patient visit?"
2. "How are you handling care gap identification and closure?"
3. "What EHRs are you working with across your network?"
4. "What's your biggest frustration with your current quality tools?"
5. "Are you managing any custom measures beyond standard HEDIS?"

**Demo Transition (1 min):**
> "Based on what you've shared, let me show you how HDIM handles [their specific pain point]."

**Demo (6 min):**
1. Search for sample patient
2. Show instant quality measure calculation
3. Demonstrate care gap prioritization
4. Show one-click gap closure workflow
5. If relevant: Custom Measure Builder

**Close (2 min):**
> "What questions do you have about what you've seen?"
> "Would it make sense to set up a deeper dive with your team?"

---

### Objection Responses

**"We already use [Epic/Cerner/Innovaccer]."**
> "Makes sense. How satisfied are you with the speed of measure calculation? Most organizations I talk to are frustrated with overnight batch processing. HDIM can complement your existing tools or serve as a bridge for providers on different EHRs."

**"We're not ready to switch platforms."**
> "Totally understand. HDIM isn't about replacing what you have—it's about filling the gaps. We're EHR-agnostic and can overlay your existing infrastructure. Many organizations use us alongside their primary analytics platform."

**"You're a new company."**
> "Fair concern. A few things that might help: First, our unit economics are exceptional—15:1 LTV:CAC, 4-month payback, 47 months of runway. Second, the platform itself is enterprise-grade: 534 test files, zero critical CVEs, SOC2-ready. Third, I've been doing this for 15+ years—Enterprise Architect at Healthix, Integration Architect at HealthInfoNet."

**"What's the pricing?"**
> "We're positioned for the mid-market: $50-150K annual, depending on size and features. That's typically 60-80% less than enterprise platforms like Innovaccer or Inovalon. Want me to put together a rough estimate based on your volume?"
