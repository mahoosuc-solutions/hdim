```
Complex Healthcare Software:
├── Timeline: 18-24 months
├── Cost: $1-5 million
├── Team: 10-20 engineers
├── Risk: High (50%+ fail or go over budget)
└── Result: Organizations don't even try
```

### New Reality (2025+)
```
Complex Healthcare Software:
├── Timeline: 3-6 months
├── Cost: $25K-150K
├── Team: 1-3 people + AI
├── Risk: Lower (faster iteration, cheaper pivots)
└── Result: Anyone can attempt it
```
