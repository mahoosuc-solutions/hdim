## Posting Best Practices

### Optimal Timing

| Day | Best Time (EST) | Why |
|-----|-----------------|-----|
| Tuesday | 8-9 AM | Start of work week engagement |
| Wednesday | 10-11 AM | Mid-week peak |
| Thursday | 8-9 AM | High professional activity |
| Tuesday | 12-1 PM | Lunch break browsing |

**Avoid:** Weekends, Monday mornings, Friday afternoons

### Post Structure

**Hook (First 2 lines):**
- Must grab attention
- Visible before "see more"
- Question, bold statement, or number

**Body:**
- Short paragraphs (1-3 lines)
- Use line breaks liberally
- Bullet points and emojis for scannability
- One clear idea per post

**CTA:**
- Always include engagement ask
- Question, poll, or action request
- Keep it simple

### Formatting Tips

```
✅ Use line breaks
✅ Short paragraphs
✅ Bullet points with emojis
✅ Numbers and statistics
✅ Clear structure

❌ Wall of text
❌ No line breaks
❌ Multiple CTAs
❌ All caps
❌ Too many hashtags
```

## Engagement Playbook

### Daily Engagement Routine (15-20 min)

**Morning (8-9 AM):**
1. Check notifications, respond to comments on your posts
2. Comment on 3-5 posts from target accounts
3. Accept/send connection requests

**Midday (12-1 PM):**
1. Share one piece of industry content with commentary
2. Respond to any new comments
3. Engage with 3-5 new posts

**Evening (Optional):**
1. Final comment check
2. Schedule next day's post

### Comment Strategy

**On Your Posts:**
- Respond to every comment within 4 hours
- Ask follow-up questions to continue conversation
- Tag people when referencing their comments
- Heart/like all comments

**On Others' Posts:**
- Add value, don't just agree
- Share a perspective or experience
- Ask thoughtful questions
- Avoid self-promotion in comments
