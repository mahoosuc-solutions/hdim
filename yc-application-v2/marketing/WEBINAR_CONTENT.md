## Webinar Topic Library

### Topic 1: Real-Time Quality Measurement 101

**Title:** "See Your Quality Score Right Now: Introduction to Real-Time Measurement"

**Format:** Educational (45 min + 15 min Q&A)
**Target Audience:** Practice managers, quality directors, CMOs
**Funnel Stage:** Top of funnel (awareness)

**Description:**
Most practices only see their quality data days or weeks after patient encounters. What if you could see your MIPS score, care gaps, and quality trends in real time? This webinar introduces the concept of real-time quality measurement and shows how it's transforming care gap closure and quality improvement workflows.

**Learning Objectives:**
1. Understand the difference between batch and real-time quality measurement
2. Identify the clinical and financial impact of data latency
3. Learn practical strategies for implementing real-time workflows
4. See examples from practices that achieved 40%+ quality score improvements

## Webinar Slide Template

### Slide Structure (30-Slide Template)

**Opening (Slides 1-5)**

| Slide | Content |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Title slide: Webinar title, speaker name, HDIM logo |
| 2 | Welcome: Housekeeping (mute, questions, recording) |
| 3 | Agenda: What we'll cover today |
| 4 | Speaker bio: Credentials, experience |
| 5 | Poll: Opening engagement question |

**Problem/Context (Slides 6-10)**

| Slide | Content |
|-------|---------|
| 6 | The problem: What's broken in current approach |
| 7 | Impact: Why this matters (stats, costs) |
| 8 | Common challenges: Audience pain points |
| 9 | The opportunity: What's possible |
| 10 | Transition: How we'll solve this |

**Core Content (Slides 11-20)**

| Slide | Content |
|-------|---------|
| 11-12 | Concept 1: First key teaching point |
| 13-14 | Concept 2: Second key teaching point |
| 15-16 | Concept 3: Third key teaching point |
| 17-18 | Case study: Real-world example |
| 19 | Key takeaways: Summary of main points |
| 20 | Transition to Q&A |

**Case Study Section (Slides 17-18 Detail)**

| Slide | Content |
|-------|---------|
| 17 | Customer story: Before state, challenge |
| 18 | Results: After state, metrics, quote |

**Q&A + Close (Slides 21-25)**

| Slide | Content |
|-------|---------|
| 21 | Q&A: Live questions |
| 22 | Resources: What attendees can download |
| 23 | Next steps: Demo offer with CTA |
| 24 | Contact info: How to reach us |
| 25 | Thank you: Closing slide |

**Backup Slides (26-30)**

| Slide | Content |
|-------|---------|
| 26-30 | Anticipated Q&A: Pre-built answers |

### Visual Design Guidelines

**Colors:**
- Primary: HDIM Blue (#0066CC)
- Secondary: Dark gray (#333333)
- Accent: Green (#00AA44) for positive/results
- Background: White or very light gray

**Typography:**
- Headings: Bold sans-serif, 32-40pt
- Body: Regular sans-serif, 18-24pt
- Minimum text: No more than 6 lines per slide

**Images:**
- High-quality screenshots
- Simple charts/graphs
- Minimal stock photography
- Icons for concepts

**Branding:**
- HDIM logo on every slide (bottom corner)
- Consistent color scheme throughout
- Tagline on title and closing slides
