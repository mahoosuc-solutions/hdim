# HDIM Customer Success Playbook

> Onboarding workflow, health scoring, expansion strategies, churn prevention, and QBR templates.

---

## Customer Success Mission

**Goal:** Ensure every HDIM customer achieves measurable improvement in quality measurement outcomes and becomes a long-term advocate.

**Key Metrics:**
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR): 120%+
- Gross Revenue Retention (GRR): 95%+
- Customer Health Score: 80+ average
- NPS: 50+
- Time to Value: <30 days

## Onboarding Workflow (30-60-90 Day Plan)

### Pre-Onboarding (Before Day 1)

**CSM Handoff from Sales:**
- [ ] Review sales notes and discovery call recordings
- [ ] Understand customer pain points and goals
- [ ] Review contract terms and pricing tier
- [ ] Identify key stakeholders
- [ ] Schedule kickoff call

**Internal Prep:**
- [ ] Create customer folder in CS system
- [ ] Set up Slack channel (#customer-[name])
- [ ] Prepare kickoff materials
- [ ] Assign technical resources if needed

---

### Week 1: Foundation

**Day 1: Welcome & Kickoff**

- [ ] Send welcome email with resources
- [ ] Kickoff call (60 min)
  - Introductions
  - Review goals and success criteria
  - Walkthrough onboarding timeline
  - Schedule recurring check-ins
- [ ] Provide login credentials
- [ ] Share onboarding checklist

**Day 2-3: Integration Setup**

- [ ] Technical discovery call
- [ ] Begin EHR integration or CSV upload
- [ ] Configure organization structure
- [ ] Set up user accounts

**Day 4-5: Initial Configuration**

- [ ] Select priority quality measures
- [ ] Configure dashboards
- [ ] Test data flow
- [ ] Troubleshoot any issues

---

### Week 2: Data Validation

**Day 8-10: Data Quality Review**

- [ ] Verify patient data loaded correctly
- [ ] Validate clinical observations
- [ ] Check measure calculations
- [ ] Identify any data gaps

**Day 11-14: First Dashboard Review**

- [ ] Review quality dashboard with customer
- [ ] Explain measure results
- [ ] Identify initial care gaps
- [ ] Gather feedback on data quality

---

### Week 3: Training

**Day 15-17: User Training**

- [ ] Admin training (1 hour)
- [ ] Provider dashboard training (30 min)
- [ ] Staff worklist training (30 min)
- [ ] Q&A session

**Day 18-21: Workflow Integration**

- [ ] Integrate care gaps into daily workflow
- [ ] Set up automated reports
- [ ] Configure alerts and notifications
- [ ] Document customer-specific workflows

---

### Week 4: Go-Live

**Day 22-25: Production Launch**

- [ ] Confirm all data flowing correctly
- [ ] Verify all users can access
- [ ] Monitor for any issues
- [ ] Daily check-ins

**Day 26-30: Stabilization**

- [ ] Address any remaining issues
- [ ] 30-day review call
- [ ] Document initial wins
- [ ] Transition to adoption phase

## Customer Health Score

### Scoring Model

**Total Score: 0-100 points**

| Category | Weight | Metrics |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Product Usage | 30% | DAU, feature adoption, API calls |
| Business Outcomes | 30% | Quality improvement, care gaps closed |
| Engagement | 20% | Training completion, support tickets, meeting attendance |
| Relationship | 20% | Stakeholder access, NPS, responsiveness |

### Detailed Scoring Criteria

**Product Usage (30 points)**

| Metric | Points | Thresholds |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Daily Active Users | 10 | 0-3: Low, 4-6: Medium, 7-10: High |
| Feature Adoption | 10 | % of features used regularly |
| Data Freshness | 10 | Days since last data sync |

**Business Outcomes (30 points)**

| Metric | Points | Thresholds |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Quality Score Change | 15 | <0: At risk, 0-5%: Neutral, >5%: Healthy |
| Care Gaps Closed | 10 | Trend vs. prior period |
| Time Savings | 5 | Reported vs. expected |

**Engagement (20 points)**

| Metric | Points | Thresholds |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Training Completion | 5 | % of users trained |
| Support Tickets | 5 | Volume and resolution |
| Meeting Attendance | 5 | % of scheduled meetings attended |
| Response Time | 5 | Average time to respond to CSM |

**Relationship (20 points)**

| Metric | Points | Thresholds |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Stakeholder Access | 8 | # of active relationships |
| NPS Score | 7 | Last survey result |
| Expansion Potential | 5 | Identified opportunities |

### Health Score Interpretation

| Score | Status | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 80-100 | Healthy | Advocate cultivation, expansion focus |
| 60-79 | Neutral | Engagement boost, address gaps |
| 40-59 | At Risk | Intervention required, escalate |
| 0-39 | Critical | Executive escalation, save plan |

## Expansion Playbook

### Expansion Triggers

| Trigger | Opportunity | Action |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| New locations/sites | Add providers | Propose tier upgrade |
| Joining ACO | Enterprise features | Discuss ACO package |
| Quality success | More measures | Propose measure expansion |
| Referral mention | New customer | Activate referral program |
| Positive NPS | Advocacy | Request case study/review |

### Upsell Opportunities

**Tier Upgrades:**
- Community → Professional: After value proven
- Professional → Enterprise: Multi-site or FQHC
- Enterprise → Custom: Large health systems

**Add-Ons:**
- Additional providers
- Custom measures
- API access
- White-label dashboards
- Priority support

### Expansion Conversation Framework

1. **Anchor on Success:** "Your quality scores improved 25%..."
2. **Identify Gap:** "I noticed you're manually tracking X..."
3. **Present Solution:** "Our [feature] would automate that..."
4. **Quantify Value:** "Based on your current spend, this would save..."
5. **Propose Next Step:** "Want me to put together a proposal?"

## Churn Prevention

### Early Warning Signs

| Signal | Risk Level | Intervention |
|--------|------------|--------------|
| Declining logins | Medium | Check-in call, training refresh |
| Support tickets unresolved | Medium | Priority support, CSM escalation |
| Key contact left | High | Rebuild relationships, exec sponsor |
| Negative NPS | High | Executive outreach, action plan |
| Invoice disputes | High | Finance + CSM collaboration |
| Competitor mentions | Critical | Executive intervention |
| Non-responsive | Critical | Multi-channel outreach |

### Risk Intervention Playbook

**Medium Risk (Score 60-79):**
1. Schedule check-in within 1 week
2. Review usage data for insights
3. Identify specific concerns
4. Create action plan
5. Increase touch frequency

**High Risk (Score 40-59):**
1. Immediate outreach (within 24 hours)
2. Escalate to CSM manager
3. Schedule executive call if needed
4. Create formal save plan
5. Weekly monitoring

**Critical Risk (Score <40):**
1. Same-day executive escalation
2. Executive-to-executive outreach
3. Offer concessions if appropriate
4. Daily monitoring
5. Document learnings if churn occurs

## Quarterly Business Review (QBR)

### QBR Scheduling

| Customer Tier | QBR Frequency | Duration |
|---------------|---------------|----------|
| Enterprise | Quarterly | 60 min |
| Professional | Bi-annual | 45 min |
| Community | Annual (opt-in) | 30 min |

### QBR Preparation

**1 Week Before:**
- [ ] Pull usage analytics
- [ ] Calculate quality improvement metrics
- [ ] Review support ticket history
- [ ] Identify expansion opportunities
- [ ] Prepare personalized deck

**2 Days Before:**
- [ ] Send agenda to customer
- [ ] Confirm attendees
- [ ] Prepare any demos

### QBR Agenda Template

**1. Executive Summary (5 min)**
- Relationship overview
- Key achievements since last QBR

**2. Business Outcomes (15 min)**
- Quality score trends
- Care gaps closed
- Time/cost savings
- ROI summary

**3. Product Usage (10 min)**
- Feature adoption
- User engagement
- Benchmark comparisons

**4. Roadmap Preview (10 min)**
- Upcoming features
- How they benefit this customer
- Beta opportunities

**5. Strategic Discussion (15 min)**
- Customer's upcoming initiatives
- How HDIM can support
- Expansion opportunities

**6. Action Items (5 min)**
- Summarize commitments
- Next steps
- Schedule follow-ups

## Renewal Playbook

### Renewal Timeline

| Days Before Renewal | Activity |
|---------------------|----------|
| 120 days | Renewal kickoff, health assessment |
| 90 days | QBR with renewal discussion |
| 60 days | Renewal proposal sent |
| 30 days | Contract review, negotiation |
| 15 days | Final terms, signature |
| 0 days | Renewal complete |

### Renewal Conversation Framework

**For Healthy Customers (Health Score 80+):**
- Lead with success metrics
- Propose multi-year for discount
- Discuss expansion

**For Neutral Customers (Health Score 60-79):**
- Address any concerns first
- Demonstrate value clearly
- Offer incentives if needed

**For At-Risk Customers (Health Score <60):**
- Executive involvement
- Formal save plan
- Concessions as appropriate

### Renewal Email Templates

**90 Days Out (Healthy):**

