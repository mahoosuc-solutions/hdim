## Objection Handling

**"We use Epic/Cerner"**
"Totally understand - most of our customers do too. HDIM integrates with Epic/Cerner via FHIR. We're not replacing your EHR, we're adding real-time quality intelligence on top. Would you be open to seeing how it works alongside your current stack?"

**"We don't have budget right now"**
"Makes sense - that's exactly why we built this. Our starting tier is $80/month, which is often less than a single consultant day. What if we did a 30-day pilot with no commitment? You'd know pretty quickly if it's valuable."

**"We tried something similar and it failed"**
"That's helpful context - what went wrong? [Listen] Our approach is different in that [address their specific concern]. Would it be useful to see a working demo before ruling it out?"

**"We're locked into a contract"**
"Understood. When does that come up for renewal? We could do a technical evaluation now so you have data when that conversation happens."

**Success Criteria:**
- Calculate measures for 100+ patients
- Identify 10+ care gaps correctly
- Complete in under 30 days start to finish

**Conversion Path:**
- Free pilot → Paid pilot ($500/mo) → Production ($1,500+/mo)
